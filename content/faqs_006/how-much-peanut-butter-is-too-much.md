However, it is also high in calories, with one tablespoon containing around 94 calories.</p>

<p>This means that if you are trying to lose weight, limit your consumption of peanut butter to one or two tablespoons per day. But, it is important to remember that too much peanut butter can quickly add up to a lot of extra calories, which can lead to weight gain.</p>

<p>If you are an athlete or someone who is looking to build muscle, then peanut butter can be a great way to get some extra protein into your diet. A good rule of thumb is to aim for around two to three tablespoons of peanut butter per day.</p>

<h2>What Are the Best Types of Peanut Butter?</h2>

<p>When it comes to choosing the best type of peanut butter, it is important to look for ones that are low in sugar and salt, and contain nothing but peanuts. Many commercial brands contain added sugar and salt, which can increase the calorie content significantly. It is also important to look for ones that are made from 100% roasted peanuts, as these can be more nutritious than other varieties.</p>

<p>It is also important to look for natural peanut butters with no added oils or preservatives. Many commercial brands contain added oils, which can increase the fat content significantly. It is also important to avoid peanut butters that contain hydrogenated oils, as these can contain trans fats, which can increase the risk of heart disease.</p>
