<h2>How Much Hamburger and Rice Should You Feed Your Dog?</h2>

The amount of hamburger and rice you should feed your dog will depend on their age, size, and activity level. Generally, you should feed your pup between 1/4 and 1/2 cup of cooked hamburger and 1/2 to 1 cup of cooked rice per day.
