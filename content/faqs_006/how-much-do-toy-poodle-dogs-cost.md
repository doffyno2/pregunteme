<h2>What Are Toy Poodles?</h2>

Toy poodles are a small-sized breed of poodles that were bred by crossing the standard poodle with smaller breeds. They are the smallest of the three types of poodles and typically weigh between 4 and 6 pounds. Toy poodles are known for their playful, energetic personalities and their intelligence. They make great companions for people of all ages.
