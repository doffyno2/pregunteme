<h2>What is a Liter?</h2>

A liter is a metric unit of volume. It is equal to 1,000 cubic centimeters (cm3) or 1,000 milliliters (mL). It is also equal to the volume of a cube with 10 cm sides. A liter is a very common unit of volume used in both the metric and imperial systems. The abbreviation for liter is “L.”

<h2>How Many 20oz Bottles of Water Equal a Liter?</h2>

One 20oz bottle of water is equal to 0.59 liters. This means that it would take 1.7 20oz bottles of water to equal one liter. Therefore, if you are looking to drink one liter of water, you would need to drink 1.7 20oz bottles of water.
