It can be a bit confusing to figure out how to do this. Luckily, it's not as hard as it seems. In this article, we'll show you how to check scheduled rides on Lyft.</p>

<h2>What is Lyft?</h2>

<p>Lyft is a ride-hailing service that connects passengers with drivers. You can use the app to request a ride or schedule one in advance. It's a convenient, affordable way to get around quickly and safely.</p>

<h2>How to Check Scheduled Rides on Lyft</h2>

<p>It's easy to check your scheduled rides on Lyft. Here's how to do it:</p>

<h3>Step 1: Open the Lyft App</h3>

<p>The first step is to open the Lyft app. You can do this from your phone's home screen or app drawer. Then, log into your account if you're not already logged in.</p>

<h3>Step 2: Tap the Menu Icon</h3>

<p>Once you're logged in, tap the menu icon in the upper left corner of the app. This will open the main menu. From here, you can access all of the app's features.</p>

<h3>Step 3: Tap the “Ride History” Option</h3>

<p>When you're in the main menu, scroll down until you see the “Ride History” option. Tap it to view your ride history.</p>

<h3>Step 4: View Your Scheduled Rides</h3>

<p>When you're in the Ride History section, you'll see a list of all of your past and upcoming rides. Tap on any “Scheduled” ride to view the details. Here, you can see the date, time, destination, and estimated cost of your ride. You can also cancel or reschedule your ride if necessary.</p>

<h2>Frequently Asked Questions</h2>

<h3>How do I pay for a Lyft ride?</h3>

<p>You can pay for your Lyft ride with a credit or debit card. You can add a card to your account in the Lyft app. When you request a ride, your card will be charged automatically.</p>

<h3>Can I change my destination while on a Lyft ride?</h3>

<p>Yes, you can change your destination while on a Lyft ride. Simply tell your driver your new destination and they will adjust the route accordingly.</p>

<p>Yes, you can schedule a Lyft ride for a later time. You can do this from the main screen of the app. Just enter your pick up location, choose a date and time, and tap the “schedule” button.</p>

<h3>Can I cancel a scheduled Lyft ride?</h3>

<p>Yes, you can cancel a scheduled Lyft ride. Just go to the “Ride History” section of the app and tap on the ride you want to cancel. Then, tap the “Cancel” button to cancel the ride.</p>

<h3>Can I add a tip to my Lyft ride?</h3>
