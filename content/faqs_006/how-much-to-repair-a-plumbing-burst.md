<h2>What Causes a Plumbing Burst?</h2>

<p>A plumbing burst can occur for a variety of reasons. In some cases, it’s due to an issue with the pipes themselves, such as corrosion or a crack. And in other cases, it can be due to a combination of any of these issues.</p>

<p>No matter what the cause, it’s important to address a plumbing burst as soon as possible, as it can cause significant damage to your home. <p>Repairing a plumbing burst typically involves a few steps. First, the damaged pipe must be identified and located. This may require the use of a camera or other inspection tools.
