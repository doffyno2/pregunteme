<h2>How to Reset a Kenmore Stove</h2>

<p>Follow these steps to reset a Kenmore stove:</p>

<h3>Step 1: Unplug The Stove</h3>

<p>The first step in resetting a Kenmore stove is to unplug the stove from the wall. To do this, locate the power cord and unplug it from the wall outlet. This will reset the control panel and other components of the stove.</p>

<h3>Step 2: Reset the Oven Temperature</h3>

<p>The next step is to reset the oven temperature. To do this, turn the oven off and unplug the power cord from the wall. Then, press and hold the "bake" and "broil" buttons for three seconds. This will reset the oven temperature.</p>

<h3>Step 3: Reset the Stovetop Heating Elements</h3>

<p>Finally, you will need to reset the stovetop heating elements. To do this, turn the stove off and unplug the power cord from the wall. Press and hold the "burner" button for three seconds. This will reset the stovetop heating elements.</p>
