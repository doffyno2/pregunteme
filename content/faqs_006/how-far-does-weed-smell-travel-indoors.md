Weed is a slang term for cannabis, the plant that is cultivated for its buds, resin, and leaves. When it’s smoked, weed has a distinct smell that’s often described as skunky, earthy, musky, and sweet. The smell of weed comes from its terpenes—the oils that give the plant its flavor and aroma. The strain of weed you’re smoking will also affect its smell.
