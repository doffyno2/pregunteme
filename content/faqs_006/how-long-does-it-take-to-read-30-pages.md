<h2>What is the Average Reading Speed?</h2>

<p>The average reading speed is 200 to 250 words per minute. This means that it takes approximately one to two minutes to read a page of standard-sized text. Therefore, it takes approximately half an hour to read 30 pages. </p>

<h2>Ways to Improve Reading Speed</h2>

<p>If you want to read faster, there are several techniques you can use to improve your reading speed. These include:</p>

<ul>
    <li><b>Pre-reading</b>: Before you start reading, take a few moments to glance through the material and get an overview of what it's about. This will help you to read faster as you already have an idea of what to expect. </li>
    <li><b>Skimming</b>: Skimming is the process of quickly reading through large amounts of text to get the gist of what it's about. This doesn't involve reading every single word, but rather reading the main points and skimming over the rest. </li>
    <li><b>Increasing font size</b>: Increasing the font size of text can make it easier to read and therefore help you to read faster. </li>
    <li><b>Using a ruler</b>: Using a ruler (or your finger) to guide your eyes while reading can help you to read faster as it forces your eyes to stay focused on the text. </li>
    <li><b>Taking breaks</b>: Taking regular breaks while reading can help to keep you focused and improve your reading speed. </li>
</ul>
