<h2>What is Classic Riding?</h2>

Classic riding is a type of riding that involves riding a horse in a specific way. It is a style of riding that focuses on balance and coordination, and it has been around for centuries. The goal of classic riding is to show off the horse's natural beauty by emphasizing the grace and agility of the animal.

<h2>The Cost of Riding in Classic Style</h2>

The cost of riding in classic style can vary depending on a number of factors, such as the type of horse you are riding, the type of saddle and bridle you are using, and the type of facility you are riding in. Generally speaking, the cost of riding in classic style can range anywhere from $30-$100 per hour, depending on the level of instruction you are receiving and the type of horse you are riding.
