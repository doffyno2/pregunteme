<h2>What Do You Need to Light a Campfire in an Igloo?</h2>

The first step to lighting a campfire in an igloo is to gather the materials you will need. <h2>How to Light a Campfire in an Igloo</h2>

Once you have gathered the necessary materials, you are ready to light your campfire in an igloo. The first step is to create a small space inside the igloo for the fire. This can be done by using an existing fire pit or by digging a shallow pit in the snow.

Once the fire pit is ready, you can begin to build the fire. Start by placing the fuel in the pit and then layer the fire starter on top of that. Be sure to leave enough space between the fuel and fire starter to allow the fire to spread.

Now it is time to light the fire. Use your matches or lighter to ignite the fire starter and then gently blow on the flames to encourage them to spread. Once the fire is burning, add more fuel as needed to keep it burning throughout the night.
