<p>GoPro cameras are known for their versatility and durability, making them an excellent choice for outdoor activities such as skiing, mountain biking, and surfing. But if you’re planning to use your GoPro for an extended period of time, you may be wondering how long the battery will last. The answer depends on a few factors, including the type of GoPro you have, the settings you use, and the battery itself.</p>

<h2>What Type of Battery Does a GoPro Use?</h2>

<p>GoPro cameras use lithium-ion batteries. These batteries are rechargeable and offer a significant amount of power in a small package. They’re also very efficient and capable of holding a charge for a long period of time.</p>

<h2>How Long Will a GoPro Battery Last?</h2>

<p>The exact battery life of a GoPro will depend on the model and settings you’re using. Generally speaking, most GoPro models will last between one to two hours on a single charge. However, if you’re using the camera in higher resolution or at higher frame rates, the battery life will be significantly reduced.</p>

<h2>Can You Increase the Battery Life of a GoPro?</h2>

<p>Yes, there are a few things you can do to increase the battery life of your GoPro. The first is to turn off any unnecessary features such as Wi-Fi or Bluetooth. These features will drain the battery much faster than if they’re turned off.</p>

<p>You should also keep your GoPro in low-power mode when not in use. This will turn off the LCD screen and any other features that may be running and draining the battery. Finally, you may want to invest in additional batteries or a power bank to extend your shooting time.</p>

<h2>Can You Replace a GoPro Battery?</h2>

<p>Yes, you can replace the battery in a GoPro camera. All GoPro models have removable batteries, so you can easily switch them out for new ones. There are also a number of third-party batteries that are compatible with GoPro cameras.</p>
