<h2>What is T20 Cricket?</h2>

T20 cricket is a variation of the traditional game of cricket and is played on a smaller pitch than the traditional game. It is designed to be a fast-paced and exciting version of the game, and it has become a popular choice for casual and club cricket. The game is played with two teams of 11 players, and each team has a maximum of 20 overs to complete their innings.

<h2>What Equipment is Used in T20 Cricket?</h2>

In order to play T20 cricket, players need a variety of equipment. This includes a cricket ball, bats, pads, gloves, and helmets. The bats used in T20 cricket are usually a bit lighter than the ones used in traditional cricket, as they need to be able to respond quickly to the pace of the game. Gloves and helmets are also important, as they protect the players from the hard-hitting balls and the fast-moving bats.
