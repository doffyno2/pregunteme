<h2>What is Device Health Services?</h2>

<p>Device Health Services is a set of services designed to keep your device running optimally. It monitors the health of your device to identify any potential problems and optimize performance. <h2>How Do I Disable Device Health Services?</h2>

<p>Disabling Device Health Services is relatively simple. You can do it through the Windows Settings app. Open the Windows Settings app, then go to the “Privacy” settings. From there, select the “Diagnostics & Feedback” tab and turn off the “Device Health Services” switch. This will disable Device Health Services and prevent it from collecting data.</p>
