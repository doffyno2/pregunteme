<h2>What Is a Pit Bike?</h2>

Pit bikes are small, lightweight motorcycles designed for off-road racing and recreational riding. They typically have a wheelbase of between 48 and 54 inches, and they are powered by a small 2-stroke or 4-stroke engine.
