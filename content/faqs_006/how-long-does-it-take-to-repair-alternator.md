<h2>What is an Alternator?</h2>

<p>An alternator is an electrical generator that converts mechanical energy from the engine into electrical energy. It is driven by a belt connected to the engine and is responsible for providing power to the electrical components in the car, such as the headlights, wipers, and stereo. Alternators also provide a voltage regulator to ensure that the voltage remains consistent throughout the car’s electrical system. </p>
