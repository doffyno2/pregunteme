<h2>What Are Fishing Minnows?</h2>

Fishing minnows are small, typically non-biting fish that are used as bait when fishing. They are typically small and come in a variety of shapes and sizes, including both live and artificial versions. Live minnows are the most popular type of minnow used by anglers and come in a variety of species including shiners, chubs, and dace. Artificial minnows are typically made of plastic and are made to imitate the shape and movement of real live minnows.

<h2>How Much Do Fishing Minnows Cost?</h2>

The cost of fishing minnows varies depending on the type of minnow you choose and where you buy them. Live minnows are typically more expensive than artificial minnows and can range from $2 to $4 per dozen. Artificial minnows are generally cheaper, with prices ranging from $1 to $3 per dozen.
