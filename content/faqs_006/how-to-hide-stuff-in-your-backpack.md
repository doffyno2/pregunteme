<h2>Designate a Secret Compartment</h2>

<p>The easiest way to hide stuff in your backpack is to create a secret compartment. You can do this by cutting a hole in the lining of your backpack and sewing in a pocket. This pocket should be deep enough to fit whatever you're trying to hide, and should be placed in an inconspicuous part of the backpack. If you don't feel comfortable cutting into the lining of your backpack, you can also buy a separate pocket that can be attached to the inside of your backpack. Make sure it is securely attached so that it won't come loose.</p>

<h2>Use Odorless Containers</h2>

<p>If you're trying to hide food or other items that have an odor, it's important to choose a container that won't give away your hiding spot. Make sure to secure them tightly so that the contents won't leak out.</p>
