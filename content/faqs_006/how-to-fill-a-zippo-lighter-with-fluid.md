<h2>Things You'll Need</h2>
<ul>
  <li>Lighter fluid</li>
  <li>Cotton swab</li>
  <li>Small screwdriver</li>
</ul>

<h2>Instructions</h2>
<ol>
  <li>Open the bottom of the lighter by rotating the wheel that is at the base of the lighter. This will open the lighter, revealing the wick and the cotton.</li>
  <li>Use the small screwdriver to remove the cotton which is located inside the lighter. Be careful not to damage the wick.</li>
  <li>Use the cotton swab to clean the inside of the lighter. This will help to remove any dirt or debris that may be present.</li>
  <li>Fill the lighter with lighter fluid until it reaches the top of the wick. Do not overfill the lighter as this may cause it to malfunction.</li>
  <li>Replace the cotton and close the lighter. Make sure that the wheel is tightened securely so that the lighter does not leak.</li>
  <li>Once the lighter is closed, it is ready to be used. To light the lighter, simply turn the wheel and depress the flint.</li>
</ol>
