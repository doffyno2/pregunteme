  <li><b>Patch Repair</b> - If the damage is more severe, a patch repair may be necessary. This involves cutting out the damaged area and replacing it with a patch made of plastic or metal.</li>
  <li><b>Replacement</b> - If the damage is too severe to repair, the bumper may need to be replaced. This is usually the most expensive option, but it is often necessary if the damage is beyond repair.</li>
