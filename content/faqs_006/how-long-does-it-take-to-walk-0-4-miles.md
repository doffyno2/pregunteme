<h2>Factors That Affect Walking Time</h2>

The amount of time it takes to walk 0.4 miles depends on a variety of factors. These include:

<h3>Surface</h3>

<h3>Incline</h3>

If you're walking up or down a hill, the incline will affect your walking speed. Walking uphill will take more time than walking on level ground, while walking downhill can be faster.

<h3>Physical Condition</h3>

Your physical condition will also affect your walking speed. A person in good physical condition will be able to walk faster than someone who is out of shape.
