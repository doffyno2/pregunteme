<h2>What Is a Pomsky?</h2>

A pomsky is a hybrid breed of dog created by crossing a Pomeranian with a Siberian Husky. They are small in size, typically weighing no more than 25 lbs, and have fluffy, thick coats of fur. They have a friendly and playful personality, making them a popular choice for many pet owners.
