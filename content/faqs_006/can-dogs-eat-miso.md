<h2>What is Miso?</h2>

Miso is a paste-like condiment made from fermented soybeans, salt, and koji, a type of fungus. It has a salty, umami flavor and is used to add flavor to dishes like soups, sauces, and marinades. Miso comes in different colors and textures, ranging from white to red and from smooth to chunky.
