<h2>What is an Employment Authorization Document (EAD)?</h2>

An Employment Authorization Document (EAD) is a document issued by the United States Citizenship and Immigration Services (USCIS) that allows foreign nationals to legally work in the US for a temporary period of time. The EAD provides the authorization for the foreign national to legally work for a specific employer or job.
