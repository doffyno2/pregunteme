Fast travel allows players to instantly transport themselves from one area to another, allowing them to save time and energy in their exploration of the world. In this article, we'll discuss how to enable fast travel in Skyrim.

<h2>What is Fast Travel?</h2>

Fast travel is a feature in Skyrim that allows players to quickly move from one point to another. It is a convenient way to get around the expansive game world without having to walk, ride a horse, or use a fast travel network. Fast travel can be enabled by opening the map and selecting the desired location. The player will then be transported to that location without having to manually travel there.

<h2>How to Enable Fast Travel</h2>

Enabling fast travel in Skyrim is easy. First, open the map. You can open the map by pressing the “M” button on your keyboard or by selecting the “Map” option in the pause menu. Once the map is open, you can select any location that you have previously visited. Selecting a location will bring up a menu with options. Select the “Fast Travel” option to instantly transport yourself to that location.

<h2>Advantages of Fast Travel</h2>

Fast travel in Skyrim has a number of advantages. It allows players to quickly get around the expansive game world without having to manually travel by foot or horseback. Additionally, fast travel is a great way to explore the world and discover new locations.
