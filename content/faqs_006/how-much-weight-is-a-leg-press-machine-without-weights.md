A leg press machine is a piece of exercise equipment designed to strengthen the lower body. It is typically used for bodybuilding, powerlifting, and strength training. The machine is made up of a platform with a seat and backrest, and a platform where the user can place their feet. The user then pushes against the platform with their feet to lift the weight. The weight is usually in the form of plates, which can be added or removed as needed.
