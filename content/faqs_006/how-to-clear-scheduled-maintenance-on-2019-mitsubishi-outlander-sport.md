<p>Once you have all the necessary tools on hand, the next step is to follow the instructions below to clear the scheduled maintenance on your 2019 Mitsubishi Outlander Sport:</p>

<ol>
    <li>Start by opening the hood of your Outlander Sport and locate the oil filter. It should be located near the bottom of the engine bay.</li>
    <li>Once you have located the oil filter, use a flathead screwdriver to remove the oil filter cover.</li>
    <li>Once the cover is removed, use a socket and ratchet to remove the oil filter.</li>
    <li>Once the oil filter is removed, use a wrench to unscrew the oil drain plug from the oil pan.</li>

    <li>Once the cover is removed, use a pair of pliers to remove the air filter.</li>
    <li>Once the air filter is removed, use a wrench to unscrew the spark plug from the spark plug housing.</li>
    <li>Once the spark plug has been removed, use a socket and ratchet to remove the spark plug wire.</li>
    <li>Once the spark plug wire is removed, use a socket and ratchet to remove the spark plug.</li>
    <li>Once the spark plug has been removed, use a socket and ratchet to remove the spark plug housing.</li>
