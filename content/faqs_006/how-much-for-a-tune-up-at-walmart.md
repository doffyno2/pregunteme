<h2>What is a Tune Up?</h2>

<p>A tune up is a service that helps keep your vehicle running smoothly and efficiently. Generally, a tune up includes replacing spark plugs, air filters, fuel filters, and other parts that can wear out over time. It may also include checking the engine's compression and the ignition system. Having a tune up performed can help extend the life of your vehicle and keep it performing at its best.</p>

<h2>Tune Up Prices at Walmart</h2>

<p>The cost of a tune up at Walmart depends on the type and model of your vehicle. Generally, tune ups at Walmart cost between $200 and $400. This cost includes parts and labor, and the prices are comparable to other major chains like Midas and Firestone. The cost may also vary depending on the type of work that needs to be performed, the cost of the parts, and the location of the store.</p> 

<h2>Services Included in a Tune Up at Walmart</h2>

<p>Walmart typically offers several services in its tune up package. This can include replacing the spark plugs, replacing the air filter and fuel filter, checking the compression and ignition system, and performing a computer diagnostic check. Some locations may also offer other services such as checking the brakes, rotors, and drums, replacing the oil and oil filter, and checking the engine's timing.</p>
