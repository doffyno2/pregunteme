<h2>What Is A Transmission Seal?</h2>

<p>A transmission seal is an important part of your car's transmission system, and it is responsible for keeping the transmission fluid inside the transmission. The seal is made of rubber and plastic and is located around the edge of the transmission, where the transmission shaft enters the transmission housing. Over time, the seal can become worn or damaged, allowing transmission fluid to leak out.</p>

<h2>What Are The Signs Of A Leaking Transmission Seal?</h2>

<p>If your car is leaking transmission fluid, it is likely that the transmission seal is damaged. The most common signs of a leaking transmission seal include:</p>

<ul>
<li>A decrease in fuel efficiency.</li>
<li>A burning smell coming from the transmission.</li>
<li>A pool of reddish-brown fluid underneath the car.</li>
<li>Grinding or whining noises from the transmission.</li>
</ul>

<h2>How Much Does It Cost To Repair A Transmission Seal?</h2>

<p>The cost to repair a transmission seal can vary depending on the type of car, the severity of the leak, and the labor involved. The cost may be higher or lower depending on the make and model of the car, the severity of the leak, and the labor involved.</p>
