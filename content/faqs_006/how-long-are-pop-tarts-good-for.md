<h2>How Long Do Fresh Pop Tarts Last?</h2>

When stored properly, a fresh pop tart can last for up to 3 months in the pantry or cupboard. After the three months, the pop tart will still be edible, but the texture and taste may be slightly off. To ensure that your pop tarts are still fresh and tasty after the three months, it is best to store them in an airtight container or in the refrigerator, where they can last for up to 6 months. 

<h2>How Does Storing Pop Tarts Affect Their Shelf Life?</h2>

Storing pop tarts in an airtight container or in the refrigerator can extend the shelf life of the pop tarts by up to three months. This is because the airtight container or refrigerator keeps the pop tarts from drying out, which can cause them to become stale. Additionally, storing the pop tarts in a cool, dry place can help to preserve the texture and flavor of the pop tarts for a longer period of time. 

<h2>What Happens If Pop Tarts Are Not Stored Properly?</h2>

If pop tarts are not stored properly, they can become stale and inedible in a short amount of time. The pop tarts will lose their flavor and texture, and may even become moldy if they are left in a humid environment. Additionally, leaving pop tarts in a hot environment can cause them to become hard and crumbly. It is important to store pop tarts properly in order to maximize their shelf life and ensure that they remain fresh and tasty. 

<h2>How to Tell if Pop Tarts Are No Longer Fresh?</h2>

It is easy to tell when pop tarts are no longer fresh. If the pop tarts have a stale smell or taste, then they are no longer fresh. Additionally, if the pop tarts have a discolored appearance or have started to crumble, then they are no longer fresh and should be discarded. 

<h2>Tips for Storing Pop Tarts</h2>

To ensure that your pop tarts remain fresh for as long as possible, it is important to store them properly. Pop tarts should be stored in an airtight container or in the refrigerator, and it is best to store them away from heat and humidity. Additionally, it is important to inspect the pop tarts before eating them to ensure that they are still fresh and have not gone bad.
