<h2>Frequently Asked Questions</h2>

<h3>How long does it take to repair shoes?</h3>

<p>The amount of time it takes to repair shoes depends on the type of repair needed. Minor repairs, such as scuffs and tears, can usually be done within a day. More serious repairs, such as replacing soles, may take longer.</p>

<h3>Do I need to bring my shoes to the shop?</h3>

<p>In most cases, yes. You’ll need to bring your shoes to the shop so that they can assess the damage and determine the best course of action. You may also need to bring in other items, such as laces or insoles, depending on the type of repair.</p>

<h3>How much does it cost to repair shoes?</h3>

<p>The cost of repairing shoes can vary depending on the type of repair needed. Minor repairs, such as scuffs and tears, can cost anywhere from $5-$50. More serious repairs, such as replacing soles, can cost up to $100 or more.</p>
