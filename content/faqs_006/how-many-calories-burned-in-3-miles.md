<p>Generally speaking, the more you weigh, the more calories you’ll burn in 3 miles of walking or running. That’s because your body has to work harder to carry the extra weight. You can use a simple calorie burn formula to calculate the number of calories you’ll burn in 3 miles. </p>

<p>The formula is: Calories Burned = (Body Weight x 0.75 x Miles) / 200. So, if you weigh 150 pounds, you’ll burn (150 x 0.75 x 3) / 200 = 2.25 calories per mile. That means you’ll burn 6.75 calories in 3 miles. </p>
