<h2>Clean Your Car Regularly </h2>

<p>The best way to keep your car free of flies is to keep it clean. It’s important to wash your car regularly, inside and out. Make sure to use a gentle car wash soap that won’t strip the paint. It’s also important to vacuum the interior of your car on a regular basis. This will help to remove any food crumbs that can attract flies. </p>

<h2>Keep Food Out of Your Car </h2>

<p>If you’re going to be in your car for an extended period of time, it’s best to avoid eating in it. Even a small crumb can attract flies, and you don’t want to have to deal with them buzzing around your head. If you must eat in your car, make sure to clean up any crumbs or spills immediately. </p>

<h2>Install a Bug Screen </h2>

<p>If your car doesn’t already have a bug screen, you may want to consider installing one. A bug screen is a mesh material that is installed around the outside of your car to keep flies and other bugs out. They are relatively inexpensive and easy to install. </p>

<h2>Keep the Windows Closed </h2>

<p>Flies often enter a car through open windows. So, it’s important to keep your car windows closed when you’re not using them. This will help to prevent flies from entering the car and will also keep the interior cool. </p>
