<p>Spray bottles are a convenient way to apply liquids, such as cleaning solutions, to surfaces that need to be cleaned. The spray bottle is equipped with a spring-loaded pump mechanism that is triggered when the trigger is pressed. When the spring breaks or fails, the spray bottle will not spray. Replacing the spring in a spray bottle is a relatively easy task.</p>

<h2>What You Will Need</h2>

<p>To fix a spray bottle spring, you will need the following items:</p>

<ul>
  <li>Replacement spring</li>
  <li>Pliers</li>
  <li>Screwdriver</li>
  <li>Small flathead screwdriver</li>
</ul>

<h2>Step-by-Step Instructions</h2>

<p>Follow these steps to fix a spray bottle spring:</p>

<ol>
  <li>Unscrew the top of the spray bottle to remove the trigger and spring.</li>
  <li>Using pliers, remove the old spring from the trigger.</li>
  <li>Take the replacement spring and insert the one end into the trigger.</li>
  <li>Using a screwdriver, reattach the trigger to the spray bottle.</li>
  <li>Using a small flathead screwdriver, tighten the screws on the trigger.</li>
  <li>Reassemble the spray bottle and check to make sure it is working properly.</li>
</ol>

<h2>Tips</h2>

<ul>
  <li>Make sure the spring is firmly in place before reassembling the spray bottle.</li>
  <li>If the spray bottle does not seem to be working properly, check the spring for signs of wear or damage.</li>
</ul>
