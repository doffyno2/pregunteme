<h2>What Is Bull Riding?</h2>

Bull riding is a rodeo event that occurs when a rider attempts to ride a bull for 8 seconds. The rider must remain on the bull’s back for the entire 8 seconds in order to receive a score. The rider’s score will be based on their ability to stay on the bull as well as their control and style.

<h2>What Equipment Is Needed?</h2>

In order to safely participate in bull riding, there is some equipment that you will need to purchase. This includes a bull rope, protective helmet, bull bell, and gloves. Additionally, you may need to purchase a chaps and vest.
