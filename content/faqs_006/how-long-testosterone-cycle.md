<h2>Testosterone Cycle</h2>
A testosterone cycle is a period of time in which a person takes testosterone to increase their levels of the hormone. Testosterone cycles can range from a few weeks to several months and are usually done in conjunction with a proper diet and exercise regimen. The cycle is then broken down into a “loading phase” and a “maintenance phase”. During the loading phase, a person will take higher doses of testosterone in order to build up their levels. During the maintenance phase, they will take lower doses of testosterone to keep their levels at the desired level.
