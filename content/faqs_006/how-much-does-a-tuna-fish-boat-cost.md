<h2>What is a Tuna Fish Boat?</h2>

A tuna fish boat is a vessel that is designed to enable fishermen to catch the large fish in the tuna family, such as bluefin, yellowfin, and albacore. These boats are specially designed to make the most of the waters in which the tuna are found, such as deep offshore waters. They are usually large and well-equipped with the latest technology, including GPS and sonar systems, to help the fishermen locate the tuna.
