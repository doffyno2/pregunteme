<h2>What Are Almonds?</h2>

Almonds are a type of tree nut that is native to the Middle East and South Asia. They are a popular snack food and are also used in many recipes. Almonds are high in protein, fiber, vitamin E, magnesium, and other essential nutrients. They are also a good source of healthy fats, which can help reduce cholesterol levels and support heart health. 

<h2>How Many Almonds in a Tablespoon?</h2>

A single tablespoon of almonds contains about 8-10 whole almonds. This is equivalent to about 10-15 almond kernels. However, this can vary depending on the size of the almond kernels. A single tablespoon of almond meal or flour will contain about 16-20 almond kernels.
