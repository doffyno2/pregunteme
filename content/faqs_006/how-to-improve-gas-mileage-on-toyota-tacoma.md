<h2>Check the Tire Pressure</h2>

<p>The tire pressure of your Tacoma is an important factor in determining its fuel efficiency. When your tires are properly inflated, your Tacoma will run more efficiently, and you’ll get better gas mileage. You should check your tire pressure at least once a month and make sure that the pressure is at the manufacturer’s recommended level. If the pressure is too low, you should add air to the tires and if it’s too high, you should release some of the air.</p>

<h2>Monitor Your Driving Habits</h2>

<p>Your driving habits can have a huge effect on your Tacoma’s gas mileage. If you accelerate and brake suddenly, you’ll burn more fuel than if you accelerate and brake gradually. You should also try to drive at a steady speed, rather than accelerating and slowing down repeatedly. Additionally, if you’re driving in the city, you should try to anticipate traffic lights and stop signs so that you don’t have to come to a complete stop. This will also help you save fuel.</p>
