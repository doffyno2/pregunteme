What to Wear in 85 Degree Weather

The summer months can be a challenge when it comes to staying comfortable and stylish. One of the most common temperatures during the summer is 85 degrees, so the question is, what should you wear in 85 degree weather? In this article, we will discuss the best types of clothing to wear when the temperature reaches 85 degrees. 

<h2>Types of Clothing to Wear in 85 Degree Weather</h2>

When the temperature reaches 85 degrees, it’s important to choose the right types of clothing to ensure that you stay cool and comfortable. Here are some of the best types of clothing for 85 degree weather: 

<h3>Lightweight Fabrics</h3>

The most important factor to consider when dressing for 85 degree weather is the type of fabric you choose. Lightweight fabrics such as cotton, linen, and rayon are ideal for hot weather because they allow your skin to breathe and keep you cool. Avoid wearing heavy fabrics such as wool and denim, as they are more likely to make you feel hot and uncomfortable. 

<h3>Loose-Fitting Clothing</h3>

In addition to choosing the right fabric, it’s also important to choose clothing that is loose-fitting. Tight-fitting clothing can trap heat and make you feel uncomfortable, so opt for clothing that is loose and airy. Wide-leg trousers, maxi dresses, and oversized shirts are all great options. 

<h3>Breathable Shoes</h3>

It’s also important to choose the right shoes for 85 degree weather. Closed-toe shoes such as sneakers can make your feet hot and sweaty, so opt for open-toe shoes or sandals that allow your feet to breathe. Additionally, make sure that the shoes have good support and cushioning, as this will help keep your feet comfortable. 

<h3>Light Colors</h3>

Finally, it’s best to opt for light colors when dressing for 85 degree weather. Dark colors absorb more heat than light colors, so opt for light colors such as white, beige, and pastel shades. These colors will help keep you cool and comfortable in the hot weather.
