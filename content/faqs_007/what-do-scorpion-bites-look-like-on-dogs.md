<h2>What Are Scorpions?</h2>

Scorpions are predatory arachnids of the order Scorpiones within the class Arachnida. They have eight legs and are easily recognized by the pair of grasping claws and the narrow, segmented tail, often carried in a characteristic forward curve over the back, ending with a venomous stinger. Scorpions are found widely distributed over all continents except Antarctica, and have adapted to a wide range of habitats.

<h2>What Do Scorpion Bites Look Like On Dogs?</h2>

In some cases, the dog may experience difficulty breathing, excessive drooling, and vomiting. If the bite is severe, or the dog has a known allergy to scorpion venom, they may experience more severe reactions like seizures or paralysis.

<h2>What Should I Do If My Dog Is Stung By A Scorpion?</h2>

<h2>How Can I Tell If My Dog Has Been Stung By A Scorpion?</h2>
