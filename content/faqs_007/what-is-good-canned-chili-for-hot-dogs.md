<h3>How long does canned chili last?</h3>

<p>Canned chili typically has a shelf life of around two years. However, it's important to check the expiration date on the can before consuming, as each can may vary. Additionally, it's best to store canned chili in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight.</p>

<h3>What is the best way to reheat canned chili?</h3>

<p>The best way to reheat canned chili is in a pot on the stove or in the microwave. Be sure to stir the chili regularly to avoid scorching. Additionally, adding a small amount of water or broth can help to keep the chili from drying out.</p>

<h3>Can canned chili be frozen?</h3>

<p>Yes, canned chili can be frozen. However, it's important to remember that freezing will affect the texture and flavor of the chili, so it's best to consume it within a few months of freezing. Additionally, be sure to transfer the chili to an airtight container before freezing.</p>

<h3>Are canned chili and chili con carne the same thing?</h3>

<p>No, canned chili and chili con carne are not the same thing. Additionally, canned chili is usually ready to eat, while chili con carne typically needs to be cooked before it is ready to eat.</p>
