What is an Address Name?

An address name is a unique identifier assigned to a specific location or address. It is typically used to identify a physical or virtual location, or its associated services or activities. It is also used to facilitate communication and navigation between two points.

In virtual terms, an address name is used to identify a specific website or online service. This could include a domain name, an IP address, or even a specific page within a website.
