<h3>Motocross Bikes</h3>
Motocross bikes are designed for high-speed track racing. These bikes typically feature powerful engines and are capable of reaching speeds in excess of 80 mph. <h3>Enduro Bikes</h3>
Enduro bikes are designed for long-distance off-road racing. These bikes feature powerful engines and are capable of reaching speeds in excess of 60 mph. Enduro bikes tend to be slightly smaller than motocross bikes, as they are designed for long distance riding rather than high speed racing. 

<h3>Cross Country Bikes</h3>
Cross country bikes are designed for cross country trail riding. These bikes are typically smaller than both motocross and enduro bikes and feature engines with less power and performance. Cross country bikes are designed to be lightweight and maneuverable, making them ideal for navigating tight trails and technical terrain.
