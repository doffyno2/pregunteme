<p>Having a fresh scent is essential in any social situation. It's not only important to be presentable, but it also prevents potential embarrassment. <h2>1. Plan Ahead</h2>

<p>The best way to avoid forgetting deodorant is to plan ahead. Make it a habit to always have an extra deodorant in your bag or car. <p>If you forget your deodorant and don't have any spare, dry shampoo is another great alternative. Dry shampoo not only absorbs moisture and oil, but it also adds volume and texture to your hair. <h2>3. Use Baby Powder</h2>

<p>If you don't have any dry shampoo, baby powder is another great alternative. Not only does it absorb moisture, but it also helps mask any unpleasant odors. <h2>5. Wear Breathable Clothing</h2>

<p>Another way to help prevent body odor is to wear breathable clothing. Cotton and linen fabrics are great for absorbing moisture and allowing air to circulate. Additionally, make sure to avoid wearing tight-fitting clothing, as this can cause sweating and make any body odors more noticeable.</p>

<p>If all else fails, then the best solution is to take a shower. Not only will this help remove any unpleasant odors, but it will also help you feel refreshed and energized.
