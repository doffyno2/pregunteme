<h2>How to Make Garlic Scent for Fishing</h2>

<p>Making garlic scent for fishing is actually quite simple. All you need is garlic and some water. Start by peeling and crushing two cloves of garlic. Place the crushed garlic in a jar or container and fill it with water. Place the lid on the container and leave it to sit for several hours. The longer it sits, the stronger the garlic scent will be. Once the garlic scent is strong enough, strain the liquid and transfer it to a spray bottle. This garlic scent can now be sprayed onto lures and bait to attract fish.</p>
