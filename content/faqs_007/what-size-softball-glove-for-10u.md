<h2>What is 10U Softball?</h2>

10U softball is a youth softball league, with players aged typically between 8 and 10 years old. <h2>What to Look for in a 10U Softball Glove?</h2>

When choosing a glove for 10U softball, it is important to look for a glove that is comfortable and allows the player to move their hand freely. The glove should fit snugly without being too tight, and should have a pocket that is large enough for the ball to be caught and held securely. Additionally, the material of the glove should be breathable and durable, allowing the player to remain comfortable during the game.

<h2>What Size Softball Glove for 10U?</h2>

The size of the softball glove for 10U players depends on a few factors, such as the player’s hand size, the position they play and how often they will use the glove. The size of the glove should be comfortable for the player, and allow them to catch the ball securely.
