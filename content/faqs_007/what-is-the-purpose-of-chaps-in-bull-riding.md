<h2>What Are Chaps?</h2>

Chaps are a protective clothing item that riders wear when they are riding bulls. They are usually made out of leather or a similar material and they are designed to provide protection to the rider’s legs and lower body. Chaps usually have a long, loose fit and they are often decorated with fringe.
