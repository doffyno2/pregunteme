<h2>What are Bike Training Wheels?</h2>

<p>Bike training wheels are two small wheels that are attached to the rear axle of a bike. They allow a child to learn how to balance and ride a bike without having to worry about falling off. <h2>Do Bike Training Wheels Touch the Ground?</h2>

<p>Bike training wheels should not always touch the ground. The amount of contact between the training wheels and the ground will depend on the size of the bike, the size of the wheels, and the weight of the rider. For example, if the bike is too big for the rider, there may be too much weight on the training wheels, causing them to drag on the ground. On the other hand, if the bike is too small for the rider, there may not be enough weight on the training wheels for them to make contact with the ground. </p>

<p>There are both pros and cons to having bike training wheels that touch the ground.
