<h2>How to Do a Burpee Exercise</h2>

<p>Burpees are a full-body exercise that involve several movements. To do a burpee, begin in a standing position with your feet shoulder-width apart and your arms by your sides. Squat down and place your hands on the ground in front of you, then jump your feet back into a plank position. Immediately lower your body into a push-up, then push yourself back up. Once you are back in the plank position, jump your feet forward to the starting position and stand up. That is one repetition. It is important to remember to keep your core engaged throughout the entire exercise.</p>

<p>For an added challenge, you can add an extra jump at the end of the repetition. This is known as a “burpee jump” and can help increase the intensity of the exercise. It is also important to remember to keep your back flat and your core engaged throughout the entire exercise.</p>
