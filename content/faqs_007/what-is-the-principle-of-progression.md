<h2>What is a Progression?</h2>

A progression is a sequence of numbers or operations that is repeated in a regular fashion. It is important to note that the sequence does not have to be constant or in a specific order. Instead, a progression is characterized by a pattern that is repeated throughout the sequence. Examples of progressions include the Fibonacci sequence, the powers of 2, and geometric progressions.
