<h2>What is a Visitor Post?</h2>

Visitor posts are posts on a Facebook page created by someone who is not an administrator or editor of the page. Visitor posts can be seen by anyone with access to the page, including other administrators, editors, and the general public. Visitor posts may include images, videos, comments, and links.
