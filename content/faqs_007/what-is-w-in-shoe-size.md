<h2>What Does “W” Mean On Shoe Size?</h2>

The letter “W” stands for width. This is the measurement of the shoe from side to side. Shoes come in different widths, and the “W” indicates that the shoe is a wide width. This can vary from brand to brand, but generally, a “W” is equal to a wide width.
