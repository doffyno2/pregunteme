<p>The standover height of a bike is the vertical distance from the ground to the top of the top tube or, in other words, the lowest point at which you can stand over the bike while straddling it. If the standover height is too low, it could result in discomfort or even injury.</p>

<p>Once you have both measurements, you can then calculate the standover height. To do this, subtract the inseam measurement from the top tube measurement. The result will be the standover height.
