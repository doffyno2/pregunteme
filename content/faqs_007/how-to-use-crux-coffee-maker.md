<h2>What You Need to Get Started</h2>

<p>Before you can use your Crux coffee maker, you will need to gather the necessary items. You will need the following items:</p>

<ul>
<li>Coffee grounds</li>
<li>Filtered water</li>
<li>A measuring cup</li>
<li>A mug</li>
</ul>

<h2>Steps to Use Crux Coffee Maker</h2>

<p>Once you have gathered the items you need, you can start using your Crux coffee maker. The following steps will help you make a perfect cup of coffee:</p>

<ol>
<li>Fill the water reservoir of the Crux coffee maker with filtered water and make sure it is not overfilled. You can fill it up to the maximum line indicated on the reservoir.</li>
<li>Measure out the desired amount of coffee grounds and add it to the filter basket. For a stronger cup of coffee, use more coffee grounds.</li>
<li>Place the filter basket in the coffee maker.</li>
<li>Place a mug on the mug stand.</li>
<li>Turn the coffee maker on. The power button is located on the back of the machine.</li>
<li>Once the coffee is done brewing, turn off the coffee maker and pour the coffee into the mug.</li>
</ol>
