<h2>Steps to Turn on a Fossil Watch</h2>

<p>Turning on a Fossil watch is a straightforward process that requires only a few simple steps. To begin, check the battery of the watch to make sure it is functioning properly. If the battery is dead, replace it with a new one.</p>

<ol>
  <li>Once the battery is replaced, press the crown of the watch (the knob located on the right side of the watch face).</li>
  <li>The watch will now be in time-setting mode. Rotate the crown clockwise to set the time.</li>
  <li>Once the time has been set, press the crown of the watch again to exit time-setting mode.</li>
  <li>The watch should now be turned on and functioning properly.</li>
</ol>
