	<li><b>Digital gauges:</b> These are more advanced than dial gauges, and are often used to measure a range of parameters, such as temperature and speed. They display readings in numerical form, rather than on a dial.</li>
	<li><b>Analog gauges:</b> These are similar to dial gauges, but have a more complex design. They are often used to measure a range of parameters, such as pressure and temperature.</li>
