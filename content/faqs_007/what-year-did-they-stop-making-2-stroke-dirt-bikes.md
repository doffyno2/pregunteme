<p>Two-stroke dirt bikes were first produced in the mid-1950s, when companies such as CZ, Husqvarna, and Bultaco began to produce off-road motorcycles. <h2>The Decline of Two-Stroke Dirt Bikes</h2>

<p>In the early 2000s, four-stroke dirt bikes began to take over the market. By the mid-2000s, two-strokes had become rare, and by the early 2010s, most manufacturers had stopped producing two-strokes altogether.</p>
