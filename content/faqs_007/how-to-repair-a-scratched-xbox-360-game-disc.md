<h2>Steps to Repair a Scratched Xbox 360 Game Disc</h2>

<p>The first step in repairing a scratched Xbox 360 game disc is to clean the disc. You can do this by using a cotton swab and a mild cleaning solution, such as isopropyl alcohol. Gently wipe the cotton swab across the disc, removing any dirt and debris. Make sure to avoid any circular motion when cleaning the disc, as this could further damage the disc.</p>

<p>After the disc has been cleaned, you will need to apply a repair solution. Simply follow the instructions provided on the product and apply the solution to the disc, using a cotton swab. Once the solution has been applied, allow the disc to dry and then attempt to play the game.</p>
