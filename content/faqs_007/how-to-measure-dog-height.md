<h2>Tools Needed to Measure Dog Height</h2>

<p>There are a few tools you’ll need to measure your pup’s height correctly. You will need a tape measure, a flat surface, and a marker. Before you begin, make sure the surface you’ll be measuring your pup on is flat, like the floor. If you don’t have a tape measure, you can use a ruler, yardstick, or any other measuring device that is marked in inches.</p>

<h2>Measuring Dog Height</h2>

<p>Once you have gathered the necessary tools, it’s time to measure your pup! Begin by standing your pup on the flat surface and having someone hold them in place. Next, mark the top of your pup’s head with the marker. To ensure accuracy, you may want to make two marks, one on the left and one on the right side of their head. Once you have marked their head, you can use the tape measure to measure the distance between the two marks. This is your pup’s height!</p>

<h2>Measuring Dog Height at Home</h2>

<p>Measuring your pup’s height at home can be a bit tricky. It is best to enlist the help of a friend or family member to help you get an accurate measurement. This will help to ensure accuracy as even the most still pup can move during the measuring process.</p>
