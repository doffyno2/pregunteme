Lime green is a bright and vibrant colour that is often used as a statement colour in interior design, fashion, and art. It's a colour that stands out and adds a fun and playful touch to any space. But it can be hard to know what colours match lime green and how to incorporate it into your decor or wardrobe.

<h2>How to Choose Colours That Go With Lime Green</h2>

When choosing colours that go with lime green, the key is to pick complementary colours that won’t clash with the bright green hue. The best way to start is to look at the colour wheel and find the opposite of lime green – blue. Blue is a great colour to pair with lime green as it creates a subtle contrast while still maintaining a cohesive look. Other colours that look great with lime green are yellows, oranges, and purples. Neutral colours like white, grey, and black are also great for adding contrast to lime green and can help to tone down the intensity of the colour.
