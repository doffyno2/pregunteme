<h2>What is Hydrolyzed Protein?</h2>

<p>Hydrolyzed protein is a type of protein that has been processed to break down the larger proteins into smaller peptides or amino acids. This makes it easier for the body to break down and absorb the proteins. It also helps reduce potential digestive issues that can be caused by large proteins.</p>

<p>Hydrolyzed proteins are most commonly used in pet foods, as they are easier for pets to digest than larger proteins. This makes them a great option for dogs with sensitive stomachs or allergies. It is important to note, however, that hydrolyzed proteins should not be used as a replacement for regular proteins. Instead, it should be used in addition to other proteins to ensure that your pet is getting all of the essential nutrients it needs.</p>
