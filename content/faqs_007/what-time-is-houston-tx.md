<h2>Daylight Saving Time in Houston, TX</h2>

Houston, TX is one of the many places in the US to observe Daylight Saving Time (DST). DST is a system of advancing clocks one hour forward during the spring and summer months. In Houston, the clocks are moved forward one hour on the second Sunday in March, and are then moved back one hour on the first Sunday in November.
