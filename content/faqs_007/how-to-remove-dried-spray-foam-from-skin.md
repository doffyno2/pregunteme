<h2>What is Spray Foam?</h2>

Spray foam is a two-part polyurethane-based foam that is used as an insulating material in homes, buildings, and other structures. It comes in two parts, an isocyanate and an activator, which are mixed together and applied as a foam using a special applicator. The foam expands and hardens as it cures, forming an airtight seal that helps to insulate the structure from heat and cold.
