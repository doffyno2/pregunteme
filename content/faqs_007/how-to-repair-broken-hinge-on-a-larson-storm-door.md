<p>Replacing a broken hinge on a Larson storm door is often a straightforward process, but it can be a tricky task, depending on the type of hinge you need to replace. In this article, we will look at how to replace both the top and bottom hinges on a Larson storm door, as well as how to remove, replace, and adjust the hinges.</p>

<h2>What You'll Need to Replace a Larson Storm Door Hinge</h2>

<p>Before you begin the process of replacing a broken hinge on a Larson door, you'll need to have a few items on hand. This list includes pliers, a screwdriver, a drill, and a replacement hinge.</p>

<h2>Removing the Old Hinge</h2>

<p>The first step in replacing a broken hinge on a Larson storm door is to remove the old hinge. To do this, open the door and locate the hinge screws. Using a screwdriver, remove the screws and then slide the hinge off the door frame. Once the hinge is removed, you'll be able to inspect it for any signs of wear or damage. If the hinge is damaged, you'll need to replace it.</p>

<h2>Installing the New Hinge</h2>

<p>Once you've removed the old hinge, you can install the replacement. Begin by sliding the new hinge onto the door frame. Make sure the hinge is properly aligned and then use a drill to secure the hinge in place. Once the hinge is secured, you can replace the screw and tighten it.</p>

<h2>Aligning the Hinge</h2>

<p>Once the new hinge is in place, you'll need to align it. To do this, open the door and look for any gaps between the door and the frame. If there is a gap, you'll need to adjust the hinge. To do this, use pliers to adjust the tension on the hinge until the gap is eliminated. You may need to repeat this process several times to get the hinge properly aligned.</p>

<h2>FAQs About Repairing a Broken Hinge on a Larson Storm Door</h2>

<h3>How Do I Know If My Hinge is Broken?</h3>

<p>If your door is not opening and closing properly, it is likely that the hinge is broken. Look for signs of wear or damage on the hinge and if it is broken, you will need to replace it.</p>

<h3>What Is the Best Way to Secure a Larson Storm Door Hinge?</h3>

<p>The best way to secure a Larson storm door hinge is to use a drill to secure it in place. Once the hinge is secured, you can replace the screw and tighten it.</p>

<h3>Do I Need to Align the Hinge After Replacing It?</h3>

<p>Yes, it is important to align the hinge after replacing it. To do this, open the door and look for any gaps between the door and the frame. If there is a gap, you'll need to adjust the hinge using pliers to adjust the tension on the hinge until the gap is eliminated.</p>

<h3>How Often Should I Check My Larson Storm Door Hinge?</h3>

<p>It is a good idea to check your Larson storm door hinge periodically to make sure it is in good working condition. Look for signs of wear or damage and if necessary, replace the hinge.</p>
