<h2>What Is a Prescription Sunglass?</h2>

A prescription sunglass is a type of eyewear that is specially designed to provide protection from the sun’s rays while also allowing a person to see clearly. Prescription sunglasses are made with special lenses that contain a prescription, which is a personalized optical prescription that is unique to each individual. This prescription is used to ensure that the lenses are tailored to the specific needs of the wearer, allowing them to see clearly despite the presence of the sun’s UV rays. 

<h2>Why Aren't Prescription Sunglasses As Dark As Regular Sunglasses?</h2>

The main reason why prescription sunglasses are not as dark as regular sunglasses is due to the fact that prescription lenses are designed to allow a certain amount of light to pass through them. This is done so that the wearer can still see clearly and comfortably, as the lenses are designed to reduce the amount of light that reaches the eyes. Regular sunglasses, on the other hand, are designed to block out all light, which makes them much darker than prescription sunglasses. 

<h2>Are There Ways to Make Prescription Sunglasses Darker?</h2>

Yes, there are ways to make prescription sunglasses darker. One way is to opt for a darker tinted lens. Darker tints can help to reduce the amount of light that passes through the lenses, making them darker and more effective at blocking out the sun’s rays. Another option is to choose polarized lenses, which are designed to reduce glare and improve visibility in bright light. Polarized lenses can also help to make prescription sunglasses darker.
