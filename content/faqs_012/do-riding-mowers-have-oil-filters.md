<h2>What Is an Oil Filter?</h2>

<p>An oil filter is a device that removes impurities from the oil in your engine. It is designed to trap dirt, dust, and other particles that can damage the engine. The oil filter helps keep the oil clean and free of debris so that it can lubricate and protect the engine components. Oil filters are typically located on the side of the engine and are easy to replace.</p>

<h2>Why Are Oil Filters Important?</h2>

<p>Oil filters are an important part of keeping your engine running smoothly. Without an oil filter, the oil in your engine can become contaminated with dirt, debris, and other particles. Regularly replacing your oil filter is an important part of regular maintenance and will help keep your engine running smoothly for years to come.</p>
