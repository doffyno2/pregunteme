<h2>What is Foundation Repair?</h2>

<p>Foundation repair is the process of strengthening and restoring the structural integrity of a home’s foundation. It is typically required when a home’s foundation has been weakened or damaged due to soil erosion, poor construction, or extreme weather conditions. Foundation repair is often necessary to ensure the safety and stability of a home, and the cost can be quite high. </p>
