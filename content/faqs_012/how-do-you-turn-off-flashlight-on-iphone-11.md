<h3>Step 1: Access The Control Center</h3>

<h3>Step 2: Tap The Flashlight Icon</h3>

Once the Control Center is open, you should see a flashlight icon at the bottom of the screen. Tap on this icon to turn off the flashlight on your iPhone 11.
