<h2>Tools Needed to Fix a Flat Tire</h2>

<p>First, you’ll need to make sure you have the right tools in order to fix your flat tire. You’ll need a tire iron or a lug wrench, a jack, and a spare tire. You’ll also need a few other tools such as a tire pressure gauge, a tire plug kit, and a tire inflator.</p>

<h2>Steps to Fix a Flat Tire</h2>

<p>Once you have all the tools you need to fix your flat tire, it’s time to get started. First, you’ll need to jack up the vehicle and remove the lug nuts from the flat tire. Once the lug nuts are removed, you can remove the flat tire and replace it with the spare. Once the spare is in place, you can then use the tire inflator to fill the tire with air. Next, you’ll need to check the tire pressure with a tire pressure gauge to make sure it’s at the proper level. If the pressure is too low, you can use the tire plug kit to plug the hole and fill it with air. After that, you can replace the lug nuts and lower the vehicle. </p>
