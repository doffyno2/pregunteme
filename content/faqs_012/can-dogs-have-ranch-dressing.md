<p>Ranch dressing typically contains a variety of ingredients that are unhealthy for dogs. Garlic and onion are two of the most common ingredients found in ranch dressing. Additionally, ranch dressing typically contains a large amount of salt, which can lead to dehydration and electrolyte imbalances. </p>

<p>Ranch dressing is also high in fat and calories. While some fat is necessary in a dog's diet, too much can lead to obesity. Additionally, high-fat foods can increase the risk of pancreatitis in dogs. </p>
