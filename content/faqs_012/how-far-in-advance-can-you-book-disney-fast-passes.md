<p>Disney FastPass is a convenient way to save time and enjoy the attractions at Disney World and Disneyland. With FastPass, guests can book a priority access to select attractions, allowing them to skip the long lines. But how far in advance can you book? Read on to find out.</p>

<h2>What is Disney FastPass?</h2>

<p>Disney FastPass is a service provided by Disney World and Disneyland that allows guests to book priority access to select attractions. This means that instead of waiting in line for an attraction, guests can book a FastPass and come back at a designated time to enter the attraction with minimal wait. Guests can book up to three FastPasses per day, with a maximum of one per hour. Disney FastPass is free for all guests.</p>

<h2>How Far in Advance Can You Book Disney FastPass?</h2>

<p>At Disney World, you can book FastPasses up to 30 days in advance. If you are staying at an on-site Disney World resort, you can book FastPasses up to 60 days in advance. For Disneyland, you can book up to 14 days in advance. If you are staying at a Disney Resort hotel, you can book up to 30 days in advance. It is important to note that FastPass booking windows open at 7am Eastern time, so you should plan accordingly.</p>

<h2>How to Book Disney FastPass?</h2>

<p>Disney FastPass can be booked in three ways: online, through the Disney World or Disneyland app, or at the park. To book online or through the app, you will need a valid ticket, Annual Pass, or Disney Vacation Club membership. Once you have logged in, you can select the date and time and the attraction you would like to book. You can also book FastPasses at the park, either by using the FastPass kiosks located near each attraction, or by asking a Disney Cast Member at the attraction entrance.</p>

<h2>What Attractions Offer Disney FastPass?</h2>

<p>At Disney World, FastPasses are available for most attractions and some shows. At Disneyland, FastPasses are available for most attractions, some shows, and select character meet-and-greets. A complete list of attractions and shows offering FastPass can be found on the Disney World or Disneyland websites.</p>

<h2>What Happens If I Miss My Disney FastPass Time?</h2>

<p>If you arrive after your scheduled FastPass time, you will not be able to use your FastPass. However, you can still enter the attraction using the regular line. If you are unable to visit the attraction during your FastPass window, you can cancel your FastPass and book a new one.</p>

<h2>Can I Change or Cancel My Disney FastPass?</h2>

<p>Yes, you can change or cancel your Disney FastPass at any time. To change your FastPass, simply book a new one. To cancel your FastPass, simply click the “Cancel” button on your My Disney Experience account. You can also cancel your FastPass at the FastPass kiosks located near each attraction.</p>
