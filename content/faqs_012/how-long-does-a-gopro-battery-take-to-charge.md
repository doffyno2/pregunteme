<p>GoPros are one of the most popular action cameras in the world. They are known for their durability, portability, and high quality footage. <p>The answer to this question depends on a few factors. The battery type, capacity, and charger type all play a role in determining the charging time. Generally speaking, it takes around two hours to fully charge a GoPro battery.</p>

<h2>Factors That Affect Charging Time</h2>

<p>The charging time for a GoPro battery can vary depending on the capacity of the battery, the type of charger you are using, and the condition of the battery. Here's a breakdown of the factors that can affect how long it takes to charge a GoPro battery.</p>

<h3>Battery Type</h3>

<p>GoPros use either lithium-ion or lithium-polymer batteries, depending on the model. Lithium-ion batteries are generally used in older GoPro models, while lithium-polymer batteries are used in newer models. Lithium-polymer batteries tend to charge faster than their lithium-ion counterparts.</p>

<h3>Battery Capacity</h3>

<p>The battery capacity specifies how much power the battery can hold and how long it will last. The higher the capacity of the battery, the longer it will take to charge. For example, a battery with a capacity of 1160mAh will take longer to charge than a battery with a capacity of 800mAh.</p>

<h3>Charger Type</h3>

<p>The type of charger you are using can also affect the charging time. Chargers that offer more amperage will charge the battery faster than those that offer less amperage. For example, a charger that delivers 2A of current will charge the battery faster than one that delivers 1A of current.</p>

<h3>Condition of the Battery</h3>

<p>The condition of the battery can also affect the charging time. If the battery is completely drained, it will take longer to charge than if it is partially drained. This is because the battery has to work harder to recharge when it is completely drained.</p>

<h2>Tips for Charging Your GoPro Battery</h2>

<p>Here are some tips to help you get the most out of your GoPro battery:</p>

<ul>
  <li>Use a high-quality charger that is compatible with your GoPro model.</li>
  <li>Don't leave your battery unused for long periods of time. Charge it every few months to keep it in good condition.</li>
  <li>Don't leave your battery charging for more than 24 hours. This can damage the battery and reduce its life.</li>
  <li>Try to avoid using the battery when it is low on charge. This can reduce the battery's capacity and performance.</li>
  <li>If you're not using your GoPro for an extended period of time, store it with a full charge.</li>
</ul>
