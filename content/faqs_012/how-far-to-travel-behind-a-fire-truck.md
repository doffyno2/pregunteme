<p>The law is clear when it comes to how far you should travel behind a fire truck. Depending on the state, the law may require you to stay at least 500 feet or 250 feet behind the fire truck. <p>When you see a fire truck, it is important to take action immediately. First, you should check that you are following the law. <h2>Other Tips for Following a Fire Truck</h2>

<p>When following a fire truck, there are a few other tips that you should keep in mind. Firstly, you should avoid passing the fire truck at any time. This could cause the fire truck to have to take evasive action and could put you at risk of an accident.</p>
