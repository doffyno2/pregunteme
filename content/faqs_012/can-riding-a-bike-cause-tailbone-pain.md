<h2>What is Tailbone Pain?</h2>

<p>Tailbone pain, also known as coccydynia, is a common condition characterized by pain in the bottom of your spine, near the tailbone. The pain can range from mild to severe, and often feels worse when sitting or lying down. It can also be accompanied by tenderness, swelling, and difficulty with bowel movements.</p>
