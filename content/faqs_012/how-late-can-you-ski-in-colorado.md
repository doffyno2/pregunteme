<p>In general, the ski season in Colorado begins in mid-November and ends in mid-April. However, some ski resorts may open earlier or close later than this, depending on the amount of snowfall. On the other hand, some ski resorts may remain open until early May if there is still enough snow.</p>

<p>It’s also important to note that some ski resorts may close down for a few days during the ski season due to lack of snow. <h2>What Is the Best Time to Ski in Colorado?</h2>

<p>The best time to ski in Colorado depends on your particular preferences. If you prefer powder skiing, then late winter and early spring are the best times to ski in Colorado. If you’re looking for a more challenging terrain, then late fall and early winter are the times for you. During this time, the snow is usually harder and there is less of a chance of avalanches.</p>
