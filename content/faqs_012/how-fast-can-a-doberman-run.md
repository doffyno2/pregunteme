<h2>Physical Attributes of a Doberman</h2>

The Doberman is a large and powerful dog, with a sleek black and tan coat. It stands between 24-28 inches tall and weighs between 65-90 pounds when fully grown. <h2>How the Doberman Runs</h2>

The Doberman is known for its distinctive gait. It is a trotter, meaning that it uses its two hind legs to propel itself forward. This gives the Doberman an elegant, graceful running style that looks like it is gliding across the ground.
