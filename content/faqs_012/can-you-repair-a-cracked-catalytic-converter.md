<h2>What Causes a Cracked Catalytic Converter?</h2>

There are several causes of a cracked catalytic converter. The most common cause is a build-up of carbon deposits in the converter or in the exhaust system. This can be caused by using the wrong fuel, or by using a fuel with a high sulfur content. Another potential cause is an engine misfire, which can cause the catalytic converter to overheat and crack. Finally, physical damage to the exhaust system can also cause a crack in the catalytic converter.
