<h2>What is Powdered Sugar?</h2>

<p>Powdered sugar, also known as confectioner's sugar, is a type of sugar which is made from finely ground granulated sugar. It is used in baking and to sweeten drinks, and it often has cornstarch added to it to prevent clumping. It is a very fine powder which dissolves easily in water.</p>

<p>Powdered sugar does not contain any nutritional value for your dog, and is essentially just empty calories. When consumed in large amounts, it can quickly lead to obesity in dogs. It also can give your dog an upset stomach and cause diarrhea.</p>
