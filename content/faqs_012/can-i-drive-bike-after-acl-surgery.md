<h2>What is ACL Surgery?</h2>

<p>An anterior cruciate ligament tear is a very common knee injury. It is caused by a sudden twisting or jamming of the knee, often during sports or other physical activities. If the tear is severe enough, then you may require surgery to repair the ligament. Surgery is used to reconstruct the ligament and restore full range of motion and stability to the knee joint. Depending on the severity of the tear, the surgery can be performed with arthroscopic techniques or with open surgery, where a larger incision is made in the knee.</p>
