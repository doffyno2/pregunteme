<h2>What is a Marshmallow?</h2>

A marshmallow is a sweet confection made from sugar, corn syrup, gelatin, and sometimes flavorings or food coloring. Marshmallows are usually white and fluffy, and they come in a variety of shapes and sizes. They can be square, round, rectangular, or even shaped like animals.

<h2>What Does a Marshmallow Taste Like?</h2>

Marshmallows are sweet and have a slightly chewy texture. They have a mild flavor that is reminiscent of vanilla. Marshmallows can be flavored with different extracts, such as mint, almond, or orange, and they can be colored with food dye. 

<h2>How Are Marshmallows Made?</h2>

Marshmallows are made by combining sugar, corn syrup, and gelatin and then whipping the mixture until it is light and fluffy. The mixture is then poured into a pan and allowed to cool. After cooling, the marshmallows are cut into desired shapes with cookie cutters or poured into molds.
