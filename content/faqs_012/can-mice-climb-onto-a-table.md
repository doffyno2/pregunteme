<h2>How Mice Can Climb onto a Table</h2>

Mice are incredibly agile creatures, they can jump up to a foot in the air, and can climb walls and other surfaces with ease. This means that they can easily climb onto a table. In fact, it is quite common for mice to climb onto tables and other surfaces in search of food or shelter. 

<h2>Why Mice Climb onto Tables</h2>

Mice climb onto tables for a variety of reasons. They may be seeking a food source, such as crumbs or spilled food. They may also be seeking shelter from predators or from cold temperatures. 

<h2>Preventing Mice from Climbing onto Tables</h2>

The best way to prevent mice from climbing onto tables is to keep surfaces clean. This means wiping down surfaces regularly and vacuuming up any food or crumbs that have been left behind. Keeping food sources away from the table is also important. This includes storing food in airtight containers and not leaving food out. 

<h2>Using Mouse Traps to Catch Mice</h2>

Using mouse traps is one of the most effective ways to catch mice that are climbing onto tables. Mouse traps come in a variety of sizes and styles, and can be used to catch mice quickly and easily.
