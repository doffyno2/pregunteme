<h2>What is Steak Fat?</h2>

Steak fat is a type of fat that comes from beef, such as ribeye or sirloin steak. It's usually found in the form of rendered beef fat, which is the fat that has been cooked and drained off the meat. This fat is usually used to season and cook food, and it's high in saturated fat and calories.
