<p>The first step you should take when you have trouble repairing Office 2013 is to make sure that your system meets the minimum requirements for the software. <p>If your system still can't repair Office 2013, then you should run the Microsoft Office Diagnostics tool. This tool will scan your system for any potential issues and help you resolve them.
