<h2>What is Cornstarch?</h2>

Cornstarch is a type of starch made from corn that is commonly used as a thickener for sauces, gravies, and other dishes. It is made from the endosperm of the corn kernel, which is then processed and ground into a fine powder. Cornstarch is often used as a substitute for flour, which can be harder to find and more expensive.

The short answer to this question is yes, you can use cornstarch to make gravy for your pup. <h2>How to Make Gravy With Cornstarch For Dogs</h2>

Making gravy with cornstarch for your pup can be done in a few easy steps. First, start by heating up some oil or butter in a pan over medium heat. Once the oil is hot, add in some diced onion, garlic, and other vegetables of your choice. Cook for a few minutes until the vegetables are tender. 

Next, add in your broth or stock of choice and bring to a simmer. Once the broth is simmering, slowly add in the cornstarch while stirring constantly. Continue stirring until the gravy thickens to the desired consistency. Finally, season with salt and pepper to taste, and serve over your pup’s favorite meal.
