<p>Before we can answer the question of just how bright a 1500 lumens flashlight is, it’s important to understand what lumens are. Lumens is the unit of measure used to describe the amount of light that is emitted from a light source. In other words, lumens measure the brightness of a light. The higher the lumens, the brighter the light. A standard 40-watt incandescent light bulb, for example, has an output of about 450 lumens.</p>
