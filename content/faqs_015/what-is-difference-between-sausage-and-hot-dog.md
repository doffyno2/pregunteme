<h2>What Is Sausage?</h2>

Sausage is a type of processed meat that is made from finely chopped or ground pork, beef, or poultry. It also contains other ingredients such as herbs, spices, and salt. The mixture is stuffed into a casing, which can either be natural or artificial. Sausage is then smoked, cured, or air-dried to give it flavor and texture. 

<h2>What Is Hot Dog?</h2>

Hot dog is a type of processed meat that is made from finely chopped or ground pork, beef, or poultry. It also contains other ingredients such as herbs, spices, and salt. Unlike sausage, hot dog is usually finely chopped or pureed, and then stuffed into a casing. The casing is then boiled, steamed, or grilled to give it flavor and texture.
