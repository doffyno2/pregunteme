<h2>What Does XL Mean?</h2>

XL stands for extra-large and is a size typically used for men’s and women’s clothing. The size is designed to fit people who are taller and wider than the average person. The exact measurements of XL size vary depending on the manufacturer, but generally, it is a size that is two sizes up from the standard size. For example, if you normally wear a medium size, an XL size would be two sizes bigger. 

<h2>How to Measure for XL Size?</h2>

When measuring for XL size clothing, it is important to take accurate measurements of your body. To get the most accurate measurements, it is best to measure with a flexible tape measure around your chest, waist, and hips. If you are buying a shirt or blouse, measure your chest and waist size. If you are buying pants or shorts, measure your waist and hips. Once you have the measurements, you can compare them to the size chart offered by the manufacturer.
