<h2>What Causes Pizza to Make You Throw Up?</h2>

<p>People may experience nausea or vomiting after eating pizza for several different reasons. First, pizza is often very greasy, and this can cause a person to feel queasy. Additionally, pizza can be very high in fat, which can also lead to an upset stomach.</p>

<p>Finally, some people may be sensitive to gluten, which is a protein found in wheat. Any food containing gluten can cause symptoms of nausea, vomiting, and/or abdominal pain in those who have a gluten sensitivity.</p>
