<h2>What is Boot Camp?</h2>

Boot camp is the period of basic military training that prepares recruits for active duty. It is the first step in a military career and it can be a difficult and stressful process. During this time, recruits are taught the basics of military life, such as weapons handling, drill, and physical training. Boot camp also includes instruction in military ethics, values, and customs.
