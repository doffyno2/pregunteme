<p>If you're a competitive cyclist, then you're probably familiar with the question: What's a good 20K bike time? <p>The answer depends on a number of factors, including age, gender, and experience level. Generally speaking, a good 20K bike time for a male cyclist in his 20s is around 32 minutes. <p>So, if you're looking to set a good time on the 20K, then you'll need to train hard and make sure that you're in peak physical condition.
