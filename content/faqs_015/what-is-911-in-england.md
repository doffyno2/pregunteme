<h2>What Is the Emergency Services Phone Number in England?</h2>

The emergency services phone number in England is 999. This number is used to contact the police, fire service, and ambulance service. It is a free call and should be used in any emergency situation. If you are not sure whether the situation is an emergency or not, it is best to err on the side of caution and dial 999.

<h2>When Should You Call 999?</h2>

The 999 number should be called in any emergency situation where there is a risk of injury or death. This includes any medical emergency, a crime in progress, a fire, or a life-threatening situation. The ambulance service will also take non-emergency calls, so if you are not sure whether it is an emergency or not, it is best to dial 999.

<h2>What Information Will You Need to Give When You Call 999?</h2>

When you call 999, you will be asked to give the following information:

<ul>
  <li>Your name and address</li>
  <li>The nature of the emergency</li>
  <li>Details of any injuries</li>
  <li>Details of any medical conditions</li> 
  <li>Information about the location of the emergency</li>
</ul>

It is important that you remain on the line and answer any questions the operator may have. This will help them to send the appropriate help to the scene.

<h2>What Happens After You Call 999?</h2>

After you call 999, the operator will assess the emergency and send the appropriate help to the scene. If the situation is potentially life-threatening, the operator may send an ambulance, fire service, and police units. If the emergency is not life-threatening, the operator may only send one service.

<h2>Are There Any Alternatives to Calling 999?</h2>

In some cases, you may be able to use an alternative service instead of calling 999. If you are in an emergency situation but do not require the emergency services, you can contact the non-emergency police number on 101. If you are experiencing a medical emergency but do not require an ambulance, you can contact the NHS 111 service.

The main difference between the emergency services phone numbers 999 and 911 is that 999 is used in the United Kingdom, while 911 is used in the United States. Other than this, both numbers are used for the same purpose: to contact the emergency services in an emergency situation.
