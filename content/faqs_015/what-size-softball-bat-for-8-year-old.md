Most bats come in various weights, and the weight of the bat should be the first thing you consider when purchasing for an 8 year old. The weight of the bat should be proportional to the player’s strength and size. Generally, bats that are too heavy can be difficult for a young person to swing, while a bat that is too light can cause the player to lose control. Look for bats that weigh between 18 and 22 ounces, depending on the player’s size and strength.
