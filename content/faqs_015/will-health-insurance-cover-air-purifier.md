<h2>What Is an Air Purifier?</h2>

An air purifier is a device that removes pollutants from the air, including dust, pollen, mold, and other airborne particles. Air purifiers are especially beneficial for people with allergies or asthma, as they can help reduce symptoms by removing irritants from the air. Air purifiers can also help reduce the risk of other respiratory illnesses, such as bronchitis and pneumonia.

<h2>Does Health Insurance Cover Air Purifiers?</h2>

No, health insurance does not cover the cost of an air purifier. Air purifiers are considered a home appliance, and most health insurance plans do not cover home appliances. Additionally, air purifiers are not considered a medical device, so health insurance does not cover the cost of a purifier either.
