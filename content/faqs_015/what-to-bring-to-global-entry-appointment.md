<p>Global Entry is a program that allows pre-approved, low-risk travelers to enter the United States quickly and easily. As part of the application process, applicants must attend an in-person interview at a Global Entry Enrollment Center. It is important for applicants to be well-prepared for the interview process, including bringing the necessary documents and materials. The following is a guide to what to bring to a Global Entry appointment.</p>

<h2>Documents</h2>

<p>The most important items to bring to the Global Entry interview are personal documents. As part of the application process, applicants must bring valid photo identification, such as a passport or driver's license. If applicable, applicants should also bring proof of residence, such as a utility bill, bank statement, or rental lease agreement. Applicants should also bring a copy of their completed Global Entry application.</p>

<h2>Payment</h2>

<p>There is a fee associated with the Global Entry program, and applicants must bring payment to the appointment. The fee is $100 and is non-refundable. Acceptable forms of payment include credit cards, debit cards, and money orders. Cash is not accepted.</p>

<h2>Other Materials</h2>

<p>In addition to documents and payment, applicants should bring a pen or pencil to the appointment. This will be needed to fill out paperwork and answer questions. Applicants should also bring an extra copy of their passport and any other important documents in case the originals are lost or stolen.</p>

<h2>Tips for Global Entry Appointments</h2>

<p>To ensure the Global Entry appointment goes as smoothly as possible, applicants should arrive at least 15 minutes early. This will give the applicant time to fill out paperwork and answer any questions the interviewer may have. Applicants should also dress professionally and be prepared to answer questions about their travels, employment, and residence. It is important to be honest and accurate when answering questions. Finally, applicants should be prepared to wait, as the appointment may take several hours.</p>
