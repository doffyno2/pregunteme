<h2>What Is a Travel Trailer?</h2>
A travel trailer is a type of recreational vehicle that can be pulled behind a vehicle, such as a car or truck. Travel trailers come in a variety of sizes, from small pop-up campers that can be towed behind a compact car to large fifth wheels that require the use of a pickup truck or SUV. Travel trailers are a great way to enjoy the outdoors without having to stay in a tent or cabin.
