<p>Water is essential for all dogs, no matter their size. It helps to regulate the body's temperature, keep the cells hydrated, and flush out toxins and waste products. If a small dog is not getting enough water, they may become lethargic, weak, and even suffer from health problems such as kidney disease.</p>

<p>Small dogs are also more prone to heatstroke, as they have less fur to help regulate their body temperature. Access to cold water can help to cool them down and keep them from becoming ill.
