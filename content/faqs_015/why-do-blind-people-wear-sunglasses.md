<h2>Why Do Blind People Wear Sunglasses?</h2>

There are a few reasons why blind people may choose to wear sunglasses. The most common reason is to protect their eyes from the sun. Blind people often experience more sensitivity to light than sighted people, so sunglasses can help reduce glare and protect their eyes from the damaging effects of the sun's UV rays.
