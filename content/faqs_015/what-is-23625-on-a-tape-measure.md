A tape measure is a measuring device used to measure length, width, and other dimensions. It consists of a flexible metal or fiberglass tape that can be pulled out and retracted. The tape measure is marked in increments of either inches or centimeters, depending on the type of tape measure. The markings are printed on the tape in both directions, so that measurements can be taken in either direction.
