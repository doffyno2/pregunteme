Sweaters and jackets are both popular clothing items and can be found in many different styles and materials. However, there are some key differences between the two garments that will help you decide which one is right for you.

<h2>Sweaters vs. Jackets: Key Differences</h2>

The main difference between a sweater and a jacket is in their purpose and design. A sweater is typically made of knitted fabric and is meant to provide warmth and comfort. Sweaters are usually lightweight and come in a variety of styles, such as cardigans, pullovers, and vests. On the other hand, a jacket is typically made of thicker and more durable fabric and is meant to protect you from the elements. Jackets are usually heavier than sweaters and come in a variety of styles, such as windbreakers, bombers, and parkas.
