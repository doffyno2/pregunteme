<h2>What Is Spirit Airlines?</h2>

Spirit Airlines is a low-cost airline based in the United States. It operates flights to more than 60 destinations in the United States, the Caribbean, Central America, and South America. The airline is known for its low fares and its no-frills service. A key aspect of Spirit's strategy is to release flights at the last minute, allowing customers to purchase tickets at the lowest prices possible.

<h2>When Does Spirit Release Flights?</h2>

Spirit Airlines releases its flight schedules up to 11 months in advance. Generally, the airline releases flights for the next month on the first business day of the current month. For example, Spirit would release its flight schedules for May on the first business day of April. 

In addition to its regular schedule, Spirit also releases a limited number of discounted flights on a weekly basis. These discounted flights are available on Mondays and Thursdays, and they must be purchased within 24 hours of their release.
