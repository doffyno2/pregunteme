<p>The exact cause of megaesophagus is not known, but it is believed to be related to a variety of conditions, including congenital defects, viral infections, and autoimmune diseases. <p>If your dog is showing signs of megaesophagus, your veterinarian will likely perform a physical examination and laboratory tests to determine the cause of the condition. <h2>How is Megaesophagus Treated?</h2>

<p>Treatment for megaesophagus is based on the severity of the condition and the underlying cause.
