<h2>What is Calorie Burning?</h2>

<p>Calorie burning is the process of using up energy stored in the body to produce metabolic activities. This type of activity can be divided into three categories: basal metabolic rate (BMR), thermogenesis, and physical activity. BMR is the energy used for basic body functions such as breathing, digesting food, and maintaining body temperature. Thermogenesis is the energy used for activities such as shivering and sweating, which help to regulate body temperature. Physical activity is the energy used for activities such as walking, running, and lifting weights. All of these activities burn calories, but the amount of calories burned varies based on the activity.</p>

Additionally, you can increase the duration of your workouts. This will help you burn more calories over a longer period of time.</p>
