<h2>Remy's Origins</h2>

Remy was born to a colony of rats in Paris, France. He was born with an unusually well-developed sense of taste and smell, which set him apart from the other rats. Despite his heightened senses, Remy was still considered an outcast among his colony due to his desire to cook, something that is not accepted among rats.
