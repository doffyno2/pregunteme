<p>When traveling with nail polish, the most important thing to keep in mind is to pack them securely. <p>You can also wrap each bottle in a piece of bubble wrap to further protect them from any damage. This will help cushion any shocks that your nail polish might experience during transit and help keep it from breaking.</p>
