<h2>Types of Lotion Available for Dogs</h2>

When it comes to lotion specifically designed for dogs, there are two main types available: natural and chemical-based. Natural lotions are made with natural ingredients such as aloe vera, shea butter, and coconut oil. Chemical-based lotions, on the other hand, contain synthetic ingredients such as parabens and sulfates. While both types of lotion can be used on dogs, natural lotions are generally considered safer and more beneficial.

<h2>Potential Risks of Using Human Lotion on Dogs</h2>

Using human lotion on dogs can be dangerous and can even be fatal in some cases. Human lotion can be too harsh for a dog’s delicate skin, especially if the lotion contains fragrances, parabens, or other harsh chemicals. Additionally, human lotion may contain ingredients that can cause an allergic reaction in some dogs, such as dyes, fragrances, or preservatives.
