<h2>What is an Autoflower?</h2>
Autoflowering cannabis is a type of cannabis strain that has been bred to flower automatically, regardless of light cycles. This makes them ideal for beginner growers, as they don’t require any light manipulation to flower. Autoflowers are usually smaller and more compact than regular cannabis strains, and they tend to produce smaller yields, but they are still capable of producing high-quality buds.

<h2>What is a Light Cycle?</h2>
