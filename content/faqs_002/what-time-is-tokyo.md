<h2>What Time Zone is Tokyo In?</h2>

Tokyo is located in the Japan Standard Time (JST) zone, which is 9 hours ahead of Coordinated Universal Time (UTC). This means that when it is noon in Tokyo, it is 3am in UTC.

<h2>What is the Time Difference Between Tokyo and Other Major Cities?</h2>

Tokyo is nine hours ahead of New York City, twelve hours ahead of Los Angeles, and five hours ahead of London. It is seven hours ahead of Moscow, four hours ahead of Abu Dhabi, and two hours ahead of Delhi.

<h2>Does Tokyo Observe Daylight Savings Time?</h2>

No, Tokyo does not observe Daylight Savings Time. The Japan Standard Time zone does not change for Daylight Savings Time, so Tokyo remains nine hours ahead of UTC all year round.
