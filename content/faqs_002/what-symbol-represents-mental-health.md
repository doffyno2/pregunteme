<h2>What is Mental Health?</h2>

Mental health refers to a person’s psychological and emotional well-being. It is how we think, feel, and behave in response to life’s situations. Mental health can be affected by both positive and negative life experiences, and it is important to recognize the importance of mental health and take steps to maintain it.

<h2>The Mental Health Symbol</h2>

The most widely recognized mental health symbol is the green ribbon. This green ribbon was first used in the 1990s to raise awareness about mental health issues. It was officially adopted as a symbol of mental health by the National Alliance on Mental Illness (NAMI) in 1999. The green ribbon is a powerful symbol that serves as a reminder of the importance of mental health, and is a sign of support for those dealing with mental health issues.
