<p>We all use navigation apps like Google Maps, Apple Maps, and Waze to get from point A to point B. While these apps are incredibly helpful, they can also be a battery drain and can slow down your device if they’re running in the background. In this article, we’ll show you how to stop Waze from running in the background so you can save battery life and keep your device running smoothly.</p>

<h2>What is Waze?</h2>

<p>Waze is a navigation app that provides real-time traffic and road information. It’s available for both iPhone and Android devices and can be used to navigate to a specific address or to find nearby points of interest like restaurants, gas stations, and more. The app uses GPS data from other users to provide up-to-date traffic information. It also provides in-app alerts for accidents, road closures, and speed cameras.</p>

<h2>How to Stop Waze from Running in the Background on Android</h2>

<p>If you’re an Android user and you want to stop Waze from running in the background, the first step is to open the app and tap the Menu icon in the top left corner. From the menu, select Settings, then App Settings, then Advanced. Here you’ll find the option to stop Waze from running in the background. Just toggle the switch to the Off position.</p>

<h2>How to Stop Waze from Running in the Background on iPhone</h2>

<p>If you’re an iPhone user, it’s a bit more complicated to stop Waze from running in the background. The first step is to open the app and tap the Menu icon in the top left corner. Then, select Settings, then App Settings, then Advanced. Here you’ll find the option to stop Waze from running in the background. Just toggle the switch to the Off position.</p>

<p>Next, you’ll need to open the Settings app on your iPhone and select General. Scroll down and select Background App Refresh. Here, you’ll see a list of all the apps installed on your device that can run in the background. Find Waze and toggle the switch to the Off position.</p>
