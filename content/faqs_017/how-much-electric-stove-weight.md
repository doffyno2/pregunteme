Electric stoves are an increasingly popular source of heat in many homes and businesses. They offer an efficient, safe, and eco-friendly way to cook food and provide warmth. But how much does an electric stove weigh?

<h2>Factors That Affect Electric Stove Weight</h2>

The weight of an electric stove can vary depending on several factors. The size of the stove, the material it's made from, and the type of features it has all play a role in the overall weight.

Additionally, stoves with features like a convection oven, warming drawer, and self-cleaning cycle will be heavier than those without these features.
