<h2>Marine Boot Camp Length</h2>

<p>Marine boot camp is the first step in becoming a Marine. It is a 13-week program that is designed to prepare recruits for service in the USMC. During boot camp, recruits will learn the basics of the Marine Corps, such as drill and ceremony, military history, physical fitness, and marksmanship. They will also learn more advanced skills, such as rappelling and weapons training. Upon completion of boot camp, recruits will receive their first military rank.</p>

<h2>MOS School Length</h2>

<p>After completing boot camp, Marines will attend MOS school, which is a specialized training program that focuses on their specific Military Occupational Specialty. MOS school is anywhere from 4 to 14 weeks in length, depending on the MOS. It is designed to teach Marines the skills and knowledge necessary to perform their duties in their chosen MOS.</p>
