<h2>What Does A Credit Repair Agent Do?</h2>

<p>A credit repair agent is responsible for helping consumers to improve their credit scores. This may involve working with credit bureaus, lenders, and debt collectors to remove errors from a credit report, negotiating with creditors to remove negative items, or helping consumers to establish a payment plan that works for them. Credit repair agents must be knowledgeable about the laws and regulations regarding credit and debt, and be able to advise consumers on how to best manage their finances.</p>
