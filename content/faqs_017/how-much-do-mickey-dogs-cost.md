<h2>Adoption Fees for Mickey Dogs</h2>

The first cost to consider when adopting a Mickey dog is the adoption fee. Most adoption fees vary depending on the breeder and the age of the dog. Generally, adopters can expect to pay anywhere from $500 to $1,500 for a Mickey dog. This fee often includes spay/neuter services, vaccinations, microchipping, and sometimes even a starter kit with food and toys. 

<h2>Food and Supplies</h2>

Like any pet, Mickey dogs require food and supplies. The type and amount of food will depend on the age and size of the dog. Generally, expect to spend around $50 to $100 per month on food. In addition to food, you'll need to purchase supplies such as a collar and leash, toys, a crate, and a bed. Depending on the quality of the items, you can expect to spend anywhere from $50 to $200 for supplies. 

<h2>Vet Bills</h2>

Vet bills are an important factor to consider when owning any pet. With Mickey dogs, vet bills can vary depending on the age and health of the dog. Generally, expect to spend between $100 and $300 per year on routine checkups, vaccinations, and other necessary care. In addition, unexpected medical bills can arise, so it's important to be prepared for the possibility. 

<h2>Ongoing Care</h2>

In addition to the initial costs of owning a Mickey dog, there are ongoing costs to consider. These include regular grooming, monthly flea and tick treatments, and the cost of training. Grooming can cost anywhere from $50 to $100 per month, depending on the type of groom. Flea and tick treatments can cost around $30 to $50 each month. Training is highly recommended for any pet, and prices vary depending on the type of training you choose. 

<h2>The Total Cost</h2>

When all these costs are added up, the total cost of owning a Mickey dog can range from $1,000 to $3,000 per year. This includes the adoption fee, food, supplies, vet bills, grooming, flea and tick treatments, and training. It's important to take these costs into consideration before making the decision to adopt a Mickey dog.
