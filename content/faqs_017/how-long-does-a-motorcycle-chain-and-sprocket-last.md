<h2>Types of Motorcycle Chain and Sprocket</h2>

<p>There are two main types of motorcycle chain and sprocket: O-ring and X-ring. O-ring chains are the most common type, and they are more economical than X-ring chains. X-ring chains are made up of a series of interlocking metal plates and X-rings, which provide better lubrication and protection from dirt and moisture than O-ring chains.
