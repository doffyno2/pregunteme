<h2>What is Powder Coating?</h2>

<p>Powder coating is a process that involves using electrostatically charged powder particles to create a protective coating on metal surfaces. The powder particles are applied to the metal surface and then cured with heat to form a hard, durable finish. Powder coating is often used on wheels because it is much more durable and resistant to chipping and scratching than traditional paint. It is also much more resistant to UV damage, so the finish will last for years.</p>
