So, how much alcohol can kill a dog? The answer to this question depends on the size of the dog and the type of alcohol consumed.

<h2>What Are the Symptoms of Alcohol Poisoning in Dogs?</h2>

If your dog has consumed alcohol, it is important to be aware of the symptoms of alcohol poisoning. Some of the most common signs of alcohol poisoning in dogs include: 

- Vomiting
- Diarrhea
- Lack of coordination
- Depression
- Seizures
- Coma

If your dog displays any of these symptoms, it is important to seek veterinary care immediately.

Yes, dogs can get drunk, although it is not recommended. Just like humans, dogs can become intoxicated if they have consumed too much alcohol. Signs of a drunk dog include stumbling, vomiting, and lethargy.
