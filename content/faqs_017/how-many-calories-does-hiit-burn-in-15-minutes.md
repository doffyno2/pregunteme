<h2>What is HIIT?</h2>

HIIT is a type of exercise that involves short bursts of intense activity followed by periods of rest or active recovery. HIIT workouts are designed to be completed in a short amount of time, usually 20 minutes or less, and can be done anywhere with minimal equipment. HIIT workouts are often described as “maximal” or “all-out” efforts.

<h2>What Are the Benefits of HIIT?</h2>

HIIT has several benefits that make it an attractive form of exercise. HIIT workouts are effective at burning calories and improving overall fitness. HIIT also offers time efficiency, as a HIIT workout can be completed in a fraction of the time of a traditional workout. Additionally, HIIT has been shown to improve endurance and strength, as well as promote fat loss.

<h2>How Many Calories Does HIIT Burn in 15 Minutes?</h2>
