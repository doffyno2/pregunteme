<h2>What Equipment is Needed to Outfit a Fishing Trawler?</h2>

<p>Before a trawler is ready to go fishing, it must be outfitted with the necessary equipment. This includes things like fishing nets, trawl doors, catch handling systems, fish processing equipment, navigation systems, communication systems, and safety equipment. The complexity and cost of the equipment required will depend on the size and type of trawler, how long it will be out at sea, and the type of fishing it will be used for.</p>

<h2>Leasing vs Purchasing Fishing Trawler Equipment</h2>

<p>When outfitting a fishing trawler, the crew must decide whether to purchase or lease the equipment. Purchasing the equipment can be expensive up front, but it can save money in the long run as there are no ongoing lease payments. Leasing the equipment, on the other hand, can be more cost effective in the short-term, but it requires ongoing payments and may not be the most cost-effective option in the long-term.</p>
