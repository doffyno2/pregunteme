<h2>What Is Fenugreek?</h2>

Fenugreek is an herb that belongs to the family Fabaceae. It is native to the Mediterranean region, but it is now widely cultivated in many parts of the world. Fenugreek is used as a spice in many dishes and as a medicinal herb. It contains several vitamins and minerals, as well as dietary fiber.

<h2>What Are the Benefits of Fenugreek for Dogs?</h2>

Fenugreek has many potential benefits for dogs. It is believed to help with digestion, as well as provide anti-inflammatory and antioxidant properties. It may also help to reduce cholesterol, improve skin and coat health, and boost the immune system.

<h2>How Much Fenugreek for Dogs?</h2>
