<h2>What Are the Duties of a Car Sales Rep?</h2>

Before we discuss how much a car sales rep makes, let’s first take a look at the duties of a car sales rep. Car sales reps are responsible for helping customers find the right car for their needs and budget. This involves working with customers to identify their needs and wants, as well as offering advice on the best car to meet their needs. 

Car sales reps also help customers with financing and insurance. They must be able to explain the various financing options available, as well as the benefits and drawbacks of each. In addition, car sales reps must be knowledgeable about the various insurance options available and how they can benefit customers. 

Finally, car sales reps must be able to close a sale. This involves negotiating with customers, offering discounts and other incentives, and being persuasive to make sure that customers make a purchase.
