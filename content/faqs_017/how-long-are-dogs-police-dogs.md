<p>A police dog, also known as a K9, is a dog that has been specifically trained to assist police officers in their duties. These dogs are highly skilled and have a special bond with their handlers. <p>Police dogs receive extensive training in order to become certified law enforcement officers. This training includes obedience training, tracking, drug detection, and protection training.
