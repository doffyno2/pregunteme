<h2>What Are Sturgeon Fish?</h2>

<p>Sturgeon fish are a family of fish species that are found in both fresh and saltwater habitats. These fish are characterized by their elongated bodies and pointed snouts. Sturgeon fish are not aggressive and they are relatively docile creatures, making them a popular choice for aquariums and pond systems.</p>

<p>Sturgeon fish are native to many parts of the world, including North America, Europe, and Asia.
