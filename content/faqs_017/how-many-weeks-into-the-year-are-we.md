<h2>What Is a Year?</h2>

A year is the time it takes for the Earth to make a full revolution around the Sun. This is typically 365 days, or 8,760 hours. This time is divided up into 12 months, 52 weeks, and 365 days. The months are further divided up into four weeks, with 7 days in each week. 

<h2>What Is a Week?</h2>

A week is a unit of time that is typically divided into seven days. It is used to measure the passage of time and to organize activities within a given period. The seven days of the week are Sunday, Monday, Tuesday, Wednesday, Thursday, Friday, and Saturday. Each day is 24 hours long, and each week is 168 hours long.
