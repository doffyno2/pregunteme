<h2>What Is Protein?</h2>

Protein is an important macronutrient that plays a major role in your body's growth, development, and overall health. It's made up of amino acids, which make up the building blocks of your body's cells and tissues. Protein can be found in many different foods, including meats, dairy products, nuts, and legumes. 

<h2>How Much Protein Is in 6 Eggs?</h2>

Six eggs contain a total of about 36 grams of protein. That’s about 6.5 grams of protein per egg. The exact amount can vary slightly depending on the size and type of egg.
