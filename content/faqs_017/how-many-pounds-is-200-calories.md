<h2>What Is a Calorie?</h2>

A calorie is a unit of energy. It is the amount of energy that a body needs to perform a certain task or activity. Calories are used to measure the energy content of food. Different types of food contain different amounts of calories.

<h2>How Many Calories Should I Eat Per Day?</h2>

The average woman should consume about 2,000 calories per day to maintain her weight, while the average man should consume about 2,500 calories per day. If you are trying to lose weight, you should aim to consume fewer calories than you burn each day.
