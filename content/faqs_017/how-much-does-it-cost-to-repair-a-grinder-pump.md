<h2>What is a Grinder Pump?</h2>

<p>A grinder pump is a type of pump that is used to grind up wastewater and send it to the sewer lines. The pump is connected to the plumbing system, and when wastewater enters the pump, it is ground up into smaller pieces, which makes it easier to pass through the sewer pipes. Grinder pumps are typically used in homes that are not connected to a public sewer system, as they are able to handle large volumes of wastewater.</p>
