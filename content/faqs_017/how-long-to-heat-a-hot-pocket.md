<p>Hot Pockets are a tasty snack that can be enjoyed at any time of the day. They are made from a combination of frozen dough and a variety of fillings. While they can be eaten cold, they’re best enjoyed when heated up. In this article, we’ll discuss how to heat a hot pocket so that you can enjoy it at its best.</p>

<h2>What You Will Need</h2>

<p>In order to heat a Hot Pocket, you will need an oven or a microwave. You will also need a flat surface, such as a baking sheet, on which to place the Hot Pocket. Finally, you will need oven mitts or kitchen towels to handle the hot pocket once it is heated.</p>

<h2>Heating In An Oven</h2>

<p>If you are using an oven to heat your Hot Pocket, you will need to preheat the oven to 425 degrees Fahrenheit. Place your Hot Pocket on a baking sheet and place it in the oven. Bake the Hot Pocket for 17 to 20 minutes, until it is heated through. Check the Hot Pocket periodically to ensure that it is not burning. Once the Hot Pocket is heated through, remove it from the oven and allow it to cool for a few minutes before handling.</p>

<h2>Heating In A Microwave</h2>

<p>If you are using a microwave to heat your Hot Pocket, you will need to place the Hot Pocket on a microwave-safe plate. Set the microwave on high power and heat the Hot Pocket for one minute. Check the Hot Pocket and if it is not yet heated through, continue to heat in 30-second intervals until it is heated through. Once the Hot Pocket is heated through, remove it from the microwave and allow it to cool for a few minutes before handling.</p>

<h2>Serving Your Hot Pocket</h2>

<p>Once your Hot Pocket is heated through, it is ready to be served. Cut the Hot Pocket in half and serve with your favorite condiments and sides. Enjoy your hot and delicious Hot Pocket!</p>
