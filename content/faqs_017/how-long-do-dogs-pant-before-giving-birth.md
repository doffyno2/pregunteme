<h2>What is Panting?</h2>

Panting is a form of respiration in which a dog takes rapid and shallow breaths. Dogs pant to regulate their body temperature, to reduce stress, or to signal excitement or fear. Panting is a normal behavior and is seen in all types of dogs.
