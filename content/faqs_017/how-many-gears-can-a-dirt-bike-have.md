<h2>What is a Gear?</h2>

<p>A gear is a toothed wheel or cog that is used in a transmission system to transfer power from one component to another. <p>Dirt bikes have a transmission system with multiple gears. When a rider shifts gears on a dirt bike, they are changing the ratio between the input and output shafts. As the ratio between the two shafts changes, the engine’s speed and power output change, allowing the rider to accelerate or decelerate quickly.</p>
