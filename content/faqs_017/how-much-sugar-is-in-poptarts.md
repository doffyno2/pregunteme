The answer to this question depends on the flavor of Pop-Tart you choose. A typical frosted Pop-Tart contains 15 grams of sugar in a single 56 gram serving. That’s equal to about 3 teaspoons of sugar per serving. The sugar content of a brown sugar cinnamon Pop-Tart is slightly higher, with 17 grams of sugar per 56 gram serving.
