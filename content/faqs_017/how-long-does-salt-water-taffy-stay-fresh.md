<p>Salt water taffy is a classic American treat that has been enjoyed by generations of sweet-toothed individuals. With its chewy texture and sweet flavor, it's no wonder that salt water taffy remains a favorite candy among candy lovers of all ages.</p>

<p>The good news is that salt water taffy can last for quite a while when stored properly. Depending on the environment in which it is stored, salt water taffy can last up to two months or longer when stored in an airtight container in a cool, dry place. </p>

<p>Salt water taffy is a combination of sugar, corn syrup, water, butter, and flavorings. The combination of these ingredients creates a sticky texture that is both chewy and sweet. The candy is then stretched and twisted into individual pieces. It is important to note that salt water taffy does not actually contain any salt, hence the name. </p>

<h2>How to Store Salt Water Taffy</h2>

<p>Salt water taffy should be stored in an airtight container in a cool, dry place. This will help keep the taffy from sticking together and help it last longer. It is also important to note that taffy should not be stored in an area that is too humid or too hot, as the sugar and butter in the taffy can cause it to melt if the temperature gets too high. </p>

<p>Once opened, the taffy should be consumed within a few days. If left in an airtight container, it should last up to two months or longer.
