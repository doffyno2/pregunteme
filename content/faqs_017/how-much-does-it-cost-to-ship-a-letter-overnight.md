<h2>What Is Overnight Letter Shipping?</h2>

<p>Overnight letter shipping is a service offered by most major courier and postal companies (e.g., FedEx, UPS, USPS). It allows you to ship your letter with guaranteed delivery the next day. In other words, it guarantees that your letter will arrive by the next day, but not necessarily that it will arrive in the morning. Depending on the time of day you ship your letter, it could arrive at its destination later in the afternoon or evening.</p>

<h2>How Much Does Overnight Letter Shipping Cost?</h2>

<p>The cost of overnight letter shipping depends on a few factors, such as the size and weight of the letter, the distance it needs to travel, and the type of delivery service you choose. For example, USPS Priority Mail Express offers overnight letter shipping for as little as $21.50 for a 1-ounce letter traveling up to 100 miles. FedEx Express Saver offers overnight shipping for as little as $20 for a 1-ounce letter traveling up to 500 miles. And UPS Next Day Air offers overnight letter shipping for as little as $30 for a 1-ounce letter traveling up to 500 miles.</p>

<h2>Tips For Keeping Your Overnight Letter Shipping Costs Low</h2>

<p>The cost of overnight letter shipping can add up quickly. To keep your costs down, here are some tips to consider:</p>

<ul>
  <li>Choose the right delivery service. Different delivery services offer different rates. Compare the rates of different services to find the best deal.</li>
  <li>Ship in bulk. If you need to ship multiple letters overnight, you may be able to get a discount by shipping them all together.</li>
  <li>Opt for ground shipping. If you don't need your letter to arrive the next day, you may be able to save money by opting for ground shipping.</li>
  <li>Negotiate. If you're a frequent shipper, you may be able to get a discount by negotiating with your delivery service.</li>
</ul>
