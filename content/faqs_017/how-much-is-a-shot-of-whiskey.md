<h2>What Is a Shot of Whiskey?</h2>

A shot of whiskey is a small measure of whiskey, usually around 1.5 ounces or 45 ml. It is equivalent to a single serving of whiskey. Some bars and restaurants may also serve a double shot of whiskey, which is two ounces or 60 ml of whiskey.

<h2>Types of Whiskey</h2>

Whiskey comes in several different varieties, including Scotch, Irish, American, Canadian, Japanese, and others. Each type of whiskey is made from a different grain, such as barley, rye, or wheat, and has its own unique flavor profile.

<h2>Prices of Whiskey</h2>

The price of a shot of whiskey can vary greatly depending on the type of whiskey, the size of the bottle, and the location in which the whiskey is purchased. Generally, a shot of whiskey can cost anywhere from $2 to $20 or more.
