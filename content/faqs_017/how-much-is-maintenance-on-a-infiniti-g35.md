<p>Infiniti G35s are luxury sports cars from Nissan's Infiniti brand, and they are known for their reliable performance and sporty handling. <h2>Oil Changes</h2>

<p>Oil changes are one of the most important parts of regular maintenance for any vehicle, and the Infiniti G35 is no exception. The recommended oil change interval for the G35 is every 7,500 miles, or every six months. The cost of an oil change for a G35 will vary depending on the type of oil used and the labor costs of the mechanic performing the service. Generally speaking, an oil change for a G35 will cost between $50 and $100.</p>

<h2>Fluid Changes</h2>

<p>In addition to regular oil changes, the Infiniti G35 also requires regular fluid changes. This includes engine coolant, transmission fluid, brake fluid, and power steering fluid. Depending on the type of fluid used and the labor costs of the mechanic, these fluid changes can range from $50 to $200.</p>

<h2>Tune-ups</h2>

<p>Tune-ups are a more comprehensive form of maintenance that is recommended for the Infiniti G35 every 30,000 miles. Depending on the type of parts used and the labor costs of the mechanic, a tune-up for a G35 can cost between $200 and $400.</p>
