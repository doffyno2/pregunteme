<h2>Weight of Standard Plates</h2>

<p>The standard plate weight for most gyms is 45 pounds. This is the most common size and is used for most machines and free weights at the gym. This is the weight of the plate itself, not including the bar, which adds additional weight. The weight of the plate depends on the manufacturer, so it's always important to check the label on the plate before you start your workout.</p>

<h2>Olympic Plates</h2>

<p>Olympic plates are larger than standard plates and are used for Olympic lifts. These plates are much heavier than standard plates and typically weigh between 25 and 55 pounds. The weight of the plate depends on the manufacturer and the size of the plate. Some Olympic plates can be as light as 25 pounds or as heavy as 55 pounds, so it's important to check the label before you pick one up.</p>

<h2>Bumper Plates</h2>

<p>Bumper plates are the lightest type of weight plates found at the gym. They are the same size as Olympic plates but are much lighter. Bumper plates are typically used for Olympic lifts and are made of rubber. They range in weight from 10 to 25 pounds, with the heavier plates being thicker than the lighter plates. It's important to check the label before you pick up a bumper plate to make sure it is the correct weight for your workout.</p>

<h2>Calibrated Plates</h2>

<p>Calibrated plates are the most accurate type of weight plates. These plates are usually made of steel or aluminum and are calibrated to a very precise weight. These plates are usually very expensive and are used for Olympic lifts and powerlifting. The weight of calibrated plates can range from 5 to 55 pounds, with the heavier plates being thicker than the lighter plates. It's important to check the label before you pick up a calibrated plate to make sure it is the correct weight for your workout.</p>

<h2>Adjustable Plates</h2>

<p>Adjustable plates are the most versatile type of weight plates. These plates are usually made of steel or aluminum and can be adjusted to different weights. Adjustable plates are usually used for strength training and can range in weight from 5 to 45 pounds. The weight of adjustable plates can be adjusted by simply adding or removing plates from the bar. It's important to check the label before you pick up an adjustable plate to make sure it is the correct weight for your workout.</p>
