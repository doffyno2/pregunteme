<h2>What Factors Affect the Cost of Dry Dog Food?</h2>

<p>Many factors play a role in determining the cost of dry dog food. The size of the dog and its age are two of the most important factors. Generally, specialty or organic dry dog food is more expensive than regular dry dog foods.</p>

<p>The average cost of dry dog food per month can vary widely depending on the factors mentioned above. For a small dog, the cost of dry dog food per month can range anywhere from $15 to $45.
