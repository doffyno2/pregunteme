<p>Squeaking pedals are usually caused by two things. The first is that the pedals may be loose and need to be tightened. <p>If the squeaking is caused by a loose pedal, then the fix is relatively simple. All you need to do is to tighten the pedal using a wrench or an Allen key. Make sure to tighten the pedal until it is snug but not too tight as this can cause damage to the pedal.</p>

<p>If the squeaking is caused by a worn out bearing, then the only solution is to replace the bearing. This is not an easy job and will require specialized tools and knowledge.
