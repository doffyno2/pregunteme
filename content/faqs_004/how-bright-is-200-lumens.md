<h2>Understanding Lumens</h2>

Lumens is a standard unit of measurement used to measure the amount of light provided by a given source. A lumen is the total amount of visible light emitted by a source. Lumens measure the total amount of light, while lux measures the amount of light per area, such as how much light is hitting a square meter.
