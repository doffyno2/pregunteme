<p>Are you frustrated by the prospect of having to repair a scratch on your flat screen TV? <h2>What You Need for the Repair</h2>

<p>Before you begin the repair process, it is important to make sure you have the right materials. The first item you will need is a flat screen TV cleaner. This should be specifically designed for use on flat screens. You will also need a microfiber cloth, which can be used to apply the cleaner and help you buff away the scratch. Additionally, you may need a small brush to help you get into tight spots.</p>

<h2>Preparing the TV for Repair</h2>

<p>Once you have gathered all of the supplies you need, it is time to prepare your TV for repair. Begin by disconnecting the power source from the TV. This is important for safety, and it will also prevent any further damage to the screen. Next, use the flat screen cleaner and the microfiber cloth to thoroughly clean the screen. This will help remove any dust or dirt that may have built up on the TV and help ensure the repair process is successful.</p>

<h2>Repairing the Scratch</h2>

<p>Once the screen is clean, you can begin the repair process. Start by gently rubbing the scratch with a microfiber cloth. This will help remove any excess dirt that may have become lodged in the scratch. If the scratch is still visible, you can try using a small brush to brush away any remaining dirt. Once the scratch is clean, you can use a flat screen repair kit to help fill the scratch and restore the finish of the TV.</p>

<h2>Finishing the Repair</h2>

<p>Once you have finished the repair, it is important to finish the job by buffing the area. Use a microfiber cloth to gently buff the area and restore the finish of the TV. This should help the scratch blend in with the rest of the screen and make it look as good as new. </p>
