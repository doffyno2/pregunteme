<h2>What is Benadryl?</h2>

Benadryl, also known as diphenhydramine, is an antihistamine used to treat allergies in both humans and dogs. <h2>What is Trazodone?</h2>

Trazodone is an antidepressant used to treat depression and anxiety in humans and dogs.
