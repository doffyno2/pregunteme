<h2>What Is a Neti Pot?</h2>

A neti pot is a small container that is used to irrigate the nasal passages. The pot is filled with water, and a saline solution is added to the water. The pot is then tilted to one side, and the solution is poured into one nostril, allowing it to flow through the nasal passages, and then out of the other nostril. This method of irrigation can help to clear away built-up mucus, allergens, and irritants, and can be used to treat sinus infections, allergies, colds, and other conditions.

<h2>What Is the Best Water to Use in a Neti Pot?</h2>

The best water to use in a neti pot is typically sterile or distilled water. These types of water do not contain any minerals or other contaminants that could lead to irritation or infection. Tap water can also be used in a neti pot, but it should be filtered first to remove any potential contaminants, including chlorine and heavy metals.
