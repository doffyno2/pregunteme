<h2>What Is an Electric Cattle Fence?</h2>

An electric cattle fence is a type of fence that uses an electric current to repel animals from a designated area. The electric current is delivered through a wire that is attached to posts, and the current can be adjusted to either a low or high level. The current is designed to provide a deterrent for animals, without causing them injury.
