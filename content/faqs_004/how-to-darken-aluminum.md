<p>Aluminum is a popular metal used in a variety of applications, ranging from aerospace to automotive. It is lightweight, strong and durable, making it an ideal material for many projects. <p>Darkening aluminum is relatively easy and can be done using various methods. The most common way to darken aluminum is through anodizing. The oxidation process not only darkens the aluminum but also makes it more resistant to corrosion and wear.</p>
