<p>The Sonic Chicago Dog is a delicious and classic Chicago-style hotdog. It is a juicy, flavorful, and savory treat that can be made at home. This hotdog is a great way to add some zest to your next cookout or party. In this article, we will discuss the ingredients, method, and tips that you need to know to make this classic hotdog. </p>

<h2>Ingredients</h2>

<p>The following ingredients are needed to make a Sonic Chicago Dog:</p>

<ul>
  <li>Hotdog buns</li>
  <li>All-beef hotdogs</li>
  <li>Yellow mustard</li>
  <li>Pickle relish</li>
  <li>Chopped onion</li>
  <li>Tomato wedges</li>
  <li>Celery salt</li>
</ul>

<h2>Method</h2>

<p>Making a Sonic Chicago Dog is easy. Here are the steps:</p>

<ol>
  <li>Start by preparing the hotdog buns. Toast them lightly in a toaster oven or on a skillet. </li>
  <li>Grill or boil the all-beef hotdogs until they are cooked through. Place the cooked hotdogs in the toasted buns. </li>
  <li>Top the cooked hotdogs with yellow mustard, pickle relish, chopped onion, tomato wedges, and celery salt. </li>
  <li>Serve the Sonic Chicago Dogs immediately. Enjoy!</li>
</ol>

<h2>Tips</h2>

<p>Here are some tips for making a Sonic Chicago Dog:</p>

<ul>
  <li>Make sure to use all-beef hotdogs for the best flavor and texture. </li>
  <li>For a spicy kick, add a few dashes of hot sauce to the cooked hotdog. </li>
  <li>Toast the hotdog buns lightly for the best texture. </li>
  <li>Add a few dashes of celery salt for a classic Chicago-style flavor. </li>
  <li>Serve the hotdogs with your favorite sides such as chips, fries, or onion rings. </li>
</ul>
