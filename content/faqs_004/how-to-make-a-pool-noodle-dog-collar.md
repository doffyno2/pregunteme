<h2>Materials Needed</h2>
<p>Before you get started, you’ll need to gather the necessary materials. For this project, you’ll need the following items:</p>
<ul>
  <li>Pool noodle</li>
  <li>Metallic paint</li>
  <li>Paintbrush</li>
  <li>Measuring tape</li>
  <li>Scissors</li>
  <li>Ribbon or fabric</li>
  <li>Glue gun</li>
</ul>

<h2>Step 1: Measure Your Dog’s Neck</h2>
<p>The first step in making a pool noodle dog collar is to measure your pup’s neck. To do this, you’ll need to measure around the widest part of your pup’s neck. Make sure to measure using a measuring tape and record the measurement in inches. </p>

<h2>Step 2: Cut the Pool Noodle</h2>
<p>Once you’ve measured your pup’s neck, you can begin cutting the pool noodle. To do this, you’ll need to measure the length of the pool noodle and mark it with a pencil. Then, use scissors to cut the pool noodle at the marked line. </p>

<h2>Step 3: Paint the Pool Noodle</h2>
<p>The next step is to paint the pool noodle. To do this, you’ll need to apply a coat of metallic paint to the pool noodle. Make sure to paint both sides of the pool noodle evenly. Allow the paint to dry before moving on to the next step. </p>

<h2>Step 4: Attach the Ribbon</h2>
<p>Once the paint is dry, you can attach the ribbon or fabric to the pool noodle. To do this, you’ll need to use a hot glue gun to attach the ribbon to either side of the pool noodle. Make sure to use enough glue to ensure that the ribbon is securely attached. </p>

<h2>Step 5: Attach the Clasp</h2>
<p>The final step is to attach the clasp to the pool noodle. To do this, you’ll need to use a hot glue gun to attach the clasp to one end of the pool noodle. Make sure to apply enough glue to ensure that the clasp is securely attached. </p>
