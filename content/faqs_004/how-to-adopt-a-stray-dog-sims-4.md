<h2>Finding a Stray Dog in The Sims 4</h2>

<p>The first step in adopting a stray dog in The Sims 4 is to find one. There are several ways to find a stray dog in The Sims 4. One way is to search the neighborhood for strays. <h2>Adopting a Stray Dog in The Sims 4</h2>

<p>Once you have found a stray dog in The Sims 4, you can adopt it. To adopt a stray dog, click on the dog and select the “Adopt” option. You will also be asked to sign a contract agreeing to take care of the dog. Once you have completed the adoption process, the dog will become a member of your household.</p>

<h2>Caring for Your Stray Dog in The Sims 4</h2>

<p>Once you have adopted a stray dog in The Sims 4, you will need to provide it with food, water, and exercise. You can purchase food and water for your dog at the local pet store. <h2>Training Your Stray Dog in The Sims 4</h2>

<p>In order to ensure that your stray dog is well behaved, you will need to train it. Training your dog in The Sims 4 is easy. All you need to do is click on the dog and select the “Train” option.
