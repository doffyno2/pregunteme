<p>Are you looking for an easy way to change the wallpaper on your Amazon Fire tablet? Changing the wallpaper on your tablet is a great way to personalize it and make it feel more like yours. Fortunately, changing the wallpaper on your Amazon Fire tablet is a relatively simple process.</p>

<h2>How to Change Wallpaper on Your Amazon Fire Tablet</h2>

<p>The first step to changing the wallpaper on your Amazon Fire tablet is to open the Settings menu. To do this, tap the gear-shaped icon on the home screen. This will bring up the Settings menu. From here, select Display & Sounds. You should see an option for Wallpaper. Select this option.</p>

<p>From here, you will be able to choose from a selection of pre-loaded wallpapers. You can also select the option to use your own photo as your wallpaper. If you choose to do this, you can either choose a photo from your device’s photo library or take a new picture with the device’s camera. Select the image you want to use as your wallpaper and then tap the Set Wallpaper button.</p>

<p>That’s it! You have now successfully changed the wallpaper on your Amazon Fire tablet. You can now enjoy your new wallpaper each time you turn on your tablet.</p>
