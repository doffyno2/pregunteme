<p>If you have a Toyota Sienna 2014, you may have noticed the maintenance light on your dashboard. This light is used to indicate when it is time to take your vehicle in for regular maintenance. It is important to reset the maintenance light after each service so that it can properly alert you to the next service interval. Here's how to reset the maintenance light on a Toyota Sienna 2014.</p>

<h2>Steps to Reset Maintenance Light Toyota Sienna 2014</h2>

<p>Resetting the maintenance light on a Toyota Sienna 2014 is a simple process that can be done in a few minutes. Follow these steps to get the job done quickly and easily:</p>

<p><b>Step 1:</b> Start the engine of your Toyota Sienna 2014 and let it run for at least 30 seconds.</p>

<p><b>Step 2:</b> Turn off the engine and leave the key in the "on" position.</p>

<p><b>Step 3:</b> Press and hold the “trip” button on the dashboard. While pressing this button, turn the key to the "on" position without turning on the engine.</p>

<p><b>Step 4:</b> Release the “trip” button and press the “reset” button. Hold the “reset” button for at least three seconds until the maintenance light turns off.</p>

<p><b>Step 5:</b> Turn off the key and remove it from the ignition. The maintenance light is now reset.</p>

<h2>Tips for Resetting the Maintenance Light Toyota Sienna 2014</h2>

<p>If the maintenance light does not turn off after following the steps outlined above, there may be an issue with the vehicle. It is recommended that you take your vehicle to a qualified mechanic for further inspection. They will be able to diagnose and repair any underlying issues.</p>

<p>You may also need to reset the maintenance light if you have recently changed the engine oil, replaced a part, or performed any other type of service. In this case, simply repeat the above steps to reset the maintenance light.</p>
