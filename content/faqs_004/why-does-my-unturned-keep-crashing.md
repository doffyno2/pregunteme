<p>Unturned is a popular survival video game that is available for free on Steam. It is a sandbox game with an open world, where you can explore, craft items, and build a base. However, many players have reported that their game keeps crashing, which can be extremely annoying. In this article, we will explore possible causes of this issue and how to fix it.</p>

<h2>What Causes Unturned to Crash?</h2>

<p>Unturned may crash for a variety of reasons. Some possible causes include:</p>

<ul>
  <li>Outdated graphics drivers</li>
  <li>Corrupted game files</li>
  <li>Incompatible mods</li>
  <li>Incompatible antivirus software</li>
  <li>Insufficient system resources</li>
</ul>

<h2>How to Fix Unturned Crashing Issue?</h2>

<p>If your Unturned game is crashing, there are a few things you can try to fix the issue. Here are some troubleshooting steps you can take:</p>

<h3>Update Your Graphics Drivers</h3>

<p>If your graphics drivers are outdated, Unturned may crash. To update your graphics drivers, you will need to download and install the latest version from your graphics card's manufacturer website.</p>

<h3>Verify Game Files</h3>

<p>If your game files are corrupted, Unturned may crash. To verify your game files, you can open Steam, right-click the game, select ‘Properties’, go to the ‘Local Files’ tab, and click ‘Verify Integrity of Game Files’.</p>

<h3>Uninstall Incompatible Mods</h3>

<p>If you have installed any mods that are incompatible with the game, Unturned may crash. To fix this issue, you will need to uninstall any incompatible mods.</p>

<h3>Disable Your Antivirus Software</h3>

<p>If your antivirus software is interfering with Unturned, the game may crash. To fix this issue, you will need to disable your antivirus software temporarily.</p>

<h3>Increase System Resources</h3>

<p>If your system does not have enough resources to run Unturned, the game may crash. To increase system resources, you can close any unnecessary applications running in the background and add more RAM to your computer.</p>
