<h2>Why Is My Pregnant Dog Not Eating?</h2>

There are a few possible reasons why your pregnant dog isn't eating. It could be due to stress, as the changes in her body can cause her to be anxious. It could also be due to the fact that her appetite has decreased due to the hormonal changes of pregnancy. It could also be due to nausea or digestive issues, which can be common during pregnancy. 

<h2>What Should I Do If My Pregnant Dog Won't Eat?</h2>

It's important to make sure that your pregnant dog gets the nutrition she needs.
