The average typing speed for adults is around 38 - 40 WPM (words per minute). This is based on several studies that have been conducted over the years. <h2>What Factors Affect Typing Speed?</h2>

There are a number of factors that can affect a person's typing speed. Age is an important factor, as younger people tend to have faster typing speeds than older people. Finally, the type of keyboard being used can also affect typing speed. Mechanical keyboards typically require less force to press keys, which can lead to faster typing speeds.

<h2>How Can Typing Speed Be Improved?</h2>

If you want to improve your typing speed, there are a few things you can do. First, practice typing regularly.
