<h2>The Four Seasons in Spanish</h2>

<p>The four seasons in Spanish are primavera (spring), verano (summer), otoño (autumn), and invierno (winter). Each season has its own unique characteristics, and they can all be used to talk about the weather, activities, and more.</p>

<h2>How to Talk About the Seasons in Spanish</h2>

<p>In order to talk about the seasons in Spanish, it is important to be familiar with the words and phrases associated with each one. Here are some useful phrases that can be used to talk about the seasons in Spanish:</p>

<h3>Primavera (Spring)</h3>

<ul>
  <li>La primavera comienza en marzo. (Spring begins in March.)</li>
  <li>Las flores comienzan a brotar en la primavera. (Flowers start to bloom in the spring.)</li>
  <li>Los días comienzan a ser más largos en la primavera. (Days start to get longer in the spring.)</li>
</ul>

<h3>Verano (Summer)</h3>

<ul>
  <li>El verano comienza en junio. (Summer begins in June.)</li>
  <li>Es un tiempo excelente para tomar el sol. (It is a great time to go out and get some sun.)</li>
  <li>Las noches son más largas en verano. (Nights are longer in the summer.)</li>
</ul>

<h3>Otoño (Autumn)</h3>

<ul>
  <li>El otoño comienza en septiembre. (Autumn begins in September.)</li>
  <li>Las hojas comienzan a caer de los árboles. (Leaves start to fall from the trees.)</li>
  <li>Los días comienzan a ser más cortos en el otoño. (Days start to get shorter in the autumn.)</li>
</ul>

<h3>Invierno (Winter)</h3>

<ul>
  <li>El invierno comienza en diciembre. (Winter begins in December.)</li>
  <li>Es un momento excelente para hacer actividades al aire libre. (It is a great time to go out and do outdoor activities.)</li>
  <li>Las noches son más largas en invierno. (Nights are longer in the winter.)</li>
</ul>

<h2>Other Useful Phrases for Talking About the Seasons in Spanish</h2>

<p>Here are some other useful phrases that can be used to talk about the seasons in Spanish:</p>

<ul>
