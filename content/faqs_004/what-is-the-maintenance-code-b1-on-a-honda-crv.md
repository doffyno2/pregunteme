<p>If you own a Honda CRV, you may have seen the maintenance code B1 and wondered what it means.  Maintenance code B1 is a general maintenance code for Honda CRVs that indicates that the vehicle needs to have its oil changed. It is the first level of maintenance for the Honda CRV and is typically done every 7,500 miles or 12 months, whichever comes first.</p>

<p>The B1 code is typically displayed by the Maintenance Minder, which is a system designed to remind you when your Honda CRV needs maintenance. This system is designed to alert you when your vehicle needs maintenance or repair, and the B1 code is a reminder to have your oil changed. It is important that you have your oil changed regularly to ensure optimal performance of your Honda CRV.</p>

<h2>How to Reset Maintenance Code B1 on a Honda CRV?</h2>

<p>Once you have had your oil changed, it is important to reset the Maintenance Minder so that it can continue to monitor your vehicle. Resetting the maintenance code B1 on a Honda CRV is a simple process that you can do in just a few steps:</p>

<ul>
	<li>Turn on the ignition with the engine off.</li>
	<li>Press the Select/Reset button on the dashboard.</li>
	<li>Scroll through the menu until you find the “Maintenance Items” option.</li>
	<li>Select the “Oil Reset” option.</li>
	<li>Confirm the reset by pressing the Select/Reset button again.</li>
</ul>

<p>You should now see a message on the dashboard that says “Maintenance Item Reset.” This means that the Maintenance Minder has been reset and it will now start to monitor your vehicle again to alert you when it needs maintenance or repair.</p>
