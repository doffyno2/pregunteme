<p>Turning on a ProForm bike is a relatively simple process. Here are the steps you need to follow:</p>

    <li><strong>Step Four:</strong> Select your desired program from the settings menu.</li>
    <li><strong>Step Five:</strong> Adjust the settings to your desired level of difficulty.</li>
</ol>

<p>Once you have followed these steps, your ProForm bike should be up and running. It is now ready for your workout.</p>
