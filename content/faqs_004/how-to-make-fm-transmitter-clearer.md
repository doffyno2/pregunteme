<h2>Check Your Antenna</h2>

<p>The most common issue with FM transmitters is the antenna. If your antenna is not positioned correctly, your signal will be weaker and your sound will be less clear. Make sure your antenna is in a good position and is not obstructed by any objects. Also, make sure your antenna is properly connected to your transmitter.</p>

<p>Another common issue with FM transmitters is that they are not tuned to the right frequency. This can cause your signal to be weak and your sound to be distorted. <p>Another issue that can cause your FM transmitter to become unclear is dirt and dust. If your transmitter is dirty, the sound can be distorted and the signal can be weakened.
