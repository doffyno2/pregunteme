<h2>What Are Food Stamps?</h2>

<p>Food stamps are vouchers issued by the government that allow people with low incomes to purchase food items at grocery stores. They’re also known as Supplemental Nutrition Assistance Program (SNAP) benefits. The program is administered by the United States Department of Agriculture (USDA). In order to qualify for food stamps, you must meet certain income requirements. </p>
