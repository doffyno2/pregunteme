<h2>Why Do Sharks Grow So Fast?</h2>

Sharks grow at an incredibly fast rate due to a combination of factors. Firstly, they are predators and feed on other animals, which gives them a high calorie diet. Secondly, they have a high metabolism which helps them to digest their food quickly. Finally, they are ectothermic, meaning they rely on the temperature of the water to regulate their body temperature. This means that they are constantly searching for warmer waters, which allows them to get more oxygen and grow faster.
