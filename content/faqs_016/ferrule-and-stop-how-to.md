<h2>Types of Ferrules</h2>

<p>There are several different types of ferrules available on the market today. Two of the most common types are the compression ferrule and the flare ferrule. Compression ferrules are typically used for plastic piping systems and are designed to provide an airtight seal. They work by compressing the pipe or tube when it is tightened. Flare ferrules are typically used in metal piping systems and are designed to provide a secure fit. They work by expanding the pipe or tube when it is tightened.</p>
