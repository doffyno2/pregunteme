<h2>What Are Mung Beans?</h2>

Mung beans are small, green-colored legumes that have a sweet and nutty flavor. They are native to India and have been cultivated for thousands of years. They are a rich source of nutrients and contain high amounts of protein, fiber, folate, and vitamin B6. They are also a good source of minerals such as iron, magnesium, and calcium.
