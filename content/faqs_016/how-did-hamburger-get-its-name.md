<h2>What is a Hamburger?</h2>

<p>A hamburger is a sandwich made with a ground beef patty, typically served between two slices of bread. The patty can be grilled, fried, or baked, and is often accompanied by condiments such as ketchup, mustard, pickles, lettuce, and onion. The hamburger is typically served hot, but can also be served cold.</p>
