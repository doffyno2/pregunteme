<h2>What Is Anesthesia?</h2>

<p>Anesthesia is a combination of medicines that are used to prevent pain during a medical procedure. It is typically administered intravenously, but can also be administered through inhalation or injection. There are three main types of anesthesia: general, regional, and local. General anesthesia is used to put a patient into a deep sleep during a procedure, while regional and local anesthesia are used to numb only a specific area of the body.</p>
