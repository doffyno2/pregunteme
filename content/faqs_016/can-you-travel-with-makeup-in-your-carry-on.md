<h2>Is It Allowed to Bring Makeup in Your Carry On?</h2>

<p>The short answer is yes, it is generally allowed to bring makeup in your carry on. However, it is important to note that the specific products you bring can affect whether or not they are allowed. Generally, all makeup, including foundation, mascara, lipstick, and other cosmetics are allowed in your carry-on. However, certain items such as aerosol sprays, perfumes, and nail polish may be restricted.</p>

<h2>What Are the Rules and Regulations for Bringing Makeup in Your Carry On?</h2>

<p>The Transportation Security Administration (TSA) has specific rules and regulations regarding the type of cosmetics and beauty products you can bring on a plane in your carry-on. Generally, all makeup and cosmetics are allowed as long as they are in solid form (i.e. not a liquid or aerosol). This includes items such as foundation, blush, eyeshadow, mascara, lipstick, and other cosmetics. However, it is important to note that aerosol sprays, perfumes, and nail polish may be restricted, so it is best to check with the TSA before packing these items in your carry on.</p>
