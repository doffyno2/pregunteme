<p>Ray-Ban Wayfarer sunglasses are a classic style that never goes out of fashion. They offer protection from the sun’s rays, as well as a stylish look. However, after a while, these iconic sunglasses can become dirty and need to be cleaned. In this article, we’ll provide a step-by-step guide to cleaning your Ray-Ban Wayfarers.</p>

<h2>Step 1: Gather the Materials</h2>

<p>The first step is to gather all the materials you’ll need. You’ll need a soft cloth, warm water, dish soap, a toothbrush, and a soft-bristled brush. You should also make sure you have a safe place to lay your sunglasses while you’re cleaning them.</p>

<h2>Step 2: Remove Dust and Dirt</h2>

<p>Once you have all the materials you need, it’s time to start cleaning. Start by using the soft cloth to remove any dust or dirt from the lenses and frame. Gently wipe the lenses and frame with the cloth, being careful not to scratch the lenses with your fingernails.</p>

<h2>Step 3: Create a Cleaning Solution</h2>

<p>Next, you’ll need to create a cleaning solution. Fill a bowl or cup with warm water and add a few drops of dish soap. Then, stir the mixture until it is thoroughly combined.</p>

<h2>Step 4: Soak the Sunglasses</h2>

<p>Once you have a cleaning solution, it’s time to soak the sunglasses. Place your sunglasses in the cleaning solution and let them soak for five to 10 minutes. This will help loosen any dirt or debris that may be stuck to the lenses or frame.</p>

<h2>Step 5: Scrub the Lenses</h2>

<p>After soaking the sunglasses, you’ll need to scrub the lenses. Use a soft-bristled brush or a toothbrush to gently scrub the lenses. Be sure to pay special attention to any areas that may be particularly dirty or grimy. Once you’ve scrubbed the lenses, rinse them with warm water.</p>

<h2>Step 6: Dry the Sunglasses</h2>

<p>Once you’ve finished scrubbing the lenses, it’s time to dry the sunglasses. Use a soft cloth or a lint-free towel to gently dry the lenses and frame. Make sure to pay special attention to the nose pads and temples, as these areas can be particularly prone to dirt and debris.</p>

<h2>Step 7: Enjoy!</h2>

<p>Finally, your Ray-Ban Wayfarer sunglasses are clean and ready to go! Enjoy your clean sunglasses and bask in the knowledge that you were able to take care of them yourself.</p>
