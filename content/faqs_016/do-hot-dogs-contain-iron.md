<h2>What is Iron and Why is it Important?</h2>

Iron is an essential mineral that is necessary for the proper functioning of the body. It helps to form hemoglobin, which is responsible for carrying oxygen to the cells in the body. Without enough iron, the body can become anemic, resulting in fatigue and other health problems. Iron is also important for healthy skin, nails, and hair, as well as for energy production.
