<h2>What is a Sherpa?</h2>

<p>The term ‘Sherpa’ is used to describe the indigenous people of the Khumbu region of Nepal, located in the eastern Himalayas. They are renowned for their mountaineering skills and have been integral to the success of many of the world’s most famous mountain climbers. Sherpas are experienced in the terrain, culture, and languages of the region, and are able to provide invaluable assistance to climbers during their ascent.</p>

<h2>How Can a Sherpa Help?</h2>

<p>Sherpas are a key asset to any Everest climb.
