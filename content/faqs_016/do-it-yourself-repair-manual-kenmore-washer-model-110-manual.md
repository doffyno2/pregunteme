<h2>Troubleshooting Common Issues with the Kenmore Washer Model 110</h2>

<p>If you’re experiencing issues with your Kenmore Washer Model 110, start troubleshooting by checking the following common issues:</p>

<p>If your Kenmore Washer Model 110 isn’t turning on, check the power cord. Make sure it’s securely plugged into the wall outlet and that the outlet is working properly. <h3>2. Not Agitating</h3>

<p>If your Kenmore Washer Model 110 isn’t agitating, check the lid switch. Make sure it’s in the closed position. If it is, but the machine isn’t agitating, you may need to replace the lid switch. <p>If your Kenmore Washer Model 110 isn’t spinning, check the drive belt. Make sure it’s properly connected and not worn or frayed. If it is, you may need to replace the drive belt. If the drive belt is intact and the washer still won’t spin, you may need to call a repair service.</p>
