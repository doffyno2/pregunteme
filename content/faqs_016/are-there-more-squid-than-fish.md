<h2>What Are Fish and Squid?</h2>

<p>Fish are aquatic animals that have fins and breathe through gills. They come in a wide variety of sizes, shapes, and colors, and can be found in both saltwater and freshwater habitats. The most common type of fish is the bony fish, which includes species such as salmon, perch, and bass. </p>

<p>Squid, on the other hand, are cephalopods, meaning they have a head and tentacles. They typically live in the deep sea and feed on small fish and crustaceans. Squid can range in size from a few inches to over 50 feet long. </p>
