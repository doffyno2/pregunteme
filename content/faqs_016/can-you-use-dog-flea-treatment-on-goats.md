<p>The short answer is no; you should not use flea treatments intended for dogs on goats. While flea treatments are generally safe for dogs, they can be toxic to goats. This is because goats are more sensitive to certain chemicals than dogs are. Also, the flea treatments designed for dogs may not be effective on goats, as the parasites which affect goats differ from the parasites which affect dogs. Therefore, it is important to use flea treatments specifically made for goats.</p>
