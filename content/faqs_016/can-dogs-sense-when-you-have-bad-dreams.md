<h2>How Do Dogs Pick Up on Bad Dreams?</h2>

Dogs are incredibly sensitive animals and they are able to pick up on a variety of subtle cues and emotions from their owners. Still other dogs may become overly protective and try to stay close to their owners in an effort to comfort them.
