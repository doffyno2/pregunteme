Many people seek out mental health counseling to cope with a variety of issues, such as depression, anxiety, trauma, relationship problems, and substance abuse.

But when it comes to writing about mental health counseling, there is some confusion about whether or not to capitalize the term. The answer is yes, mental health counseling should always be capitalized when used as a title or to describe a profession.

<h2>Why Is Mental Health Counseling Capitalized?</h2>

Mental health counseling is capitalized because it is a profession and a specific type of therapy. It is important to capitalize this term because it is a distinct field of study and practice. By capitalizing the term, it helps to differentiate it from other forms of counseling.
