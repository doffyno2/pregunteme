<p>Obesity in fighting fish can be caused by several factors, including overfeeding, poor nutrition, and a sedentary lifestyle. Let’s take a closer look at each of these factors:</p>

<p>Like other types of fish, fighting fish need to be fed the right amounts of food. If they are overfed, they can become overweight and eventually obese. Too much food can also lead to digestive problems and other health issues.</p>

<p>Fighting fish need exercise to stay healthy. If they do not get enough exercise, they can become overweight and even obese. This is because they are not getting the exercise they need to burn off the extra calories they are consuming.</p>

<p>The best way to prevent obesity in fighting fish is to feed them the right amounts of food and provide them with exercise. Here are some tips on how to do this:</p>

<ul>
