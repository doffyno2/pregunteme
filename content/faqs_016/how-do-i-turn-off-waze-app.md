<p>Waze is a popular GPS navigation app that can be used on both Android and iOS devices. It provides live traffic information, route guidance, and other helpful features to make your driving experience easier and safer. Unfortunately, with all of the great features that Waze offers, it can be a bit of a battery hog. If you find that Waze is draining your battery, it might be time to turn it off.</p>

<p>In this article, we'll discuss how to turn off Waze on both Android and iOS devices. We'll also provide some tips on how to reduce battery drain from the app if you'd like to keep using it.</p>

<h2>Turning Off Waze on Android</h2>

<p>If you're using an Android device, turning off Waze is a simple process. Here's how to do it:</p>

<ul>
<li>Tap the Home button on your device to go to the home screen.</li>
<li>Open the Settings app.</li>
<li>Tap the Applications option.</li>
<li>Tap the Waze app.</li>
<li>Tap the Force Stop button.</li>
</ul>

<p>Once you've completed these steps, Waze will be turned off and will no longer run in the background. To turn the app back on, simply open the app and tap the Start button.</p>

<h2>Turning Off Waze on iOS</h2>

<p>If you're using an iOS device, the process for turning off Waze is slightly different. Here's what you need to do:</p>

<ul>
<li>Tap the Home button on your device to go to the home screen.</li>
<li>Open the Settings app.</li>
<li>Tap the General option.</li>
<li>Tap the Background App Refresh option.</li>
<li>Toggle the Waze option to the Off position.</li>
</ul>

<p>Once you've completed these steps, Waze will be turned off and will no longer run in the background. To turn the app back on, simply open the app and tap the Start button.</p>

<h2>Reducing Battery Drain from Waze</h2>

<p>If you'd like to keep using Waze but don't want it to drain your battery, there are some settings you can adjust to reduce battery drain. Here are a few tips:</p>

<ul>
<li>Turn off location services when you're not using Waze. This will prevent the app from constantly checking your location and draining your battery.</li>
<li>Turn off push notifications. You can turn off notifications for the app so that it won't send you notifications when it detects a new traffic incident.</li>
<li>Turn off audio alerts. Waze can be set up to give you audio alerts when it detects traffic incidents. You can turn these off to save battery life.</li>
<li>Turn off automatic zoom. Waze can be set up to automatically zoom in and out based on your speed. This can be a battery drain, so you can turn it off if you don't need it.</li>
</ul>
