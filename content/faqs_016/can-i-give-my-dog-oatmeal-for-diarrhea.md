<h2>What Is Diarrhea In Dogs?</h2>

<p>Diarrhea is a common problem in dogs, and it can be caused by a variety of things. It is defined as having three or more loose or watery stools within a 24-hour period. <h2>What Are The Symptoms Of Diarrhea In Dogs?</h2>

<p>The most common symptom of diarrhea in dogs is obviously having three or more loose or watery stools in a 24-hour period.
