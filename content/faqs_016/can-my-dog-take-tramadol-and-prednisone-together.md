<h2>What Is Prednisone and What Is It Used For?</h2>

<p>Prednisone is an anti-inflammatory steroid medication that is used to treat a variety of medical conditions. It works by blocking the production of certain chemicals in the body that cause inflammation. Prednisone is typically prescribed for conditions such as asthma, allergies, and certain autoimmune disorders. It can also be used to treat some types of arthritis, skin conditions, and certain types of cancer.</p>
