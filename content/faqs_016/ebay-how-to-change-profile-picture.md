<p>Having a profile picture on eBay can help you make a great first impression when buyers are considering buying something from you. It makes you appear more professional, and it’s a great way to establish your brand. Fortunately, it’s easy to change your profile picture on eBay. Here’s how.</p>

<h2>Step 1: Log into Your eBay Account</h2>

<p>The first step is to log into your eBay account. This will take you to your homepage. From here, click on the “My eBay” option near the top of the page.</p>

<h2>Step 2: Find Your Profile Picture</h2>

<p>Once you’re on the “My eBay” page, you’ll see your profile picture in the top right corner. You’ll also see a link that says “Change” next to it. Click that link to get started.</p>

<h2>Step 3: Choose a New Profile Picture</h2>

<p>When you click the “Change” link, you’ll be taken to a page where you can select a new profile picture. You can either upload a new photo from your computer, or you can choose one from your Facebook or Google+ accounts. Once you’ve selected a picture, click “Save Changes” to save it.</p>

<h2>Step 4: Crop Your Photo</h2>

<p>Once you’ve selected a new profile picture, you’ll be prompted to crop it so that it fits the size requirements of eBay. You can drag the box to select the area of the photo that you want to use, and then click “Crop Image” when you’re done.</p>

<h2>Step 5: Save Your Profile Picture</h2>

<p>When you’ve finished cropping your photo, you’ll be taken to a page where you can see the new profile picture. If you’re happy with it, click “Save Changes” to save it. Your new profile picture will now appear on your eBay profile.</p>
