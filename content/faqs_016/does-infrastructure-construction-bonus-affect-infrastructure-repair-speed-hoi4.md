<h2>What is Infrastructure Construction in HOI4?</h2>

Infrastructure construction in HOI4 is the process of building new infrastructure, such as roads, bridges, and railroads, which are necessary for a nation to keep its economic and military operations running smoothly. Infrastructure construction is managed through the game's Construction window, which is where players can see how many projects are currently in progress, how much money each project will cost, and how long it will take to complete.

<h2>What is Infrastructure Repair in HOI4?</h2>

Infrastructure repair in HOI4 is the process of repairing existing infrastructure that has been damaged by war or natural disasters. Repairing infrastructure is necessary to ensure that a nation's economy and military operations can continue to function efficiently. Like construction, repair is managed through the Construction window, where players can see how much money and resources are needed to complete the repairs.

<h2>How Does Infrastructure Construction Bonus Affect Infrastructure Repair Speed in HOI4?</h2>

In HOI4, the Infrastructure Construction Bonus is an in-game bonus that increases the speed at which a nation can construct and repair infrastructure. The bonus is activated by researching the Infrastructure Construction technology in the game's Technology tree, and is applied to every construction and repair project in the nation. The bonus can be increased further by researching additional technologies, such as Advanced Infrastructure Construction.

The Infrastructure Construction bonus affects infrastructure repair speed by reducing the amount of time it takes to complete repairs. This can be especially beneficial in times of war, when a nation needs to quickly repair its infrastructure in order to maintain its military operations. The bonus can also be beneficial during peacetime, as it allows a nation to build and repair infrastructure more quickly and efficiently.

The Infrastructure Construction bonus in HOI4 has several benefits for nations. The most obvious benefit is the increased speed at which infrastructure can be built and repaired, which can save a nation both time and resources.
