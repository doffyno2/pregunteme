<h2>What is Fast Travel?</h2>
Fast travel is a type of transportation available in some video games. It allows the player to quickly travel from one place to another, usually at no cost. Fast travel is often used to save time when traveling long distances or to access certain areas.

<h2>How Does Fast Travel Work in RDR2?</h2>
In Red Dead Redemption 2, fast travel is available through the use of horses, stagecoaches, and trains. Horses are the most common form of fast travel, as they are available everywhere in the game world. Players can call their horse at any time, and it will appear wherever they are. Stagecoaches are another form of fast travel in RDR2, and they are available in certain locations. These stagecoaches will take the player to a different town or city for a fee. Finally, trains are available for fast travel in RDR2. Trains are not available in every location, but they are available in some cities and towns.
