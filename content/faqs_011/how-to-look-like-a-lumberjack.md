<p>There’s something undeniably attractive about the rugged look of a lumberjack. With their workwear-inspired getup and ruggedly handsome features, it’s no wonder why many aspiring fashionistas are looking to emulate the look. To help you get the lumberjack look just right, we’ve rounded up the top tips and tricks to looking like a real lumberjack.</p>

<h2>Choose the Right Clothing</h2>

<p>The key to looking like a real lumberjack is all in the clothing. Start by picking up the classic workwear pieces such as flannel shirts, denim jeans, and work boots. For a more modern take on the look, try pairing these items with a vest, hoodie, and beanie for a hipster-inspired look that’s sure to turn heads.</p>

<h2>Accessorize with Care</h2>

<p>No lumberjack look is complete without the right accessories. Think suspenders, a bow tie, and a watch. For a more casual look, try a bandana or a knit beanie. Add a pair of aviator sunglasses for a truly rugged feel.</p>

<h2>Grow a Beard</h2>

<p>No lumberjack look is complete without a healthy, full beard. Growing a beard is not as easy as it looks – it takes time and patience. Start by investing in some quality beard oil and shampoo to ensure that your beard is well-groomed and looks its best. For extra style points, try adding a pair of moustache wax for a more classic look.</p>

<h2>Invest in Quality Workwear</h2>

<p>When it comes to looking like a lumberjack, the key is all in the details. Invest in some quality workwear pieces that will last you for years. Look for items that are made from durable materials such as cotton, canvas, and leather. Not only will these pieces look great, but they’ll also stand up to the wear and tear of your outdoor lifestyle.</p>

<h2>Keep it Simple</h2>

<p>When it comes to the lumberjack look, less is definitely more. Keep your look simple and focus on the basics – flannel shirt, denim jeans, and work boots. Avoid trying to over-accessorize or adding too many layers. A simple, classic look is all you need to look like a real lumberjack.</p>
