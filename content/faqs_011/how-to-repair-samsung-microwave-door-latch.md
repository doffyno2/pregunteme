<h2>How to Repair a Samsung Microwave Door Latch?</h2>

<h3>1. Clean the Latch</h3>

<p>If the latch seems to be working properly but still isn't holding the door closed, it may be that it has become clogged with dirt and debris. <h3>2. Check the Spring</h3>

<p>The next step is to check the spring that keeps the latch in place. Make sure that the spring is not broken or worn out. If it is, you may need to replace it. Additionally, make sure that the spring is properly connected to the latch. </p>
