Why Is Bleached Flour Not Good For Health?



In today's world of processed foods, bleached flour has become a staple ingredient in many recipes. Unfortunately, bleached flour is not the healthiest option when it comes to flour. In this article, we'll discuss why bleached flour is not good for health, and what alternatives are available.

<h2>What is Bleached Flour?</h2>

Bleached flour is a type of refined wheat flour that has been treated with chemicals to make it whiter and more refined. It is typically used to make products such as white bread, pastries, and other baked goods. Bleached flour can also be used to make pasta and other food products that require a white appearance. 

The process of bleaching flour involves the use of chlorine dioxide, which is a toxic chemical. This chemical is used to strip away the natural color of the wheat, leaving the flour a bright white color. This process also destroys some of the nutrients in the wheat, such as vitamins and minerals, while also changing the texture of the flour. 

<h2>Why Is Bleached Flour Not Good For Health?</h2>

Bleached flour is not the healthiest option when it comes to flour. This type of flour is highly processed and lacks the necessary nutrients that are found in whole wheat flour. The process of bleaching also strips away vitamins and minerals, which are essential for a healthy diet. 

The use of chlorine dioxide to bleach the flour can also be dangerous. This chemical can cause irritation to the skin, eyes, and respiratory system. It is also a known carcinogen and can cause cancer if consumed in large amounts over a period of time. 

In addition to this, bleached flour is also high in saturated fat and cholesterol. It is also high in sugar and sodium, which can contribute to obesity, high blood pressure, and other health problems.

Fortunately, there are several healthier alternatives to bleached flour. Whole wheat flour is an excellent option, as it contains all the nutrients of the wheat grain and is much less processed than bleached flour. Whole wheat flour is also a good source of fiber and protein, which are essential for a healthy diet.
