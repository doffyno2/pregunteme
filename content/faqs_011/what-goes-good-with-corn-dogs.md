Corn dogs, also known as pluto pups, are a classic American favorite. These hot dogs on a stick have been a staple of state fairs and carnivals for decades. But what else goes good with corn dogs? If you're looking for the perfect side dish, read on for some inspiration.

<h2>Fries</h2>

Potato fries are a classic accompaniment to corn dogs. Whether you opt for thick-cut steak fries, crinkle-cut fries, or curly fries, these potatoes are a great way to round out a corn dog meal. Some people like to dip their corn dogs in ketchup or mustard, a perfect opportunity to also dip the fries.

<h2>Onion Rings</h2>

If you are looking for something a bit more flavorful and crunchy than potato fries, why not try onion rings? Onion rings are delicious and can be made in many different ways. They range from thin and crispy to thick and soft. They also come in a variety of flavors, so you can find the perfect onion ring to accompany your corn dog.

<h2>Salad</h2>

Corn dogs can be a bit heavy, so a salad is a great way to lighten up the meal. You can make your own with lettuce, tomatoes, cucumbers, and other vegetables. Add some croutons, cheese, and dressing of your choice for an even more delicious salad.

<h2>Macaroni and Cheese</h2>

Macaroni and cheese is a classic side dish for corn dogs. Whether you make it from scratch or opt for the boxed variety, macaroni and cheese is a great way to add some cheesy goodness to your meal.

<h2>Fruit Salad</h2>

Fruit salad is a great way to get some healthy vitamins and minerals into your meal. You can make your own with whatever fruits you have on hand, or opt for pre-made fruit salads from the grocery store. Apples, oranges, and berries are all great choices for a fruit salad.

<h2>Pasta Salad</h2>

If you're looking for something a bit more substantial than a fruit salad, why not try a pasta salad? You can make your own with cooked pasta, vegetables, and a dressing of your choice. Or buy pre-made pasta salads from the grocery store. If you're feeling adventurous, you can even try making a macaroni and cheese salad.

<h2>Cole Slaw</h2>

Cole slaw is another classic side dish that goes great with corn dogs. It's cool and crunchy and adds a nice contrast to the warm and savory corn dogs. You can buy pre-made coleslaw at the grocery store, or make your own with shredded cabbage and a dressing of your choice. 

<h2>Baked Beans</h2>

Baked beans are a great side dish for corn dogs. They are savory and sweet, and the texture is perfect for a corn dog meal. You can buy pre-made canned baked beans, or make your own from scratch.

<h2>Baked Potato</h2>

Baked potatoes are a great accompaniment to corn dogs. They are filling and can be topped with all sorts of toppings to make them even more delicious. Choose from butter, sour cream, cheese, bacon, and more.

<h2>Corn on the Cob</h2>

Corn on the cob is a great way to add a bit of sweetness to your corn dog meal. You can steam or boil the corn, then slather it in butter, salt, and pepper. You can also add other toppings, like cheese, chili powder, or garlic butter.
