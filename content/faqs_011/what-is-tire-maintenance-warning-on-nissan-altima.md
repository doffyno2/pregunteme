<h2>What Does the Tire Maintenance Warning Light Mean?</h2>

<p>The tire maintenance warning light on the dashboard of your Nissan Altima will be triggered by several different issues. The most common cause is low tire pressure. This can be caused by a slow leak, a puncture, or simply by the tires being underinflated. The warning light will also come on if the tire pressure is too high. This can be caused by overinflating the tires or by using the wrong tires for your vehicle. The warning light can also indicate that the tires are worn out and need to be replaced.</p>

<p>If the tire maintenance warning light comes on in your Nissan Altima, it is important to take action as soon as possible. The first step is to check the tire pressure in all four tires.
