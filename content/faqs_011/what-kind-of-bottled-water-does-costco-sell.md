<h2>Different Types of Bottled Water Available at Costco</h2>

<p>At Costco, you can find a variety of bottled water types, including:</p>

<ul>

  <li>Sparkling Water: Sparkling water is water that has been infused with carbon dioxide gas. This gives it a fizzy, bubbly taste. It is available in both still and flavored varieties.   <li>Mineral Water: Mineral water is sourced from underground springs, and it contains naturally occurring minerals and other trace elements.   <li>Purified Water: Purified water is water that has been processed to remove impurities, bacteria, and other contaminants. It is often labeled as “distilled” or “reverse osmosis” water. It is a great option for those looking for a pure, clean-tasting water.</li>
  <li>Alkaline Water: Alkaline water is water that has been filtered and treated to increase its pH level. It is believed to have a variety of health benefits, including aiding in digestion and boosting immunity. Alkaline water is available in both still and sparkling varieties.</li>
</ul>
