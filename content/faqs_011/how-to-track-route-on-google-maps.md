<p>Google Maps is one of the most popular online mapping services available today. It is also an incredibly useful tool for tracking a route. <p>The process of tracking a route on Google Maps is relatively simple. All you need to do is input the start and end points of your journey, and Google Maps will provide you with a step-by-step guide on how to get there. You can even customize your route by adding waypoints along the way. This article will explain how to track a route on Google Maps, as well as provide some tips and tricks for making the most of the service.</p>

<h2>How to Create a Route on Google Maps</h2>

<p>Creating a route on Google Maps is easy. All you need to do is open up the Google Maps app (or website) and type in the start and end points of your journey. Once you have done this, Google Maps will generate a route for you to follow. You can also add waypoints along the route if you wish to customize it even further.</p>

<p>To add waypoints to your route, simply click the “+” symbol in the top right corner of the map. This will bring up a box where you can enter in the address or location you want to add as a waypoint. Once you have added all of your waypoints, simply click the “Done” button and your route will be displayed on the map.</p>

<h2>Tips and Tricks for Tracking a Route on Google Maps</h2>

<p>Once you have created your route, there are a few additional tips and tricks you can use to make the most out of Google Maps:</p>

<ul>
  <li><b>Choose the Best Route:</b> Google Maps will automatically generate a route for you, but it may not always be the best or most efficient route. To ensure that you are getting the best route, you can use the “Options” button to choose from several different routes. This will allow you to select the route that works best for you.</li>
  <li><b>Avoid Traffic:</b> One of the most useful features of Google Maps is its ability to show you traffic conditions in real-time. You can use this information to avoid congested areas and find the fastest route to your destination.</li>
  <li><b>Save Your Route:</b> Once you have created your route, you can save it for easy access in the future. Simply click the “Save” button at the top of the map and give your route a name. This will allow you to quickly access the route whenever you need it.</li>
</ul>
