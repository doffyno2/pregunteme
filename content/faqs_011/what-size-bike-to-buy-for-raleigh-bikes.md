<h2>Types of Raleigh Bikes</h2>

<p>Raleigh offers a variety of bikes ranging from road bikes, mountain bikes, hybrid bikes, and comfort bikes. Each type of bike has different features that make it suitable for different kinds of riders. Here is an overview of the different types of Raleigh bikes:</p>

<ul>
  <li><strong>Road Bikes</strong>- Road bikes are designed for speed and efficiency, and are perfect for long distance rides on paved surfaces. They typically feature a lightweight frame, narrow tires with low rolling resistance, and drop handlebars.</li>
  <li><strong>Mountain Bikes</strong>- Mountain bikes are designed for off-road riding on rough terrain. They feature a sturdy frame, wide tires with deep treads, and suspension systems to absorb the impact of rough terrain.</li>
  <li><strong>Hybrid Bikes</strong>- Hybrid bikes are a mix of road and mountain bikes, and are ideal for riders who want to ride both on and off-road. They typically feature a lightweight frame, wider tires than a road bike, and suspension forks.</li>
  <li><strong>Comfort Bikes</strong>- Comfort bikes are designed for casual riding on paved roads and trails. They feature a relaxed frame geometry, wider tires, and upright handlebars for a more comfortable riding position.</li>
</ul>
