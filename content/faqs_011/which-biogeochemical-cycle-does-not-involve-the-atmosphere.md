There are four main biogeochemical cycles: the carbon cycle, the nitrogen cycle, the oxygen cycle, and the phosphorus cycle. Each of these cycles involves the atmosphere in some way, but there is one cycle that does not involve the atmosphere: the sulfur cycle.
