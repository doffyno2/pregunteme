<h2>Introduction</h2>

Cotton hoodies are a great way to stay warm and comfortable during the cold winter months. Unfortunately, cotton hoodies can quickly become too big or too small if they are not cared for properly. If you’ve recently purchased a cotton hoodie that is too big, you may be wondering how to shrink a cotton hoodie. Fortunately, shrinking a cotton hoodie is a fairly simple process. In this article, we’ll discuss how to shrink a cotton hoodie in a few simple steps.

<h2>Steps to Shrink a Cotton Hoodie</h2>

<h3>Step 1: Gather Supplies</h3>

Before you begin shrinking your cotton hoodie, you’ll need to gather the necessary supplies. You’ll need a medium-sized bucket, a cup of white vinegar, and a cup of salt. You’ll also need a washing machine, laundry detergent, and a clothes dryer.

<h3>Step 2: Wash and Dry the Hoodie</h3>

Once you have all the necessary supplies, you can begin the process of shrinking your cotton hoodie. First, you should wash and dry the hoodie. Put the hoodie in the washing machine and add a small amount of laundry detergent. Wash the hoodie on the delicate cycle and then put it in the dryer and dry it on the low heat setting.

<h3>Step 3: Soak the Hoodie</h3>

Next, you’ll need to soak the hoodie in a mixture of vinegar and salt. Fill the bucket with warm water and add a cup of vinegar and a cup of salt. Stir the mixture until the salt and vinegar are dissolved. Then, put the hoodie in the water and let it soak for at least 30 minutes.

<h3>Step 4: Rinse the Hoodie</h3>

After soaking the hoodie, you’ll need to rinse it with cold water. This will help remove any excess vinegar or salt from the fabric. Once the hoodie has been rinsed, you can put it back in the washing machine and wash it on the delicate cycle.

<h3>Step 5: Dry the Hoodie</h3>

Once the hoodie has been washed, you can put it in the dryer and dry it on the low-heat setting. Make sure to check the hoodie periodically to make sure it is not shrinking too much. Once the hoodie has been dried, it should fit you perfectly.

<h2>Conclusion</h2>

Shrinking a cotton hoodie is a fairly simple process. All you need is a few supplies, a washing machine, a dryer, and a little bit of patience. By following the steps outlined above, you can easily shrink your cotton hoodie and ensure that it fits you perfectly.
