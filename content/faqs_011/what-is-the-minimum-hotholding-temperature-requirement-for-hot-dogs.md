<h2>What is the Ideal Hotdog Holding Temperature?</h2>

<p>According to the United States Department of Agriculture (USDA), the ideal temperature for hotdog holding is 140°F (60°C). This temperature should be maintained throughout the entire hotdog holding process, from the time of purchase to the time of service. Hotdogs should never be held at a temperature above 140°F (60°C) or below 135°F (57°C). If they are, they must be discarded within two hours.</p>

<p>If hotdogs are held at an improper temperature, they can become unsafe to consume. When hotdogs are held at too low of a temperature, bacteria can begin to grow and spoil the hotdogs. Additionally, if hotdogs are held at too high of a temperature, the proteins in the hotdogs can become denatured, which can make them tough and unpalatable.</p>
