<h3>Step 2: Press and Hold the Reset Button</h3>

<p>Once you have located the reset button, press and hold it for three seconds. This will reset the maintenance light on your Highlander.</p>

<h3>Step 3: Release the Reset Button</h3>

<p>After three seconds, release the reset button. The maintenance light should now be reset, and your Highlander is ready for regular maintenance.</p>
