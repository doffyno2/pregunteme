<h2>What is Peppermint Oil?</h2>

Peppermint oil is extracted from the leaves of the Mentha piperita plant and is composed of essential oils, including menthol and menthone. <h2>Is it Safe for Dogs to Inhale Peppermint Oil?</h2>
