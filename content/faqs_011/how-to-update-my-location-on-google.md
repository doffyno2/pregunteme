<h2>How to Update Your Home Address on Google</h2>

<p>Updating your home address on Google is quite simple. All you need to do is follow the steps below:</p>

<ol>
  <li>Go to <a href="https://maps.google.com" target="_blank">Google Maps</a> and search for your address.</li>
  <li>Click on the "Add a missing place" option.</li>
  <li>Fill out the form with your address information. Make sure to include your full address, including the city, state, and zip code.</li>
  <li>Click the "Submit" button.</li>
  <li>Google will review your request and, if approved, your address will be added to Google Maps.</li>
</ol>
