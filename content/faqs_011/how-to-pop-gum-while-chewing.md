<h2>What is “Popping” Gum?</h2>

“Popping” gum is a way of making a loud sound with your gum. It involves pushing the gum with your tongue to create a popping sound. This technique is often used to impress friends or to relieve boredom. It's similar to “clicking” gum, which is when you use your teeth to make a clicking sound.

<h2>How to Pop Your Gum Correctly</h2>

Popping your gum correctly is key to avoiding a mess and protecting your teeth. Here's how to do it: 

<h3>Step 1: Place the Gum in Your Mouth</h3>

Start by placing the gum in your mouth. Make sure it's not too big, as this could make it difficult to control. 

<h3>Step 2: Place Your Tongue on the Gum</h3>

Once the gum is in your mouth, place your tongue on the gum. This will help create a seal that will prevent the gum from flying out of your mouth. 

<h3>Step 3: Push the Gum with Your Tongue</h3>

Once you have your tongue on the gum, push the gum with your tongue in order to create a popping sound. This will require a bit of practice, but with time you'll be able to master it. 

<h3>Step 4: Spit Out the Gum</h3>
