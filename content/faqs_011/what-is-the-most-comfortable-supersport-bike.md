<h2>What Makes a Supersport Bike So Uncomfortable?</h2>

Supersport bikes are designed to be as light and agile as possible, which means that they often sacrifice comfort for performance. The riding position of a supersport bike is often very aggressive, with the rider leaning forward over the tank and gripping the handlebars tightly to maintain control. The seat is typically low and hard, which can cause discomfort during long rides. The suspension is typically stiff and the tires are narrow, which can make the ride bumpy and uncomfortable.
