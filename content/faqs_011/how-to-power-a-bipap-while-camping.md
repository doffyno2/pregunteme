<h2>Understanding BiPAP Machines</h2>

BiPAP stands for Bilevel Positive Airway Pressure, and it is a type of medical ventilator that is used to treat sleep-related breathing disorders such as sleep apnea, chronic obstructive pulmonary disease, and other respiratory illnesses. BiPAP machines provide two levels of air pressure, an inhalation pressure and an exhalation pressure, that help to regulate the patient’s breathing and ensure that the patient is receiving an adequate amount of oxygen.
