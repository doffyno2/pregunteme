<h2>Steps for Resetting the Maintenance Required Light on a 2017 Toyota Camry</h2>

<p>Before you start, make sure your vehicle is parked on a level surface and the engine is off. Then, follow these steps:</p>

<ol>
  <li>Open the hood and locate the oil filter cap. It will be on the front of the engine and should have a yellow label with a “Maintenance Required” light symbol.</li>
  <li>Unscrew the oil filter cap and take out the old oil filter. Replace it with a new one and make sure it is securely tightened.</li>
  <li>Locate the oil dipstick. It will be on the front of the engine and should have a yellow label with an oil can symbol.</li>
  <li>Remove the dipstick and check the oil level. If it is low, add oil until it reaches the full mark. If it is already full, you can skip this step.</li>
  <li>Replace the dipstick and make sure it is firmly secured.</li>
  <li>Locate the reset button, which will be located near the oil filter cap. It should have a yellow label with a “Maintenance Required” light symbol.</li>
  <li>Press and hold the reset button for 3-5 seconds until the “Maintenance Required” light turns off.</li>
  <li>Start the engine and allow it to run for a few minutes.</li>
  <li>Turn off the engine and close the hood.</li>
</ol>
