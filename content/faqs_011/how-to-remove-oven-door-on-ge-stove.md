<h2>Step 1: Removing the Door Handle</h2>

<p>The first step in removing the oven door is to remove the handle. To do this, insert a putty knife or flat head screwdriver between the handle and the door, and gently pry the handle off. This should be done with care, as the handle is held in place with plastic clips. After the handle has been removed, unscrew the screws located on the inside of the handle, and remove them. </p>

<h2>Step 2: Removing the Oven Door</h2>

<p>Once the handle has been removed, the oven door can be taken off. To do this, open the oven door as far as possible, and locate the two screws at the top and bottom of the door. Unscrew these screws, and gently pull the door off of the oven. It may be necessary to wiggle the door slightly to ensure that all clips are released. </p>

<h2>Step 3: Reinstalling the Oven Door</h2>

<p>Reinstalling the oven door is just as easy as removing it. Simply line up the door with the oven, and press gently until all clips are secured. Make sure that the door is firmly in place before continuing. Once the door is in place, replace the screws at the top and bottom, and reattach the handle. </p>
