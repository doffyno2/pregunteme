<p>The maintenance light on your Highlander 2018 is a signal that it is time for your vehicle to be serviced. The light indicates when it is time to change the oil, check the brakes, and replace filters and other parts. Resetting the maintenance light on a Highlander 2018 is a simple process that requires a few steps. Here's how to do it.</p>

<h2>Steps to Resetting the Maintenance Light on a Highlander 2018</h2>

<p>To reset the maintenance light on your Highlander 2018, you will need to do the following steps:</p>

<h3>Step 1: Put the key in the ignition</h3>

<p>Turn the key to the "on" position and keep the engine off. Make sure all doors are closed.</p>

<h3>Step 2: Locate the control panel</h3>

<p>Locate the control panel on the instrument cluster. It should be located in the lower center of the dashboard.</p>

<h3>Step 3: Press and hold the "Reset" button</h3>

<p>Press and hold the "Reset" button for five seconds. The light should then turn off.</p>

<h3>Step 4: Turn off the car</h3>

<p>Once the light has been reset, turn off the car and remove the key from the ignition.</p>

<h2>Frequently Asked Questions</h2>

<h3>How often should I reset the maintenance light?</h3>

<p>It is recommended that you reset the maintenance light every time you have your vehicle serviced. This will help to ensure that the light is accurate and will alert you when it is time to have your vehicle serviced again.</p>

<h3>What happens if I don't reset the maintenance light?</h3>

<p>If you don't reset the maintenance light, it may stay illuminated even though your vehicle has been serviced. This could lead to inaccurate readings and may also cause unnecessary wear and tear on your vehicle.</p>

<h3>Can I reset the maintenance light myself?</h3>

<p>Yes, you can reset the maintenance light on your Highlander 2018 yourself. The steps outlined above should help you to do this quickly and easily.</p>

<h3>What other maintenance should I do to my Highlander 2018?</h3>

<p>In addition to resetting the maintenance light, it is important to have regular maintenance performed on your Highlander 2018. This includes changing the oil, checking the brakes, and replacing filters and other parts as necessary.</p>

<h3>What should I do if the maintenance light won't turn off?</h3>

<p>If the maintenance light won't turn off after you have reset it, it is possible that there is a problem with the vehicle. You should consult with a qualified mechanic to determine the cause of the problem and to have it fixed.</p>

<h3>What happens if I ignore the maintenance light?</h3>

<p>Ignoring the maintenance light can lead to serious problems with your vehicle. Lack of regular maintenance can cause premature wear and tear, leading to costly repairs and possibly even a breakdown. It is important to follow the manufacturer's recommendations for servicing your vehicle.</p>
