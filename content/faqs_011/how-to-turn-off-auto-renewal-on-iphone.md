<p>iPhone users can easily subscribe to applications and services that offer automatic renewal. But when the time comes for renewal, some users may not want to pay for the automated subscription. Fortunately, it's easy to turn off auto-renewal on iPhone for all your subscriptions.</p>

<h2>What is Auto-Renewal?</h2>

<p>Auto-renewal is a feature on iPhone that allows users to automatically renew their subscription for applications or services. This feature is available on most apps and services, including Apple Music, Apple TV+, and even some games. Auto-renewal is convenient as it allows users to continue enjoying their services without having to manually renew them each time.</p>

<h2>Why Should You Turn Off Auto-Renewal?</h2>

<p>Auto-renewal may be convenient, but it can also be costly if you forget to cancel your subscription. Some applications and services may also have hidden fees or extra charges that are automatically charged to your credit card if you don't turn off auto-renewal. It's also important to note that turning off auto-renewal does not cancel your current subscription, so you will still be able to enjoy the services until the end of the current billing cycle.</p>

<h2>How To Turn Off Auto-Renewal On iPhone</h2>

<p>Turning off auto-renewal on iPhone is easy and straightforward. Here's a step-by-step guide on how to do it:</p>

<h3>Step 1: Open the App Store on Your iPhone</h3>

<p>The first step is to open the App Store on your iPhone. To do this, simply tap on the App Store icon on your home screen.</p>

<h3>Step 2: Tap on Your Profile Picture</h3>

<p>Once you've opened the App Store, navigate to the bottom of the page and tap on your profile picture. This will open the Account Settings page.</p>

<h3>Step 3: Select Subscriptions</h3>

<p>On the Account Settings page, scroll down and select the "Subscriptions" option. This will open a list of all your current subscriptions.</p>

<h3>Step 4: Select the Subscription You Want to Cancel</h3>

<p>From the list of subscriptions, select the one you want to cancel. This will open the subscription details page.</p>

<h3>Step 5: Turn Off Auto-Renewal</h3>

<p>On the subscription details page, select the "Turn Off Automatic Renewal" option. This will turn off auto-renewal for the selected subscription.</p>
