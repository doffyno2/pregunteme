Podium training is a type of strength and conditioning program that is used by professional athletes and fitness enthusiasts alike. It involves performing exercises on a raised platform, or “podium”, which increases the difficulty of the exercises and forces the athlete to use more stabilization muscles. Podium training is an effective way to improve balance, coordination, and overall strength, as well as develop explosive power.
