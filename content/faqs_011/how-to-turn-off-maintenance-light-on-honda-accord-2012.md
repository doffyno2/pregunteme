<h2>What Is the Maintenance Light?</h2>

<p>The maintenance light is a small indicator light located on the dashboard of the Honda Accord 2012. It looks like a wrench or a key and is usually yellow or orange in color. The light will come on when the vehicle needs an oil change, a tire rotation, or some other type of service. It is important to pay attention to this light, as it will alert you to any maintenance that needs to be done in order to keep your vehicle running efficiently.</p>

<h2>How to Turn Off the Maintenance Light on a Honda Accord 2012</h2>

<p>If the maintenance light comes on and you're not sure how to turn it off, then follow these steps:</p>

<ul>
  <li>Start the engine and let the car idle for a few minutes.</li>
  <li>Turn off the engine and wait for the maintenance light to turn off.</li>
  <li>Turn the engine back on and wait for the maintenance light to turn off again.</li>
  <li>Turn the engine off and then start it again. This should reset the maintenance light.</li>
</ul>

<p>If the light is still on after following these steps, then you should take your car to a mechanic for further inspection. The mechanic will be able to diagnose the problem and advise you on the best course of action.</p>
