<h2>What Are Dirt Bikes?</h2>

A dirt bike is a type of motorcycle designed for off-road use. It typically has a lightweight frame and knobby tires, which makes it ideal for navigating terrain that is difficult to traverse with regular vehicles. Dirt bikes are known for their agility, speed, and maneuverability, which makes them popular with both amateur and professional riders.

<h2>Do I Need a License or Registration to Ride a Dirt Bike in Washington?</h2>

Yes, you do need a license and registration to ride a dirt bike in Washington. Additionally, you must register your dirt bike with the Washington Department of Licensing. The registration fee for a dirt bike is $30.
