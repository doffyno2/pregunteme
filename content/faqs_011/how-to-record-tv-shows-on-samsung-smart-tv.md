<p>Having a Samsung Smart TV is great for watching your favorite movies and TV shows. But what about when you want to record them for later viewing? Recording TV shows on a Samsung Smart TV is easy, and there are a few different methods you can use.</p>

<h2>Using the Smart Hub</h2>

<p>If you have a Samsung Smart TV with the Smart Hub feature, you can use it to record your favorite shows. First, open the Smart Hub and select the “Record” option. Then, select the show you want to record, and press the “Record” button. Your show will begin recording, and you can watch it later.</p>

<h2>Using a USB Flash Drive or External Hard Drive</h2>

<p>If you have a USB flash drive or external hard drive, you can use it to record shows on your Samsung Smart TV. First, connect the flash drive or hard drive to the TV. Then, open the Smart Hub and select the “Record” option. Select the show you want to record, and press the “Record” button. Your show will begin recording, and you can watch it later.</p>

<h2>Using a DVR</h2>
