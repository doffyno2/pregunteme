<h2>Steps to Measuring Shoulders for Clothing</h2>

<p>Measuring your shoulders for clothing is relatively straightforward. All you need is a tape measure and access to a full-length mirror. Here are the steps to measure your shoulders for clothing:</p>

<h3>Step 1: Stand Upright</h3>

<p>The first step is to stand in front of a full-length mirror. Make sure that you are standing upright, with your chest and head straight. Do not slouch or hunch your shoulders, as this can affect the accuracy of the measurement.</p>

<h3>Step 2: Measure Across the Back</h3>

<p>Once you are standing upright, take the tape measure and wrap it around your back, across your shoulder blades. Make sure that the tape measure is level and not twisted. Once you have done this, take a look at the measurement. This is your shoulder measurement.</p>

<h3>Step 3: Measure from the Neck</h3>

<p>If you want to be extra precise, you can also measure from your neck. Wrap the tape measure around your neck, making sure that it is not twisted. The tape measure should be touching the base of your neck, but not too tight. Once you have done this, take a look at the measurement. This is your shoulder measurement.</p>
