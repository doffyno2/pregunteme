<h2>What is 4C?</h2>

4C is a sizing system used by European and Japanese shoe manufacturers. It is also sometimes referred to as "size 4". The system is based on the length of a person's foot in centimeters, with each "C" representing a 1 cm increase in length. For example, if your foot is 28 cm long, you would wear a 4C (or size 4) shoe.
