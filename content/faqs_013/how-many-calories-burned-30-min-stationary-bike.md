<h2>What is Your Basal Metabolic Rate?</h2>

Your basal metabolic rate (BMR) is the amount of energy your body needs to function at rest. It’s determined by your age, gender, height, and weight. The higher your BMR, the more calories you’ll burn when exercising.

<h2>How Many Calories Does a 30-Minute Session on the Stationary Bike Burn?</h2>

The number of calories burned during a 30-minute session on the stationary bike depends on a few different factors, including your BMR and the intensity of your workout. Generally speaking, you can expect to burn between 200 and 500 calories per 30-minute session on the stationary bike.
