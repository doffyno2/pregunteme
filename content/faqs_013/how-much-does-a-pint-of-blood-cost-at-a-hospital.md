<h2>What is a Blood Transfusion?</h2>

A blood transfusion is a procedure in which whole blood or blood components are transferred from one person to another. Blood transfusions are used to treat a variety of conditions, including anemia, cancer, and traumatic injuries. Blood transfusions can also be used to replace lost blood during surgery.
