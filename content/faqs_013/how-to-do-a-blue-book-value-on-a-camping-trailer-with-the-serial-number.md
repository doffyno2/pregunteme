<h2>What is a Blue Book Value?</h2>

A Blue Book Value is an estimate of the market value of a camping trailer. It is determined by looking at the trailer's make and model, condition, year of manufacture, and other factors. <h2>What do I Need to Calculate a Blue Book Value?</h2>
