<h2>How to Prepare Popcorn?</h2>

Popcorn can be prepared in a variety of ways. It can be cooked in a pot on the stovetop, in a popcorn machine, or in the microwave. For stovetop popcorn, simply heat a pot over medium heat and add a few tablespoons of oil. Once the oil is hot, add a few kernels of popcorn and cover the pot with a lid. Once the kernels start to pop, shake the pot back and forth over the heat until all of the kernels are popped. For microwave popcorn, simply place a few tablespoons of popcorn kernels in a brown paper bag, fold the top of the bag over, and place it in the microwave.
