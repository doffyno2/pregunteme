<h2>Using Commercial Cleaners</h2>

<p>The easiest way to clean pine tar off a bat is by using a commercial cleaner designed specifically for removing sticky substances. These cleaners typically come in aerosol cans and can be sprayed directly onto the bat. Some of these products are specifically designed for use on baseball bats and may even be labeled as such. Once the cleaner has been applied, use a soft cloth to gently scrub the bat in a circular motion. This will help to loosen any stubborn bits of pine tar that may have adhered to the bat. After cleaning, rinse the bat with water and allow it to air dry.</p>
