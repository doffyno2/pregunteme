<h2>What Is the Pantry Moth Life Cycle?</h2>

<p>The pantry moth life cycle consists of four stages: egg, larvae, pupae and adult moth. Adult moths lay their eggs in food sources such as grains, cereals and dried fruits. The entire life cycle can take anywhere from four to eight weeks.</p>
