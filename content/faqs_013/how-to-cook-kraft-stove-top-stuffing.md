<h2>Ingredients</h2>

<p>Kraft Stove Top Stuffing mix contains all the necessary ingredients to make a delicious stuffing, including the bread crumbs, seasonings, and butter. Depending on the flavor you choose, the ingredients may vary slightly. For instance, the original flavor contains bread crumbs, seasoning salt, onion powder, and butter. The herb and garlic flavor contains bread crumbs, garlic powder, herbs, and butter. The chicken flavor contains bread crumbs, chicken bouillon, onion powder, and butter.</p>

<h2>Preparation</h2>

<p>Kraft Stove Top Stuffing is easy to prepare in just minutes. First, bring one and a quarter cups of water to a boil in a medium saucepan. Then, add the contents of the stuffing mix and stir until all of the ingredients are well combined. Reduce the heat to low and simmer for five minutes, stirring occasionally. Once the stuffing has cooked, remove it from the heat and serve it hot.</p>
