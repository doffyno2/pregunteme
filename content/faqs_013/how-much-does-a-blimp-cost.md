<h2>What Is a Blimp?</h2>

A blimp is a type of aircraft that is filled with helium and has no rigid frame. Blimps are much larger than traditional hot air balloons and are often seen in the sky advertising products and services. Many blimps are equipped with cameras and lights, and they can be used to capture stunning footage of the area below.
