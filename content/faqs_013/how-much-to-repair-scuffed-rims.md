<h2>What Causes Scuffing of the Rims?</h2>

<p>Scuffing of the rims can be caused by a number of factors, including wear and tear, road debris, and improper maintenance. Over time, your tires can wear down, leaving the rims exposed to the elements. Road debris such as rocks and gravel can chip away at the paint and clear coat on your rims, causing scuffing. In addition, improper care and maintenance of your rims can also contribute to their scuffing. For example, if you don't regularly clean and wax your rims, dirt and grime can build up and wear away at the paint and clear coat.</p>
