<h2>Understanding Battery Capacity</h2>

<p>AA batteries also have a rated capacity, which is measured in milliamp-hours (mAh). The rated capacity of a AA battery is an indication of how much energy the battery can store, and is usually printed on the battery's label. For example, a AA alkaline battery may have a rated capacity of 2,500 mAh, while a NiMH battery may have a capacity of 1,500 mAh. The higher the capacity, the longer the battery will last before needing to be recharged or replaced.</p>
