<h2>What is a Crankbait?</h2>

<p>A crankbait is an artificial lure designed to imitate the swimming action of a small fish. When retrieved properly, it will dive below the water’s surface, creating the illusion of a baitfish or other prey. The motion of the lure attracts predators such as bass, walleye, and pike. Crankbaits can be fished in both fresh and saltwater, making them a versatile and popular choice among anglers.</p>

  <li>Shallow diving crankbaits – These are designed to dive shallow and are typically used for fishing in shallow water.</li>
  <li>Deep diving crankbaits – These are designed to dive deep and are typically used for fishing in deep water.</li>
  <li>Jerkbaits – These are designed to imitate a wounded baitfish and create a jerking action when retrieved.</li>
