<h2>What is Protein? </h2>

Protein is an essential macronutrient that is necessary for the growth and maintenance of all cells in the body. It is made up of amino acids that are used by the body to build and maintain muscle, bone, and other tissues. Protein is also necessary for many metabolic processes and can help to boost energy levels. 

<h2>How Much Protein is in Breakfast Sausage? </h2>

The exact amount of protein in breakfast sausage will vary depending on the type and brand. Generally speaking, breakfast sausage contains around 8-10 grams of protein per 2 ounce serving. This is equivalent to around 16-20% of the daily recommended intake of protein for an adult.

<h2>Types of Breakfast Sausage </h2>

The type of breakfast sausage you choose will affect the amount of protein in your serving. For example, pork sausage usually contains more protein than turkey or vegetarian sausage.

<h2>Other Protein Sources </h2>

<h2>Protein Requirements </h2>

The amount of protein each person needs will vary depending on their age, sex, and physical activity level. Generally speaking, adults should aim to consume 0.8-1 gram of protein per kilogram of bodyweight per day.
