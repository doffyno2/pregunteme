<h2>What Is a Best Man?</h2>

A best man is a male friend or family member of the groom who stands alongside him at the altar. He is responsible for delivering a speech on behalf of the groom during the reception, as well as ensuring the groom looks and feels his best on his wedding day. He is also responsible for hosting any pre-wedding events such as bachelor and bachelorette parties.

<h2>How Many Best Men Can You Have?</h2>

The short answer is that there is no definitive answer to this question. Ultimately, the decision of how many best men you want to have is entirely up to you. Some couples decide to have just one best man, while others choose to have two or even three.

<h3>Pros and Cons of Having Multiple Best Men</h3>

There are both pros and cons to having multiple best men. Here we will outline the most common advantages and disadvantages of having more than one best man.

<h4>Pros</h4>

  <li>More speeches – If you have more than one best man, this means that there will be more speeches to prepare. This can add more pressure to the wedding day and take away from the time available for other activities.</li>
