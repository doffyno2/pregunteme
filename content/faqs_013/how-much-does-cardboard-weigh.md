<h2>Types of Cardboard</h2>

<p>There are two main types of cardboard: corrugated cardboard and paperboard. Corrugated cardboard is made up of three layers: an outer layer of paper, a middle layer of fluted cardboard, and an inner layer of paper. This type of cardboard is usually used for packaging, as it is very strong and durable. </p>

<p>Paperboard is made from a single layer of paper, and is usually used for lighter packaging needs. It is not as strong and durable as corrugated cardboard, but it is lighter in weight. </p>
