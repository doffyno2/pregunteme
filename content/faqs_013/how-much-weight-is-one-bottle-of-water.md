<p>The average weight of a water bottle depends on its size and material. A 16.9-ounce (500ml) plastic water bottle weighs around 0.9 ounces (25 grams). A metal water bottle, such as a stainless steel bottle, may weigh slightly more than a plastic bottle.</p>
