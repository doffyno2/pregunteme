<p>Jiffy Pop is a brand of popcorn that can be cooked on a stovetop. It is fun and easy to make and can be enjoyed as a snack or a meal. Making Jiffy Pop on a gas stove is a simple process that only requires a few ingredients and a few steps. With some basic kitchen equipment, you can make Jiffy Pop in minutes.</p>

<h2>Ingredients</h2>

<p>The ingredients for Jiffy Pop are simple and inexpensive. You will need:</p>

<ul>
  <li>1 cup of Jiffy Pop popcorn kernels</li>
  <li>2 tablespoons of oil or butter (optional)</li>
  <li>Salt and other seasonings to taste</li>
</ul>

<h2>Preparing the Jiffy Pop</h2>

<p>Before you begin, make sure your gas stove is clean and free of any debris. Place the Jiffy Pop pan on the stove, add the popcorn kernels and oil (if using), and turn the heat on medium. When the oil is hot, add the seasonings and stir to combine. Place the lid on the pan and leave it on the stove.</p>

<h2>Cooking the Jiffy Pop</h2>

<p>Once the heat is on, the popcorn will begin to pop. Listen carefully and watch the lid as the popcorn pops. The lid will begin to move when the popcorn is ready. When the popping slows down, turn off the heat and remove the pan from the stove. Let the Jiffy Pop cool for a few minutes before serving.</p>

<h2>Serving the Jiffy Pop</h2>

<p>Once the Jiffy Pop has cooled, carefully remove the lid and serve. You can enjoy the popcorn plain or add butter and other seasonings for extra flavor. You can also add other ingredients such as cheese, nuts, or dried fruit for a more creative snack. Enjoy!</p>
