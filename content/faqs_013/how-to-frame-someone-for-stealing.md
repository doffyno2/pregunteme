<h2>What Is Framing Someone?</h2>

Framing someone is a criminal act of setting up an innocent person to receive blame or punishment for a crime they did not commit. It involves misleading or manipulating evidence, witnesses, and other information to make it appear as though the innocent person is guilty of the crime. Framing someone for stealing is a serious offense, and should not be taken lightly.
