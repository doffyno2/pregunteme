<h2>Gas and Fuel Costs</h2>

The first cost associated with a cross-country road trip is fuel. Depending on the size of the vehicle and the length of the trip, the cost of fuel can vary greatly. A trip across the United States in a sedan or SUV can cost anywhere from $500 to $2,000 in fuel alone. It is important to budget for fuel costs before embarking on a cross-country journey.

<h2>Lodging Costs</h2>

In addition to fuel costs, travelers will need to budget for lodging. Lodging costs can vary greatly depending on the destination, with hotel prices ranging from $50 to $200 per night.
