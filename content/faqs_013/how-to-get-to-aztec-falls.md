<p>Aztec Falls is located in the San Juan National Forest in New Mexico. It is a popular destination for hikers and nature lovers alike, as it offers stunning views of the Sangre de Cristo Mountains, the Rio Grande River, and the surrounding forest.</p>

<p>The best way to get to Aztec Falls is by car. The nearest major city is Albuquerque, which is about an hour and a half drive away.
