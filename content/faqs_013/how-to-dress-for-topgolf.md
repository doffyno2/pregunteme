<h2>What to Wear to TopGolf</h2>

<p>When you're playing TopGolf, comfort should be your top priority. You'll be swinging a golf club for long periods of time, so you want to make sure that you're wearing loose-fitting clothing that won't restrict your movements. Shorts or lightweight trousers are ideal, as are breathable fabrics like cotton or linen. Avoid wearing anything too tight-fitting, as it could restrict your range of motion.</p>

<p>It's also important to wear comfortable shoes. You'll be on your feet for extended periods of time, so it's important to wear shoes that provide adequate support. Tennis shoes, golf shoes, or lightweight running shoes are all good options. Avoid wearing sandals or flip-flops, as they won't provide you with the stability and support you need.</p>

<p>You also want to avoid wearing bulky clothing and jewelry, as they can interfere with your swing. If you are playing in a competition, you may need to adhere to a specific dress code, so make sure you check with the organizers before you arrive.</p>

<h2>What Not to Wear to TopGolf</h2>

<p>As a general rule, you want to avoid wearing anything too tight-fitting or restrictive. This includes jeans, as they can restrict your range of motion. You should also avoid wearing shorts that are too short, as this can be distracting for other players. Furthermore, avoid wearing any clothing that is too revealing, as this can be inappropriate in a sporting environment.</p>

<p>You should also avoid wearing any jewelry that could interfere with your swing, such as long necklaces or rings. If you are wearing a watch, make sure that it is securely fastened so that it does not get in the way of your swing.</p>

<h2>What Accessories to Wear to TopGolf</h2>

<p>In addition to the right clothing, there are a few accessories that can make your TopGolf experience more enjoyable. A hat or visor will help to keep the sun out of your eyes, while sunglasses can help protect your eyes from the sun's glare. A towel can be useful for wiping away sweat, while a water bottle can help you stay hydrated.</p>

<p>If you're playing in a competition, you may need to wear a specific type of shirt or blouse. In this case, make sure to check the dress code before you arrive. You may also need to wear a glove to protect your hands, so make sure to bring one with you.</p>
