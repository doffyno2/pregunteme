<h2>What is a Cylinder 6 Misfire?</h2>

<p>A cylinder 6 misfire is a problem that occurs when one or more of the spark plugs in the vehicle’s engine fails to ignite the fuel-air mixture properly. This can lead to incomplete combustion, which can cause the engine to run poorly and even cause damage to the engine components. The most common cause of a cylinder 6 misfire is a faulty spark plug, but other causes can include a worn-out ignition coil, a bad fuel injector, or an incorrect spark plug gap.</p>

<h2>Cylinder 6 Misfire Repair Cost</h2>

<p>The cost of repairing a cylinder 6 misfire will depend on the cause of the misfire and the type of vehicle. Generally speaking, the cost of replacing a faulty spark plug will be much cheaper than replacing a worn-out ignition coil. Replacing a fuel injector or spark plug gap can also be expensive, so it is important to diagnose the exact cause of the misfire before attempting to repair it. On average, a cylinder 6 misfire repair can cost anywhere from $50 to $500, depending on the cause and type of vehicle.</p>
