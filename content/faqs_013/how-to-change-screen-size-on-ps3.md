<h2>What You Need to Change the Screen Size on PS3</h2>

<p>To change the screen size on your PS3, you will need the following:</p>

<ul>
	<li>A PS3 console</li>
	<li>A television or monitor</li>
	<li>A controller</li>
</ul>

<h2>Steps to Change Screen Size on PS3</h2>

<p>Once you have all the necessary items, you can follow these steps to change the screen size on your PS3:</p>

<ol>
	<li>Turn on your PS3 and television or monitor.</li>
	<li>Connect your PS3 to your television or monitor using the appropriate cable.</li>
	<li>Select the “Display Settings” option from the “Settings” menu on your PS3.</li>
	<li>Select the “Screen Size” option and choose the desired screen size.</li>
</ol>

<p>Once you have selected the desired screen size, it will be applied to your PS3. You can now enjoy playing your favorite games on the new screen size.</p>
