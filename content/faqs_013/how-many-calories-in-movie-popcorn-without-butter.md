<h2>What Is Movie Popcorn?</h2>

Movie popcorn is popcorn that is prepared specifically for movie theaters. It is made with kernels that are larger than typical kernels and are cooked in large batches with specialized oil. This oil is typically made with highly refined soybean oil, which is high in monounsaturated fats and is a healthier alternative to other types of oils. The popcorn is then cooked in a large popper that uses hot air to pop the kernels. 

<h2>How Many Calories Are in Movie Popcorn Without Butter?</h2>

Movie popcorn without butter contains approximately 30-35 calories per cup. This is significantly lower than the average of 55-60 calories per cup for movie popcorn with butter. Furthermore, the popcorn without butter does not contain any saturated or trans fats, which makes it a healthier alternative to popcorn with butter.

Movie popcorn without butter is a great snack option for those who are trying to maintain a healthy diet. It is low in calories, fat, and sodium, and is also a good source of fiber and protein. Furthermore, movie popcorn without butter does not contain any cholesterol or added sugar.
