<h2>What is Strava?</h2>

<p>Strava is a social fitness platform that allows athletes to track and share their activities with other users. It includes activities such as running, cycling, swimming, and more. Strava also provides users with access to a variety of tools such as route building, leaderboards, and segment tracking. Additionally, users can compare their performance to other users and find new routes to explore. Strava is available on iOS, Android, and Apple Watch.</p>

<h2>How to Connect Strava to Apple Watch</h2>

<p>Connecting Strava to an Apple Watch is a simple process. First, open the Strava app on your Apple Watch. Next, select the “Settings” tab, and select “Connect to Device.” From there, select the Apple Watch, and follow the instructions to connect the device. Once connected, you can begin using the Strava app on your Apple Watch. You can track your activities, view your performance data, and compare your performance to other users.</p>

<h2>What Can You Do with Strava on Apple Watch?</h2>

<p>Once connected, you can use the Strava app on your Apple Watch to track a variety of activities. With the app, you can record your runs, rides, and other activities. Additionally, you can view your performance data in real-time on the watch face. You can also compare your performance to other users, and find new routes to explore. Finally, you can use Strava’s leaderboard feature to compare your performance to other users.</p>

<p>If you are having trouble connecting Strava to your Apple Watch, there are a few steps you can take to troubleshoot the issue. First, make sure that the Apple Watch is paired to your iPhone.
