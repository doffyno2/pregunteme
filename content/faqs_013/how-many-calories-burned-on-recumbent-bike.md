<h2>What is a Recumbent Bike?</h2>

<p>A recumbent bike is a type of exercise bike that allows you to sit in a reclined position while riding. The seat on a recumbent bike is larger and more comfortable than the seat on an upright bike, and the handlebars are positioned closer to the body. This allows you to work out in a more relaxed position, putting less strain on your lower back and reducing the risk of injury.</p>
