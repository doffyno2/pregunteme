<h2>Types of Clownfish</h2>

<p>There are numerous species of clownfish available in the aquarium trade, and they can range in price from a few dollars to hundreds of dollars. Rarer species, such as the Tomato clownfish (Amphiprion frenatus) and the Clarkii clownfish (Amphiprion clarkii), can cost upwards of $50.</p>
