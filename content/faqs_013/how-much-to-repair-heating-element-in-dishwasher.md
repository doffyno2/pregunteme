<h2>What Is A Heating Element?</h2>

<p>A dishwasher's heating element is a metal coil that is responsible for heating the water in the dishwasher. The heating element is located inside the dishwasher and is usually located near the bottom of the dishwasher. It is connected to the water supply line and is responsible for heating the water to the right temperature for cleaning dishes. </p>

<p>There are several signs that indicate that the heating element may need to be replaced. If your dishes are not getting clean, it could be a sign that the heating element is not working properly. Additionally, if your dishwasher is taking longer than usual to complete a cycle, this could be a sign that the heating element needs to be replaced. </p>
