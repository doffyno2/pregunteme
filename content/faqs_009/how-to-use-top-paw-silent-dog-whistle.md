<h2>What Is a Silent Dog Whistle?</h2>

<p>A silent dog whistle is a device that produces a sound at a frequency that most humans can’t hear. While the sound may be silent to us, it’s loud and clear to our canine friends. Dog whistles are a great way to communicate with your pup without having to shout commands.</p>

<h2>How Does a Silent Dog Whistle Work?</h2>

<p>A silent dog whistle works by emitting a high-pitched sound that is inaudible to the human ear. The sound produced is usually in the range of 22 to 28 kilohertz, which is too high for the human ear to detect. When the whistle is blown, the sound travels long distances, making it ideal for training your pup from a distance. </p>
