<p>When the maintenance light comes on in your 2019 Toyota RAV4, it's a sign that you need to have your vehicle serviced. <h2>How to Reset the Maintenance Light on a 2019 Toyota RAV4</h2>

<p>The process for resetting the maintenance light on a 2019 Toyota RAV4 is relatively simple. All you need to do is follow these steps:</p>

<ul>
  <li>Turn the ignition to the “On” position but don’t start the engine.</li>
  <li>Press and hold the “Trip” button until the “Maintenance Required” warning light comes on.</li>
  <li>Press and hold the “Trip” button again until the “Maintenance Required” warning light goes off. This will reset the maintenance light.</li>
</ul>

<p>Once you have completed these steps, your maintenance light will be reset and you can continue driving your 2019 Toyota RAV4. It's important to remember, however, that the light will come on again when it's time to take your car in for service.</p>
