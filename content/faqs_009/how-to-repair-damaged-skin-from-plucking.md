Plucking is a form of hair removal that has been around for centuries. It is a popular method of removing unwanted facial and body hair, as it is relatively easy and inexpensive. It is often used as an alternative to waxing or shaving, as it is less painful and can be done in the comfort of one's own home. However, it can also cause damage to the skin if not done properly.

Therefore, it is important to take care when plucking to ensure that it is done correctly.
