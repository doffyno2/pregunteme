<h2>What Are the Different Types of Mail?</h2>

<p>There are several types of mail, including first-class mail, priority mail, express mail, certified mail, and registered mail. First-class mail is the most commonly used type of mail and typically takes the longest to arrive. Priority mail is faster than first-class mail and is typically used for more urgent deliveries. Express mail is the fastest type of mail and is typically used for time-sensitive items. Certified mail requires a signature upon delivery and is typically used for legal documents. Registered mail is the most secure type of mail and is usually used for items of value.</p>
