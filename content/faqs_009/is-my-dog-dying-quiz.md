It’s important to be aware of the signs and symptoms of a sick dog so that you can act quickly if you’re concerned about your pup’s health. Common signs of a sick dog include: 

<ul>

	<li>A decrease in appetite</li>
	<li>Weight loss</li>
	<li>Vomiting or diarrhea</li>
	<li>Coughing or difficulty breathing</li>
	<li>Changes in behavior or temperament</li>
	<li>Limping or favoring one limb over another</li>

</ul>
