<h2>What is a Razor Scooter?</h2>

A Razor scooter is a small, two-wheeled vehicle that is powered by a rider’s feet. It is commonly used as an alternative form of transportation, as well as a recreational activity. The scooter has a handlebar and a metal frame that is connected to two small wheels. The rider stands on the metal frame and pushes off with their feet in order to move forward. Razor scooters come in a variety of sizes, colors, and styles. 

<h2>Why is Knowing the Weight Limit Important?</h2>

Knowing the weight limit on a scooter is important for safety reasons. If the rider is too heavy for the scooter, it can cause the scooter to be unstable and difficult to control. This can lead to falls and serious injury. Additionally, if the scooter is overloaded, it can cause the frame and wheels to break, which could result in an accident.
