The production costs of bottled water are considerably higher than those of soda. This is because a much more complex and expensive process is involved in producing bottled water than in producing soda.

Finally, bottled water producers must also package their product in plastic bottles or other containers. This process involves the purchase of materials, machinery, and labor, all of which add to the cost of production.
