<p>Angel eyes, also known as weeping eyes, are a special type of eye drops used to help dogs with ocular discharge and other eye problems. While this type of eye drop can be purchased from a veterinarian, some pet owners prefer to make their own angel eyes at home. While making your own angel eyes is not as easy as buying them from a store, it is a cost-effective way to keep your pet's eyes healthy. In this article, we'll show you how to make angel eyes for dogs at home.</p>

<h2>What Are Angel Eyes?</h2>

<p>Angel eyes are a type of eye drops made from a combination of ingredients including boric acid, tea tree oil, and witch hazel. This combination helps to reduce inflammation and irritation in the eyes, as well as to help remove any discharge from the eyes. Angel eyes are typically used to treat dogs with ocular discharge, allergies, or other eye problems. </p>

<h2>Ingredients for Making Angel Eyes</h2>

<p>Making angel eyes at home requires a few simple ingredients. You'll need:</p>

<ul>
  <li>Boric acid</li>
  <li>Tea tree oil</li>
  <li>Witch hazel</li>
  <li>Distilled water</li>
  <li>Eye dropper</li>
</ul>

<h2>Instructions for Making Angel Eyes</h2>

<p>Making angel eyes is a simple process. Here are the steps:</p>

<ol>
  <li>Mix one teaspoon of boric acid with two tablespoons of distilled water in a small bowl or cup.</li>
  <li>Add two drops of tea tree oil and one tablespoon of witch hazel to the mixture.</li>
  <li>Stir the mixture until the ingredients are fully combined.</li>
  <li>Transfer the mixture to an eye dropper.</li>
  <li>Administer the angel eyes to your dog's eyes, one eye at a time.</li>
  <li>Allow your dog to blink a few times to help spread the solution in their eyes.</li>
  <li>Discard any remaining solution after use.</li>
</ol>
