<h2>What is an I-94 Form?</h2>

An I-94 form is a United States Department of Homeland Security form used for international travel. It is required for any non-U.S. citizen who wishes to enter or remain in the United States for any period of time. The I-94 form is also used to document the individual’s departure from the United States, and is an important document for immigration and other purposes.
