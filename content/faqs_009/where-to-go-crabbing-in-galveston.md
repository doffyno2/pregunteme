<h2>What is Crabbing?</h2>

Crabbing is a popular recreational activity that involves catching crabs with a net or fishing line. It's a great way to spend a day outdoors and can be a fun activity for the whole family.

The best places to go crabbing in Galveston are the shorelines, jetties, piers, and boat launches. The jetties are also a great spot for crabbing, as the waters here are usually teeming with crabs. The piers are another popular spot for crabbing, as they offer plenty of crabbing opportunities as well as a stunning view of the bay. Lastly, the boat launches are a great spot to go crabbing, as they offer easy access to the open waters.

<h2>What You Need for Crabbing</h2>

Crabbing requires some basic equipment to be successful. You will need a crab line, a net, bait, and a container to put your catch in.
