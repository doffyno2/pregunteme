<h2>What is a Ferrule and Stop?</h2>

A ferrule and stop is a two-part device that connects electrical cables and assemblies. The ferrule is a round tube, typically made of brass, with a hole in the center. The stop is a plastic tube that fits over the ferrule, and locks it in place. This creates a secure connection between the two parts.

<h2>Why Use a Ferrule and Stop?</h2>

Ferrules and stops are a great choice for any situation where you need to secure a connection between electrical cables or assemblies. They provide a reliable seal that prevents moisture and other contaminants from entering the connection. They also provide a secure grip that won't slip or come apart, ensuring your connections remain safe and secure.

<h2>How to Install a Ferrule and Stop</h2>

Installing a ferrule and stop is easy. First, you'll need to slide the ferrule onto the end of the cable or assembly. Make sure the ferrule is pushed all the way onto the end. Then, slide the stop over the ferrule and push it down until it's flush with the end of the cable or assembly. This will ensure the ferrule and stop are securely fastened.

<h2>Tools for Installing a Ferrule and Stop</h2>

In most cases, you won't need any special tools for installing a ferrule and stop. However, in some cases, you may need to use a crimping tool to ensure the ferrule and stop are securely fastened.
