Business Select Southwest is designed for frequent business travelers who are looking for an easy way to earn rewards and save money on their business trips.

<h2>How Does Business Select Southwest Work?</h2>

Business Select Southwest is a tiered rewards program. When you choose Business Select Southwest, you'll be assigned a tier based on the number of Rapid Rewards points you accumulate. The higher your tier, the more rewards and benefits you'll receive.

At the entry level, you'll receive priority boarding, which ensures that you get on the plane first. You'll also get an extra 15 Rapid Rewards points per eligible dollar spent on your Southwest Airlines ticket. 

As you move up the tiers, you'll receive additional benefits such as access to exclusive discounts, free upgrades to Business Select, and free checked bags.
