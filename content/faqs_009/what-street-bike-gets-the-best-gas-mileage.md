<h3>Check Your Tires</h3>

<p>Making sure your tires are properly inflated can help improve your bike's fuel efficiency. Underinflated tires can increase rolling resistance, which can cause your engine to work harder and consume more fuel. So make sure to check your tires regularly and keep them properly inflated.</p>
