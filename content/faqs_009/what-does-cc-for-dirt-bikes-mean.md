The engine displacement is a measure of the total volume of air and fuel mixture that is drawn into all cylinders of the engine during one operating cycle. In other words, the higher the CC, the bigger the engine. </p>

The bore is the diameter of the cylinder, while the stroke is the distance between the top and bottom of the cylinder.
