<h2>What Causes White Spots On Hot Dogs?</h2>

The white spots that can sometimes appear on hot dogs are caused by a process known as “albumenization”. Albumenization occurs when proteins from the meat used to make the hot dogs become exposed to high temperatures. When this happens, the proteins start to coagulate and form small white spots on the surface of the hot dog.
