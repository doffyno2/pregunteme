<h2>What Does the Blue Flashing Light on a GoPro Mean?</h2>

The blue flashing light on a GoPro camera indicates that your device is in the pairing mode. This means that you are able to connect other devices to your GoPro, such as a remote, mobile device, or another device that is compatible with the GoPro. When the blue flashing light is on, it is ready to be paired with another device.

<h2>How Do I Connect My GoPro to Another Device?</h2>

Connecting your GoPro to another device is easy. All you need to do is make sure that the blue flashing light is on and that the device you want to connect is within range. Then, open the device’s Bluetooth settings and search for your GoPro. Once it appears, select it and enter the pairing code that appears on your GoPro’s display. After entering the pairing code, your device should now be connected to your GoPro.
