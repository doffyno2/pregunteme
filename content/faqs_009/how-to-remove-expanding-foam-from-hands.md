<h2>Using Soap and Water</h2>

The simplest and most effective way to remove expanding foam from your hands is to use soap and water. Start by washing your hands with a gentle soap and warm water. This will help dissolve some of the foam and make it easier to remove. After washing your hands, use a soft brush to gently scrub away any remaining foam. If you don't have a brush, you can use a cloth or paper towel.

<h2>Using Rubbing Alcohol</h2>

If soap and water don't do the trick, you can try using rubbing alcohol. Rubbing alcohol helps to break down the expanding foam, making it easier to remove. Dip a cotton ball in rubbing alcohol and use it to gently scrub away the foam. It's important to use a gentle scrubbing motion so as not to damage your skin.

<h2>Using Solvent Cleaners</h2>
