<h2>What is a 125cc Motorcycle?</h2>

<p>A 125cc motorcycle is a relatively small bike with a 125cc engine. <h2>What is a 150cc Motorcycle?</h2>

<p>A 150cc motorcycle is a larger, more powerful bike. They’re also more expensive than 125cc bikes, so they’re not as budget-friendly. They’re great for those who want to go on longer rides, or those who need more power for challenging terrain.</p>
