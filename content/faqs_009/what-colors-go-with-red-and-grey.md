<h2>Colors That Go With Red and Grey</h2>

When choosing colors to go with red and grey, the key is to choose colors that either contrast or complement the two colors. Colors that contrast will make the design more dynamic, while colors that complement will create a more tonal, harmonious look. 

<h3>Colors That Contrast With Red and Grey</h3>

Colors that contrast with red and grey might include shades of blues, greens, purples, and yellows. These colors will provide a pop of color to the design, and can be used to draw attention to certain features. <h3>Colors That Complement Red and Grey</h3>

Colors that complement red and grey might include shades of pinks, browns, and oranges. These colors will create a harmonious look, and can be used to add warmth to a design. For example, if you want to create a cozy look, you might use shades of pink and brown.
