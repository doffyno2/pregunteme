<h3>Step 2: Fold the Sleeve in Half</h3>

Once the cuffs are unbuttoned, you can fold the sleeve in half. This will help to create a neat and uniform roll. 

<h3>Step 3: Roll Up the Sleeve</h3>

Once the sleeve is folded in half, you can begin to roll it up. Start at the cuff and work your way up, making sure to keep the roll tight and even. 

<h3>Step 4: Secure the Roll</h3>

Once the sleeve is rolled up, you can secure it by buttoning the cuff. This will keep the roll in place and prevent the sleeve from unrolling.
