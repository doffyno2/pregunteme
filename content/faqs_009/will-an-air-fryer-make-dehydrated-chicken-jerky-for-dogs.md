<h2>What Is an Air Fryer?</h2>

<p>An air fryer is a kitchen appliance that cooks food using hot air instead of oil or fat. It works by circulating hot air around the food, creating a crispy texture on the outside while keeping the inside juicy and tender. Air fryers are becoming increasingly popular because they enable you to cook food without the added fat and calories that come with deep-frying.</p>

<h2>What Do I Need to Make Chicken Jerky in an Air Fryer?</h2>

<p>To make chicken jerky in an air fryer, you’ll need chicken breasts, an air fryer, and a dehydrator. Thinly slice the chicken breasts into strips and place them in the air fryer basket. Set the temperature to 400 degrees Fahrenheit and cook for 10 minutes. Once the chicken is cooked, transfer it to the dehydrator and follow the instructions in the dehydrator manual.</p>

<h2>How Do I Store Homemade Chicken Jerky?</h2>

<p>Once your chicken jerky is finished, you’ll need to store it properly to ensure it stays fresh and safe for your pup to eat. Store it in an airtight container in a cool, dry place. Chicken jerky can last up to two weeks at room temperature and up to six weeks in the refrigerator.</p>
