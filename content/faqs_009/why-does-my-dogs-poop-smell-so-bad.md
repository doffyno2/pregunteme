<h2>Bacteria in the Gut</h2>

<p>The bacteria in a dog's gut plays a major role in the smell of their poop. Every dog's gut contains different types of bacteria that can produce different smells. Some of these bacteria produce a strong odor and some produce a more mild smell. </p>
