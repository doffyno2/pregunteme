<h2>Steps to Reset a Maintenance Reminder on Mazda CX-9</h2>

<p>To reset the maintenance reminder on your Mazda CX-9, follow these simple steps:</p>

<h3>Step 1: Turn on Your Mazda</h3>

<p>The first step is to turn on your Mazda CX-9. Make sure the engine is running and the vehicle is in park before you proceed.</p>

<h3>Step 2: Locate the Maintenance Reminder Button</h3>

<p>Once the vehicle is running, locate the maintenance reminder button. This button is usually located on the center console or dashboard, and it is typically labeled with the words "Maintenance Reminder" or "MR."</p>

<h3>Step 3: Push the Maintenance Reminder Button</h3>

<p>Once you have located the Maintenance Reminder button, press and hold it for approximately 10 seconds. You may hear a beeping sound as the maintenance reminder is reset.</p>

<h3>Step 4: Release the Maintenance Reminder Button</h3>

<p>Once the reminder has been reset, release the button and the maintenance reminder light should turn off. Your maintenance reminder is now reset, and you can go about your day without worrying about forgetting to take care of your car.</p>
