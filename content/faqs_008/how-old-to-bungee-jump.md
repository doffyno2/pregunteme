<h2>What Is Bungee Jumping?</h2>

Bungee jumping is an extreme sport that involves leaping from a high platform, usually a bridge, tower, or crane, while attached to a long elastic cord. The cord is specially designed to absorb the shock of the jump and slowly bring the jumper back to the ground.

<h2>The Age Requirement to Bungee Jump</h2>

The age requirement to bungee jump varies by country and by specific location. In the United States, most bungee jumping companies will not allow anyone under the age of 18 to participate in the activity. There are some exceptions, however, such as in California and Colorado, where people 16 and older are allowed to participate with parental consent.

In other countries, such as the United Kingdom, the minimum age is 14 and in Australia, the minimum age is 16. For those under the required age, some bungee jumping companies may offer a junior bungee program for children as young as 7 or 8 years old.
