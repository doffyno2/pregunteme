<h2>Types of Car Door Damage</h2>

<p>Car door repair costs can depend on the type of damage that needs to be repaired. Some of the most common types of door damage include dents, scratches, and rust. Dents can be caused by an accident or by an object coming into contact with the car door. Scratches can be caused by a key, a piece of gravel, or even a fingernail. Rust can occur over time due to exposure to the elements. Depending on the type of damage, the repair costs will vary.</p>

<p>The labor costs associated with repairing a car door can vary considerably. Some repair shops may charge by the hour, while others may have a flat fee for the job.
