<h2>What Is a Mucus Plug?</h2>

<p>The mucus plug, also known as a “bloody show”, is a thick plug of mucus that seals off the cervix during pregnancy. It is made up of mucus from the uterus and other secretions from the cervix. It forms a barrier to help protect the developing puppies from infection and other potential threats. The mucus plug may be lost in one large mass or in several smaller bits before labor. It can be clear, pink, brown, or even tinged with blood. When the plug is lost, it is usually an indication that labor is imminent.</p>
