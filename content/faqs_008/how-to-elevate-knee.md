<p>Elevating your knee is beneficial after an injury as it helps to reduce inflammation and swelling, as well as reduce pain. <p>There are several ways that you can elevate your knee to reduce swelling and inflammation. The most common way is to use a pillow or cushion to support your knee in a slightly elevated position. <p>When elevating your knee, it’s important to make sure that it’s not too high.
