<h2>How to Add Oil to a Mercury Outboard 2 Stroke</h2>

<p>Adding oil to your Mercury outboard 2 stroke is a relatively straightforward process. Here are the steps you should follow:</p>

  <li>Choose the right oil: Refer to your owner’s manual to determine the correct type and viscosity of oil for your motor. Use an oil that is TC-W3 or NMMA FC-W certified.</li>
  <li>Open the oil fill cap: Locate the oil fill cap and remove it. This is usually located on the top of the motor.</li>
  <li>Add the oil: Carefully pour the oil into the oil fill hole until it is at the recommended level on the dipstick.</li>
  <li>Replace the oil fill cap: Make sure the oil fill cap is securely replaced and tighten it if necessary.</li>
  <li>Check the oil level: Check the oil level again to make sure it is within the recommended range. If the oil level is too low, then you may need to add more oil.</li>
</ul>
