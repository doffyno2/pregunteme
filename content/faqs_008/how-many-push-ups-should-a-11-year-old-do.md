<h2>What Are Push Ups?</h2>

Push ups are a form of exercise that involves lifting your body up and down while keeping your arms straight. This exercise works the chest, arms, shoulders, and core muscles. It is a great way to build strength and stamina.

<h2>How Many Push Ups Should an 11 Year Old Do?</h2>

The number of push ups an 11 year old should do depends on their physical fitness level. If they are just starting out, they should do as many as they can without feeling pain or strain. A good starting point is 10-15 push ups. As they become stronger and more fit, they can gradually increase the number of push ups they do. 

For an 11 year old who is already physically fit, they should aim to do at least 30-50 push ups in a single session. This will help them build strength and stamina. It is important to note that the number of push ups an 11 year old can do will depend on their individual fitness level.

<h2>Tips For Doing Push Ups Properly</h2>

To get the most out of the exercise, it is important to do push ups properly. Here are some tips to help an 11 year old do push ups correctly:

<h3>Keep Your Body In Line</h3>

When doing push ups, make sure your body is in a straight line from your head to your toes. This will help ensure that you are using the correct muscles and getting the most out of the exercise.

<h3>Engage Your Core</h3>

When doing push ups, make sure to engage your core muscles. This will help you keep your body in the correct position and will help you get the most out of the exercise.

<h3>Go At Your Own Pace</h3>

It is important to go at your own pace when doing push ups. Make sure you are pushing yourself but not pushing too hard. If you are feeling any pain or discomfort, stop the exercise and take a break.

<h3>Take Breaks</h3>

When doing push ups, take breaks in between sets to allow your muscles to recover. This will help you get the most out of the exercise and will help prevent injury.
