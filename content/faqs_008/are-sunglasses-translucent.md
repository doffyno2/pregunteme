<h2> What is Translucency? </h2>

Translucency is the property of allowing light to pass through a material, but diffusing it or scattering it so that the image on the other side is not completely visible. Think of it like a window shade, where you can still see the light coming in, but you can’t make out the details of what’s on the other side.
