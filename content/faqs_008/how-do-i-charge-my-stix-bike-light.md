<h2>Charging Your Stix Bike Light with a USB Cable</h2>

<p>The Stix Bike Light can be charged with a USB cable. To charge it, simply plug one end of the USB cable into the back of your Stix Bike Light and the other end into a power source. You can plug the USB cable into a laptop, a USB wall adapter, or any other device that has a USB port. Make sure your power source is on, and after a few moments, you'll see the red LED light on the back of the Stix Bike Light start to flash. This means your light is charging.</p>

<p>Your Stix Bike Light will take about three hours to fully charge. When it's finished charging, the red LED light will turn off. Once you've finished charging your Stix Bike Light, unplug the USB cable and store it in a safe place. It's important to note that you should never leave your Stix Bike Light plugged in to a power source when not in use.</p>

<h2>Charging Your Stix Bike Light with a Solar Panel</h2>

<p>If you want to go green and not use up your energy resources, you can charge your Stix Bike Light with a solar panel. To do this, you'll need to purchase a compatible solar panel. The Stix Bike Light is designed to be used with any 5V solar panel with a USB port.</p>

<p>Once you have the solar panel, plug the USB end of the solar panel into the back of your Stix Bike Light. Place the solar panel in direct sunlight and make sure it's facing the right direction. You'll know the light is charging when the red LED light on the back of the Stix Bike Light starts to flash. Depending on the strength of the sunlight, it should take about two to four hours for your Stix Bike Light to fully charge.</p>

<p>When the light is finished charging, the red LED light will turn off. Unplug the USB cable from the back of the Stix Bike Light and store it in a safe place. As with charging with a USB cable, make sure you never leave your Stix Bike Light plugged into a power source when not in use.</p>
