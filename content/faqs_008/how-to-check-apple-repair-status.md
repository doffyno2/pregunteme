<h2>How to Check the Apple Repair Status of Your Device</h2>

<p>The easiest way to check the repair status of your device is to use the Apple Repair Status website. This website allows you to easily check the status of your repair without having to call the Apple Repair Center. To access the website, simply go to apple.com/repairstatus. From this website, you can enter your device’s serial number or your Apple ID, and you’ll be able to see the current status of your repair.</p>

<p>When you check the repair status of your device, you’ll see a few different statuses. Here’s what they mean:</p>

    <li><strong>Cancelled:</strong> Your repair has been cancelled by the Apple Repair Center.</li>
