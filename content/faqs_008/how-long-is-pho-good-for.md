<h2>How Long Does Pho Last?</h2>

When it comes to the shelf life of pho, it really depends on how it is stored. Generally, pho will last up to four days in the refrigerator, or up to three months in the freezer. It is important to note that the shelf life of pho can vary depending on the ingredients used and how it is prepared.

<h2>Storing Pho in the Refrigerator</h2>

When it comes to storing pho in the refrigerator, it is best to do it within two hours of cooking. If you do not plan on eating the pho within that time frame, it should be cooled down as quickly as possible and placed in an airtight container. This will help to preserve its flavor and texture for up to four days.

<h2>Storing Pho in the Freezer</h2>

If you plan on storing pho in the freezer, it is best to do it within two hours of cooking. Make sure that the pho is cooled down quickly and transferred to an airtight container or freezer bag. This will help to preserve its flavor and texture for up to three months. 

<h2>Reheating Pho</h2>

When it comes to reheating pho, it is best to do it in the microwave. Make sure that the pho is heated in a microwave-safe container and that it is heated thoroughly. It is important to note that pho can also be reheated on the stovetop, but this can cause it to become dry and lose its flavor.

<h2>Tips for Storing Pho</h2>
