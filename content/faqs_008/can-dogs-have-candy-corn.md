<h2>What Is Candy Corn?</h2>

Candy corn is a traditional American confection that has been around for over a century. It is made of sugar, corn syrup, fondant, and artificial colors and flavors. The candy is shaped like a kernel of corn and comes in a variety of colors. It is most often found during the Halloween season, when it is used to decorate pumpkins and other decorations.
