<h2>What Factors Determine the Value of an Antique Stove?</h2>

<p>When determining the value of an antique stove, there are a few key factors that must be taken into account. These include:</p>

<ul>
  <li><b>Age:</b> The age of the stove is one of the most important factors when determining its worth. Generally, the older the stove, the more valuable it is considered to be.</li>
  <li><b>Condition:</b> The condition of the stove is also important. If the stove is in excellent condition, it will be worth more than one that is in poor condition. </li>
  <li><b>Rarity:</b> Rarity is another factor that affects the value of an antique stove. If the stove is a rare model, or is in a unique style, it will be worth more than a more common model.</li>
  <li><b>Demand:</b> The demand for a particular model of antique stove can also affect its value. If the model is in high demand, it will be worth more than a model that is not in demand.</li>
 </ul>
