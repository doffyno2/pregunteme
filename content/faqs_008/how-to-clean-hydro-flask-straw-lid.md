<h2>What You Need to Clean Your Hydro Flask Straw Lid</h2>
<p>The materials you need to clean your Hydro Flask straw lid are simple and easy to find. You will need a bottle brush, a soft cloth, a mild dish soap, and a few tablespoons of white vinegar. <h2>How to Clean Your Hydro Flask Straw Lid</h2>
<p>Before you start, make sure the lid is completely dry. Then, start by using the bottle brush to scrub away any visible grime or debris. Swish it around to mix the soap and water and then let it sit for a few minutes. After a few minutes, rinse the lid with warm water and use the bottle brush to scrub away any remaining dirt or grime.
