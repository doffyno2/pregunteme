<h2>What is the Speed of Sound?</h2>

<p>The speed of sound is the rate at which sound waves travel through a medium, such as air or water. It is measured in units of distance over time, such as meters per second (m/s). The speed of sound is not constant, however; it can vary depending on the properties of the medium it is traveling through. For instance, the speed of sound is faster in denser media, such as water, than it is in less dense media, such as air.</p>
