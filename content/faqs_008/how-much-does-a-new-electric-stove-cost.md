<h2>Types of Electric Stoves</h2>

<p>There are three main types of electric stoves, each with its own price tag. The three types of electric stoves are:</p>

<ul>
    <li><strong>Drop-in Electric Stove:</strong> Drop-in electric stoves are the most affordable type of electric stove, costing an average of $400 to $600. These stoves are typically the least efficient type, but they are the most convenient to install and use.</li>
    <li><strong>Slide-in Electric Stove:</strong> Slide-in electric stoves cost an average of $1,000 to $2,500. These stoves are more efficient than drop-in models, but they require more installation work and are more difficult to maintain.</li>
    <li><strong>Countertop Electric Stove:</strong> Countertop electric stoves cost an average of $200 to $400. These stoves are the most efficient type, but they are not as durable as the other two types.</li>
</ul>

<h2>Features to Consider</h2>

<p>When selecting an electric stove, you should also consider the features you want. The most common features include:</p>

<ul>
    <li><strong>Size:</strong> Electric stoves come in a variety of sizes, ranging from small, single-burner stoves to full-size stoves with six burners. The size of the stove you choose will affect the price.</li>
    <li><strong>Heating Elements:</strong> Different electric stoves have different numbers and types of heating elements. Some stoves have a single heating element, while others have multiple elements. The more elements a stove has, the more expensive it will be.</li>
    <li><strong>Timer:</strong> Many electric stoves come with a timer that automatically turns the stove off after a specified amount of time. This feature is convenient for busy households, but it will also add to the cost of the stove.</li>
    <li><strong>Temperature Settings:</strong> Some electric stoves come with adjustable temperature settings that allow you to adjust the heat of the stove based on what you’re cooking. This feature is convenient, but it also adds to the cost.</li>
    <li><strong>Controls:</strong> Electric stoves come with either manual or digital controls. Manual controls are less expensive, but digital controls offer more precision and convenience.</li>
    <li><strong>Cleaning Features:</strong> Some electric stoves come with features that make cleaning easier. These features, such as self-cleaning ovens, are more expensive but can save you time and energy.</li>
    <li><strong>Surface Material:</strong> Electric stoves come in a variety of surface materials, such as stainless steel, porcelain, and ceramic. The surface material you choose will affect the cost of the stove.</li>
</ul>
