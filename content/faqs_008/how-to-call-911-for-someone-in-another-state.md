<h2>What Is 911?</h2>

911 is the national emergency number for the United States. It is a three-digit number that is used to contact emergency services such as police, fire, and medical services. The number was established in 1968 and is now one of the most important numbers to know in an emergency. 

<h2>Can You Call 911 for Someone in Another State?</h2>

Yes, you can call 911 for someone in another state. However, it is important to remember that each state has its own 911 system, and you must call the number for the state where the person is located. For example, if the person is in California, you would need to call the California 911 number, not the number for your own state.
