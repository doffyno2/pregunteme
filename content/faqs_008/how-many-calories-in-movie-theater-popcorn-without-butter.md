<h2>Types of Movie Theater Popcorn</h2>

<p>Popcorn is typically divided into two main types: air-popped and oil-popped. Air-popped popcorn is made by blowing hot air through the kernels, while oil-popped popcorn is made by heating oil and kernels in a special machine. </p>

<p>Movie theater popcorn is usually oil-popped, as this method of popping yields a larger, fluffier popcorn. Oil-popped popcorn also tends to have a better flavor, as the oil helps to bring out the natural sweetness of the kernels. </p>
