<h2>Types of Holes on a Shirt</h2>

The most common type of hole found on a shirt is the buttonhole. Buttonholes are usually located near the collar, cuffs, and plackets of the shirt. The number of buttonholes on a shirt can vary depending on the type of shirt, but most shirts have at least four.

Another type of hole found on a shirt is the eyelet. Eyelets are small holes located in the fabric of the shirt. They are usually used for threading a drawstring or lace through.
