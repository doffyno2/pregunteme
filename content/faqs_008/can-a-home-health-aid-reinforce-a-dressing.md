<h2>What is a Dressing?</h2>

<p>A dressing is any type of material that is placed over a wound in order to protect it. Dressings can be made of several different materials, including gauze, foam, or other materials that are designed to absorb fluids and help protect the wound from further damage or infection. Dressings are usually held in place by a bandage or other type of wrap.</p>
