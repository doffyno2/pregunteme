<p>The transmission is also an important factor in determining a vehicle's top speed. The transmission plays a major role in how fast the vehicle can go, as it helps to efficiently transfer power from the engine to the wheels. With the right transmission, a smart car can reach speeds of up to 130 mph.</p>

<p>Finally, the tires play an important role in a vehicle's top speed. The standard tires on a smart car are all-season tires, which are designed to provide good traction in all weather conditions.
