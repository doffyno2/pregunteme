<p>The first step to visiting your friends camp in Pokemon Sword is to open up the Y-Comm menu. To do this, press the “+” button on your Switch to open the home menu, then select the “Y-Comm” icon. <p>Once you’ve opened the Y-Comm menu, you’ll need to access the friend list. To do this, select the “Friends” option in the Y-Comm menu. This will open up a list of all your friends who are currently playing Pokemon Sword. You can also add new friends from this list if you’d like.</p>

<h2>Step 3: Select a Friend and Connect</h2>

<p>Once you’ve opened the friend list, select the friend you’d like to visit and press the “A” button. This will open up a pop-up window which will allow you to connect with your friend. Once you’ve connected with your friend, you’ll be given the option to visit their camp.</p>

<h2>Step 4: Visit the Camp</h2>

<p>Once you’ve connected with your friend, select the “Visit Camp” option in the pop-up window. This will take you to your friend’s camp, allowing you to explore and interact with their Pokemon. You can also trade Pokemon and items with your friend while you’re at the camp.</p>

<h2>Step 5: Leave the Camp</h2>

<p>When you’re done visiting your friend’s camp, select the “Leave Camp” option in the pop-up window. This will take you back to the Y-Comm menu, where you can disconnect from your friend and return to the game.</p>
