<h2>What Is the Difference Between Fish and Other Animals?</h2>

The main difference between fish and other animals is that fish are aquatic and breathe through gills, while other animals require lungs to breathe. Fish have gills which extract oxygen from the water, while other animals such as mammals, reptiles and birds have lungs which extract oxygen from the air.
