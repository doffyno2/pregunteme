<h2>Types of Gravel for a 75 Gallon Fish Tank</h2>

Gravel comes in a variety of shapes, sizes, and colors. The type of gravel you choose for your 75 gallon fish tank will depend on the type of fish you plan to keep. For example, if you plan to keep cichlids, then you’ll likely want to choose a gravel that is coarse and has sharp edges, as this will provide the fish with a secure footing and help reduce stress. On the other hand, if you plan to keep a community tank, then you’ll want to choose a smoother, finer gravel.

It’s also important to consider the color of the gravel you choose. For example, if you plan to keep live plants, then you’ll want to choose a gravel that is light colored, as this will reflect more light into the tank and help the plants grow. On the other hand, if you plan to keep fish that like to dig, such as goldfish, then you’ll want to choose a darker gravel, as this will help camouflage any debris that your fish may dig up.
