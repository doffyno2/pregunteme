<h2>What Are the Different Types of Fishing Line?</h2>

<p>Fishing line comes in many different types. Each type of line is designed for different fishing scenarios. The most common types of fishing line are monofilament, fluorocarbon, and braided line. Monofilament line is the most popular and least expensive type of fishing line. It's made from a single strand of nylon and is great for a variety of fishing techniques. Fluorocarbon line is more expensive than monofilament, but is more invisible to fish and is more abrasion-resistant. Braided line is the most durable type of fishing line and is popular for techniques that involve heavy weights and lures. </p>
