<h2>What You Will Need</h2>

<p>To begin, you will need a few items to clean your Marc by Marc Jacobs canvas backpack. You will need a soft-bristled brush, a mild cleaning solution, a lint roller, and a damp cloth. Make sure that the cleaning solution is designed specifically for use on canvas. You will also need a washing machine and a dryer.</p>

<h2>Prepping Your Backpack</h2>

<p>Before you begin washing your Marc by Marc Jacobs canvas backpack, it is important to prep the bag. Take the time to check over your bag and remove any dirt, dust, and debris that may have accumulated on the surface of the bag. Use a soft-bristled brush to remove any dirt particles. Pay special attention to the seams and pockets of the bag, as these areas can accumulate dirt more easily.</p>

<p>Once you have removed all of the dirt and debris, use a lint roller to remove any lingering particles. If there are any stains on your bag, you can use a damp cloth and a mild cleaning solution to remove them. Be sure to read the label of the cleaning solution to make sure that it is safe to use on your bag.</p>

<h2>Washing Your Backpack</h2>

<p>Once your bag is prepped, it is time to wash it. Place your bag in the washing machine with a mild detergent. Set the machine to a gentle cycle and use warm water. Do not use hot water, as this can damage the canvas. Once the cycle is complete, remove your bag from the machine and place it in the dryer. Set the dryer to a gentle cycle and use low heat. Once the cycle is complete, remove your bag from the dryer.</p>

<h2>Caring for Your Backpack</h2>

<p>Once your Marc by Marc Jacobs canvas backpack has been washed and dried, it is important to take steps to keep it looking its best. Store your bag away from direct sunlight, as this can cause fading. If your bag gets wet, be sure to dry it thoroughly before storing it away. Do not use harsh chemicals or abrasive cleaners on your bag, as this can damage the canvas.</p>
