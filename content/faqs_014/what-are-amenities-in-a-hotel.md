<p>When traveling or looking for a place to stay, amenities are the features that make a hotel stand out. Amenities can be anything from a complimentary breakfast to a fitness center to a spa or even a concierge service. Hotels offer amenities to attract guests, make them feel comfortable and provide them with additional services that enhance their experience. It's important to be aware of the amenities offered by a hotel before booking so that you know what to expect. </p>

<h2>What Amenities Are Common in Hotels?</h2>

<p>The amenities that are offered in hotels vary from one hotel to the next, but some of the more common amenities include:</p>

<ul>
    <li><b>Free Wi-Fi:</b> Most hotels offer complimentary Wi-Fi service for guests. Some hotels also offer business centers with complimentary Wi-Fi.</li>
    <li><b>Complimentary Breakfast:</b> Many hotels offer complimentary breakfast for guests. This can range from a continental breakfast to a full breakfast buffet.</li>
    <li><b>Fitness Centers:</b> A fitness center is a great way for guests to stay active while on vacation. Some hotels offer access to a fitness center with a variety of exercise equipment.</li>
    <li><b>Restaurants:</b> Some hotels offer on-site restaurants that serve breakfast, lunch and dinner. This is a great way to save time and money while still enjoying a meal out.</li>
    <li><b>Concierge Service:</b> Many hotels offer concierge services that can help guests with local attractions, directions and other needs.</li>
    <li><b>Spa Services:</b> Spa services can range from massage and facial treatments to saunas and steam rooms. Some hotels offer these services on-site. </li>
</ul>

<h2>Are There Any Amenities That Are Not Commonly Offered?</h2>

<p>Yes, there are amenities that are not commonly offered, but they may be available at some hotels. Some of the more unusual amenities that you may find include:</p>

<ul>
    <li><b>Pet-Friendly Rooms:</b> Some hotels offer rooms that are pet-friendly. This means that you can bring your pet with you when you stay at the hotel.</li>
    <li><b>Child Care Services:</b> Some hotels offer child care services for guests. This can include babysitting services or even supervised activities for children.</li>
    <li><b>Laundry and Dry Cleaning Services:</b> Many hotels offer laundry and dry cleaning services on-site. This is a great way to save time while traveling.</li>
    <li><b>Room Service:</b> Room service is a great way to enjoy a meal in the comfort of your own room. Some hotels offer room service for breakfast, lunch and dinner.</li>
    <li><b>Airport Shuttle Services:</b> Some hotels offer shuttle services to and from the airport. This can save you time and money when traveling.</li>
    <li><b>Valet Parking:</b> Valet parking is a great way to save time when coming and going from the hotel. Valet parking is offered at some hotels.</li>
</ul>
