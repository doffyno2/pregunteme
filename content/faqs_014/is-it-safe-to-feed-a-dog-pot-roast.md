<h2>What is Pot Roast?</h2>

<p>Pot roast is a slow-cooked cut of beef that has been braised in liquid. It typically cooks for several hours, and can be cooked in a variety of liquids, such as stock, water, or beer. Pot roast is usually served with vegetables, such as potatoes, carrots, and onions. Pot roast is a popular dish because it is both hearty and flavorful. </p>

<h2>Is Pot Roast Safe for Dogs?</h2>
