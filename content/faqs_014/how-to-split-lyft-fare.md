<h2>How to Split the Fare Before You Get in the Car</h2>

<p>The easiest way to split the Lyft fare is to get your friends or family members to join the ride before you get in the car. Once everyone has requested the ride, you can easily split the fare between all the riders. Here’s how to do it:</p>

<ul>
  <li>Open the Lyft app and select your ride.</li>
  <li>Tap the “Split Fare” button in the upper right corner of the screen.</li>
  <li>Add your friends or family members to the ride.</li>
  <li>Tap “Split Fare” again to confirm your selection.</li>
</ul>

<p>Once you’ve completed these steps, your friends or family members will be added to the ride and the fare will be evenly split between all the riders. You can also choose to split the fare unevenly if you wish. For example, if one person is paying for the entire ride, you can select that person as the “Primary Rider” and the fare will be automatically adjusted.</p>
