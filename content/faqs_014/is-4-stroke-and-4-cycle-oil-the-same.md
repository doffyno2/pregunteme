Is 4 Stroke and 4 Cycle Oil the Same?

When it comes to motor oil, there is a lot of confusion about the differences between 4 stroke and 4 cycle oil. What is 4 Stroke Oil?

4 stroke oil is a lubricant specifically designed for use in 4 stroke engines. It is a relatively thin oil that is designed to flow quickly and easily through the engine, providing lubrication and protection from wear and tear. 4 stroke oil is designed to reduce friction between the engine's moving parts, while also providing protection against corrosion and rust. 

What is 4 Cycle Oil?

4 cycle oil is a lubricant specifically designed for use in 4 cycle engines. Unlike 4 stroke oil, it is a much thicker oil that is designed to provide a much higher level of protection. 4 cycle oil is designed to reduce the wear and tear on the engine, while also providing protection against corrosion and rust.
