<h2>What Are the Different Types of Worms in Dogs?</h2>

<p>Worms are parasites that live inside the digestive system of a dog or other animal. The most common types of worms found in dogs are roundworms, hookworms, whipworms, and tapeworms. Roundworms are the most common type of worm, and they can be white or yellowish in color. Hookworms are small, thin worms that attach to the intestinal wall of a dog, and whipworms are small, thread-like worms that can cause severe diarrhea. Tapeworms are long, flat worms that attach to the intestines of a dog and can be seen in the stool of an infected pet. </p>
