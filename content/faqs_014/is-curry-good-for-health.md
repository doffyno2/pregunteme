<h2>What Is Curry?</h2>

<p>Curry is a blend of spices, herbs, and vegetables that is typically used in Indian, Southeast Asian, and Middle Eastern cuisine. Curry can be served as a dry dish or as a sauce. The spices and vegetables used in curry can provide a variety of health benefits.</p>

<h3>Instructions</h3>
