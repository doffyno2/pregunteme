For example, bright lime green looks great with navy blue and white, while muted lime green looks great with gray and pastel pink. Next, consider the tone of the room in which you will use the lime green. For a bright, airy room, choose colors that are lighter and brighter, such as white and pastel pink. For a darker, more intimate room, choose colors that are darker and more muted, such as navy blue and gray.</p>
