<h2>How to Measure Golf Driver Length</h2>

Measuring the length of your golf driver is a simple process. All you need is a tape measure and a few minutes of your time.

<h3>Step 1: Find the End of the Grip</h3>

The first step in measuring your driver is to find the end of the grip. The grip is the section of the club that you hold onto in order to swing the club. The end of the grip is usually located at the end of the club, near the head.

<h3>Step 2: Measure from the End of the Grip</h3>

Once you have located the end of the grip, you can then measure from there. Using a tape measure, measure from the end of the grip to the end of the club head. This is the total length of your golf driver.

<h3>Step 3: Record the Length</h3>

Once you have measured the length of your driver, it is important to record the length. This will help you find clubs that are the right size for your swing and help you dial in the correct launch angle and spin rate for your shots.
