<h2>What You Need to Turn On a Peloton Bike Screen</h2>

The Peloton bike is powered by a rechargeable battery, so there’s no need to plug it into an outlet. All you need to turn on the bike’s screen is the power button located on the side of the bike’s console. Make sure the bike is fully charged before you attempt to turn on the screen. 

<h2>How to Turn on the Peloton Bike Screen</h2>

Once you’ve located the power button, press and hold it for two seconds to turn on the bike’s screen. You should see a blue light appear to indicate that the bike is turned on. You can then use the touchscreen to access the Peloton library of classes, music, and other features.

<h3>Adjust the Screen Brightness</h3>
