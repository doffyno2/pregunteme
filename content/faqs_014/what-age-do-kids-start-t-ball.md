<h2>What Is T Ball?</h2>

T ball is a sport that is based on the game of baseball but adapted for younger children. It is played with a soft, foam ball and a slightly smaller bat that is easier for kids to handle. The bases are also not as far apart as in baseball, which makes it easier for the players to run around the diamond.

<h2>Benefits of T Ball</h2>

T ball is a great way to introduce kids to the basics of team sports. The rules are simple enough that even young children can understand them, and the game is designed to be fun and entertaining. Kids also learn to work together as a team, practice their coordination, and develop important physical skills. 

<h2>What Age Do Kids Start T Ball?</h2>

Most kids start playing T ball between the ages of 4 and 7. This is the optimal age for kids to start learning the basics of the game, as they can still focus on the fundamentals and have fun while doing so.

<h2>What Skills Do My Child Need to Play T Ball?</h2>

Kids don't need to have any special skills to play T ball. Basic coordination and an understanding of the rules of the game are all that's required.
