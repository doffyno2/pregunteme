<h2>What is Rice Cereal?</h2>

<p>Rice cereal is a processed food made from rice flour and other ingredients, like sugar and salt. It is usually sold in boxes, and is usually served as a breakfast cereal, either dry or with milk. It is also often used as an ingredient in processed foods, such as pet food.</p>

<h2>Why is Rice Cereal Bad for Dogs?</h2>

<p>Rice cereal is not an ideal food for your dog for several reasons. First, it is highly processed, meaning that many of the beneficial nutrients and vitamins have been removed. Finally, rice cereal is also high in sodium, which can lead to dehydration and other health problems.</p>
