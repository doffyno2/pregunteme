<h2>How to Tie a Wrap Dress</h2>
<p>Tying a wrap dress is a relatively simple process. Start by placing the wrap dress flat on a table or other flat surface. Take the top of the dress, and wrap it around your body, making sure to keep the wrap snug. Once you’ve wrapped the dress around your body, tie the ends of the wrap together at the waist, and adjust the fit as needed. You can also create a bow with the tie if you’d like to add a touch of femininity to your look.</p>
