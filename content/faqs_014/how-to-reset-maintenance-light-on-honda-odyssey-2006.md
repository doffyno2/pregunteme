<h2>What is the Maintenance Light?</h2>

<p>The maintenance light is a reminder to let owners know that the car needs to be serviced. This includes getting oil changes, tire rotations, and other routine maintenance tasks. The maintenance light is triggered when the car has driven a certain number of miles or when a certain amount of time has passed since the last service. When the maintenance light is on, it is important to get the car serviced as soon as possible.</p>

<h2>How to Reset the Maintenance Light on a Honda Odyssey 2006?</h2>

<p>Resetting the maintenance light on a Honda Odyssey 2006 is a quick and easy process. Here is a step-by-step guide for resetting the maintenance light on a Honda Odyssey 2006: </p>

<h3>Step 1: Locate the Reset Button </h3>

<p>The first step in resetting the maintenance light on a Honda Odyssey 2006 is to locate the reset button. The reset button is usually located on the dashboard near the steering wheel, or underneath the dashboard near the driver's side door. To locate the reset button, refer to the owner’s manual, or consult a qualified automotive technician.</p>

<h3>Step 2: Press and Hold the Reset Button </h3>

<p>Once the reset button has been located, press and hold it for several seconds. This will reset the maintenance light and turn it off. If the light does not turn off after a few seconds, try pressing and holding the button again.</p>

<h3>Step 3: Start the Engine </h3>

<p>After the reset button has been pressed and held for several seconds, start the engine and the maintenance light should be off. If the light is still on, try resetting the maintenance light again.</p>
