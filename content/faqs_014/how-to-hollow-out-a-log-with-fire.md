<p>The first step in hollowing out a log with fire is to gather the necessary materials. You will need a large log, a saw or axe, a shovel, a metal bucket, some kindling, and a large fire pit. <h2>Creating the Fire Pit</h2>

<p>Once you have all the materials ready, you can begin to create the fire pit. Dig a hole in the ground that is about two feet deep and two feet wide. <p>Once the fire is burning, you can start to burn the log. Start by burning the outside of the log.
