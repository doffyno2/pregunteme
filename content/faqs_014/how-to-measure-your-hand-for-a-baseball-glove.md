<p>The first step to finding the right size baseball glove for you is to measure your hand. To do this, you will need to use a measuring tape. Record the measurement in inches.</p>

<p>Next, measure the length of your hand. Start at the base of your palm and measure to the tip of your middle finger. Record this measurement in inches as well. Once you have these two measurements, you can then use them to determine the size of the glove that will fit you best.</p>

<h2>How to Break In Your Baseball Glove</h2>

<p>Once you have selected the perfect glove for your hand, you will need to break it in. Breaking in your glove is an important step in ensuring that it will provide you with the best performance. <p>Using a glove mallet to break in your glove is one of the most popular methods. You can also use glove oil to soften the leather and make it easier to break in.
