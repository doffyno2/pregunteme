<h2>What is Calcium Chloride?</h2>

Calcium chloride is an inorganic salt, commonly used in food and beverage applications to enhance flavor, texture, and shelf life. It is also used as a food preservative and a common ingredient in bottled water. It is a white, odorless, and tasteless powder.
