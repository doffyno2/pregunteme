<h2>How Often Should You Service Your 2018 Rogue?</h2>

<p>Your owner’s manual will provide you with an exact maintenance schedule, but in general, you should be scheduling regular service for the following components:</p>

<ul>
<li>Oil and filter change every 5,000-7,000 miles</li>
<li>Tire rotation and inspection every 7,500-10,000 miles</li>
<li>Brake inspection and replacement every 25,000-30,000 miles</li>
<li>Spark plug replacement every 50,000-60,000 miles</li>
<li>Transmission flush every 70,000-80,000 miles</li>
</ul>

<p>It’s a good idea to keep a log of all of your service appointments, as they will help you keep track of when you need to schedule regular maintenance. You should also check in with your Nissan dealer or mechanic to make sure that you’re following the proper maintenance schedule for your Rogue.</p>

<p>It’s important to keep an eye on the condition of these components, as they can have a major impact on the performance of your Rogue. If you notice any issues, it’s best to have them addressed as soon as possible.</p>
