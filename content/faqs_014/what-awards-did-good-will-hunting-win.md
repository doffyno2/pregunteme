<h2>What Is Good Will Hunting About?</h2>

Good Will Hunting tells the story of Will Hunting, a 20-year-old South Boston janitor with a genius-level IQ and an uncanny aptitude for mathematics. Will's life is a tumultuous one, as he struggles to come to terms with his past and his potential future. His friends are a tight-knit group of working-class South Boston men and his professor, Gerald Lambeau, is a prestigious mathematics professor at MIT. Lambeau recognizes Will's talent and offers him a deal - if Will can successfully answer a series of difficult mathematics problems, Lambeau will help him to develop his skills and apply to college. Will eventually accepts the challenge and begins the journey to becoming a successful mathematician.
