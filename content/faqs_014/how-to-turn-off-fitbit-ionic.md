<h2>How to Turn Off Fitbit Ionic</h2>

<p>1. Open the Fitbit app on your mobile device.</p>

<p>2. Select the “Account” tab in the app, and then select “Ionic”.</p>

<p>3. Select the “Settings” tab in the app.</p>

<p>4. Scroll down and find the “Power Off” option.</p>

<p>5. Select the “Power Off” option and then confirm that you want to turn off your Fitbit Ionic.</p>

<p>Your Fitbit Ionic will turn off, and you will need to wait for 10 seconds for it to completely shut down. Once it is off, you can power it back on by pressing the button on the left side of the device.</p>
