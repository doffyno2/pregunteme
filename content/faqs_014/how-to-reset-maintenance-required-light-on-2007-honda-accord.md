<p>The following steps will guide you through the process of resetting the Maintenance Required Light on a 2007 Honda Accord:</p>

<h3>Step 2: Press and Hold the Reset Button on the Instrument Panel</h3>

<p>Once you have the ignition switch in the "ON" position, locate the Reset button on the instrument panel. This will be located on the left side of the instrument panel, near the speedometer. Press and hold the Reset button for 10 seconds.</p>

<h3>Step 3: Release the Reset Button</h3>

<p>Once the 10 seconds has elapsed, release the Reset button. The Maintenance Required Light should now be reset.</p>

<h3>Step 4: Turn the Ignition Switch to the "OFF" Position</h3>

<p>Once the Maintenance Required Light has been reset, turn the ignition switch to the "OFF" position and exit the vehicle. This will complete the process of resetting the Maintenance Required Light.</p>
