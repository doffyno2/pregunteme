<h2>What Is Psi?</h2>

<p>PSI stands for Pounds per Square Inch, and it is a measurement of pressure. It is a unit of measure that is used to measure the amount of pressure that is exerted on an area of a certain size. In the case of tires, it is the amount of pressure that is exerted on the tire surface. PSI is the unit of measure that is used to determine the amount of air pressure that is needed in tires.</p>
