<p>Peloton is a digital streaming service that offers a wide variety of exercise classes for a monthly fee. The classes are streamed live and recorded for later access, providing a wealth of exercise options for all types of workout enthusiasts. But what if you’d like to share your Peloton subscription with a friend or family member? Is it possible?</p>

<p>The answer is yes! Peloton subscriptions can be shared with up to five people in the same household. This makes it possible to get the most out of your Peloton subscription and share the cost with others. Here’s a step-by-step guide on how to share a Peloton subscription.</p>

<h2>Step 1: Sign Up for a Peloton Subscription</h2>

<p>The first step is to sign up for a Peloton subscription. You can sign up for a monthly or annual subscription online at <a href="https://www.onepeloton.com/">the Peloton website</a>. Once you’ve signed up, you’ll be able to access the Peloton app and start streaming classes.</p>

<h2>Step 2: Set Up an Account for Each Member of Your Household</h2>

<p>The next step is to set up an account for each member of your household who wishes to use the Peloton subscription. This can be done in the Peloton app by selecting “Create New User” in the Profile tab. You’ll be asked to enter the name and email address of each household member.</p>

<h2>Step 3: Invite Each Household Member to the Peloton Subscription</h2>

<p>Once each household member has a Peloton account, you’ll need to invite them to the Peloton subscription. This can be done in the Peloton app by selecting “Invite to Subscription” in the Profile tab. You’ll be asked to enter the name and email address of each household member. Once the invitation is accepted, they’ll be able to access all of the classes included in the subscription.</p>

<h2>Step 4: Monitor Usage and Adjust as Needed</h2>

<p>Once all household members have been invited to the Peloton subscription, you can monitor their usage and adjust as needed. You can do this in the Peloton app by selecting “Manage Subscription” in the Profile tab. You’ll be able to view which classes each household member has taken, as well as adjust the settings for each user.</p>
