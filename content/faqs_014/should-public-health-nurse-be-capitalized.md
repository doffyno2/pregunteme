<h2> What Is Capitalization? </h2>

Capitalization is the process of using capital letters to denote the importance of a word or phrase. It is often used to emphasize the significance of a term in a sentence or to signify the start of a new sentence. For example, when writing the title of a book, it is common to capitalize all words in the title.
