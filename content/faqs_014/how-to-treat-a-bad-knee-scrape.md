<h2>Steps to Take for a Proper Treatment of a Knee Scrape</h2>

<p>The first step in treating a knee scrape is to clean the wound. It's important to use a mild soap and warm water to clean the scrape, taking care to remove any dirt or debris. <p>The next step is to rest the knee. This will help the scrape heal more quickly and reduce any swelling or discomfort.
