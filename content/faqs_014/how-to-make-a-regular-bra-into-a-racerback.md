<h2>Materials Needed</h2>

<p>In order to make a regular bra into a racerback, you will need the following materials:</p>

<ul>

  <li>A needle and thread</li>
  <li>A ruler</li>
</ul>

<h2>Step 1: Cut the Straps</h2>

<p>The first step is to cut the straps of the bra. Place the ruler across the top of the bra and use it as a guide for cutting the straps. Make sure to cut the straps at an angle, so that they can be easily sewn back together in the next step.</p>

<h2>Step 2: Sew the Straps Together</h2>

<p>Once the straps have been cut, you will need to sew them back together. Place the two cut straps together and use a needle and thread to sew them back together. Sew the straps together in a straight line, making sure to make the stitches as small and tight as possible.</p>

<p>Once the straps have been sewn together, you will need to check the fit of the bra. Put the bra on and make sure that the straps are comfortable and secure. Make any adjustments as necessary to ensure the perfect fit.</p>
