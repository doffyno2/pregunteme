<h3>Step 2: Cut the Wires</h3>

<p>The next step is to cut the wires. Use wire cutters to cut the wires at the desired length. Make sure to cut the wires at an angle so that they fit into the seven pin trailer plug.</p>

<h3>Step 3: Strip the Wires</h3>

<p>Once the wires are cut, use wire strippers to strip the insulation off of each wire. This will expose the metal strands inside the wire. Make sure to strip at least one inch of insulation off each wire.</p>

<h3>Step 4: Connect the Wires</h3>

<p>The next step is to connect the wires to the seven pin trailer plug. Start by inserting each wire into the appropriate slot in the plug. Make sure that the wires are connected securely and that the metal strands are touching.</p>

<h3>Step 5: Secure the Plug</h3>
