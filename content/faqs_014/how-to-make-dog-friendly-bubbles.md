<h2>Ingredients and Supplies Needed</h2>

<p>To make your own dog-friendly bubbles, you will need the following ingredients and supplies:</p>

<ul>
  <li>Glycerin</li>
  <li>Dish soap</li>
  <li>Distilled water</li>
  <li>A shallow bowl</li>
  <li>A bubble wand</li>
</ul>

<h2>Instructions</h2>

<p>Making your own dog-friendly bubbles is a simple process that can be done with just a few ingredients and supplies. Here are the steps to follow:</p>

<ol>
  <li>Mix one cup of distilled water with one tablespoon of glycerin in a shallow bowl.</li>
  <li>Add two tablespoons of dish soap to the mixture and stir until it is thoroughly mixed.</li>
  <li>Dip your bubble wand into the mixture and blow bubbles into the air.</li>
  <li>Allow your pup to play with the bubbles and pop them as they please.</li>
</ol>
