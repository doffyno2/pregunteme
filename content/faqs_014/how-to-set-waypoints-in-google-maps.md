<h2>Step 1: Open the Google Maps App</h2>

The first step to setting waypoints in Google Maps is to open the Google Maps app. The app is available for both Android and iOS devices, so you can access it from any device you own. Once the app is opened, you will be presented with a map of your current location.

<h2>Step 2: Enter Your Starting Location</h2>

Once the Google Maps app is opened, you will need to enter your starting location. You can do this by typing the address of your starting location into the search bar at the top of the app. Once you have entered the address, you will be presented with a map of your starting location.

<h2>Step 3: Set Your Destination</h2>

The next step is to set your destination. You can do this by typing the address of your destination into the search bar at the top of the app. Once you have entered the address, you will be presented with a map of your destination.

<h2>Step 4: Add Waypoints</h2>

Once you have set your starting location and destination, you can add waypoints to your route. To add a waypoint, tap the plus (+) icon at the bottom of the map. You will then be presented with a list of options, including “Add Waypoint”. Tap this option to add a waypoint to your route. You can add as many waypoints as you like, and each waypoint will appear as an icon on the map.

<h2>Step 5: Customize Your Route</h2>

Once you have added your waypoints, you can customize your route. To do this, tap the three-dot menu icon in the upper-right corner of the map. This will open a menu of options, including “Reroute”. Tap this option to customize your route. You can add and remove waypoints, or change the order of your waypoints. You can also change the mode of transportation, such as driving, walking, or cycling. Once you are satisfied with your route, tap the “Done” button to save your changes.

<h2>Step 6: Get Directions</h2>

Once you have customized your route, you can get directions to your destination. To do this, tap the “Directions” button in the lower-right corner of the map. This will open a menu of options, including “Get Directions”. Tap this option to generate directions to your destination. You will then be presented with step-by-step instructions, including turn-by-turn navigation.
