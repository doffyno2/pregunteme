<h2>Steps to Reset the Maintenance Light on a 2006 Toyota Highlander</h2>

<p>To reset the maintenance light on your 2006 Toyota Highlander, you will need to follow a few simple steps. First, turn your ignition key to the “on” position. Once the vehicle is on, press and hold the odometer reset button on your dashboard. While still holding the odometer reset button, turn your key to the “off” position. After about five seconds, the maintenance light will turn off. You can now release the odometer reset button and turn the ignition key back to the “on” position. Your maintenance light is now reset.</p>
