<h3>Step 2: Push and Hold the Reset Button</h3>

Once you have located the reset button, you are ready to reset the maintenance light on your 2012 Volvo S60 T5. Push and hold the reset button until the maintenance light turns off. This can take a few seconds, so be sure to hold the button down until the light turns off.

<h3>Step 3: Release the Reset Button</h3>

Once the light is off, you can release the reset button. This will reset the maintenance light and you can now drive your Volvo without the maintenance light being on.
