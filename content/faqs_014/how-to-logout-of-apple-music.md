<p>Apple Music is a popular streaming service that offers access to millions of songs, as well as exclusive content. The service is available on iOS devices, Macs, and PCs. However, if you're no longer using the service, you may want to log out of it on all devices.</p>

<p>In this article, we'll show you how to log out of Apple Music on your iPhone, iPad, Mac, and PC. We'll also explain why it's important to log out of the service after you're done using it, and how to delete your Apple Music account.</p>

<h2>Why Log Out of Apple Music?</h2>

<p>Logging out of Apple Music is important for a few reasons. First, it prevents other people from using your subscription and making purchases on your account. Second, it helps you stay within your monthly data plan limit since streaming music uses a lot of data. Finally, logging out of Apple Music will free up the space on your device since the songs you've downloaded won't be accessible anymore.</p>

<h2>How to Log Out of Apple Music on iPhone and iPad</h2>

<p>If you're using an iPhone or iPad, you can easily log out of Apple Music with these steps:</p>

<ol>
  <li>Open the Settings app on your device.</li>
  <li>Tap on Music.</li>
  <li>Tap on the Account option.</li>
  <li>Tap on the Sign Out button.</li>
  <li>Confirm you want to sign out.</li>
</ol>

<p>Once you've completed these steps, you'll be logged out of Apple Music on your iPhone or iPad. You'll be able to log back in with your Apple ID whenever you want.</p>

<h2>How to Log Out of Apple Music on Mac</h2>

<p>If you're using a Mac, you can log out of Apple Music with these steps:</p>

<ol>
  <li>Open the Music app on your Mac.</li>
  <li>Click on the Account menu in the sidebar.</li>
  <li>Click on the Sign Out button.</li>
  <li>Confirm you want to sign out.</li>
</ol>

<p>After you've completed these steps, you'll be logged out of Apple Music on your Mac. You can log back in with your Apple ID at any time.</p>

<h2>How to Log Out of Apple Music on PC</h2>

<p>If you're using a PC, you can log out of Apple Music with these steps:</p>

</ol>

<p>After you've completed these steps, you'll be logged out of Apple Music on your PC. You can log back in with your Apple ID at any time.</p>

<h2>How to Delete Your Apple Music Account</h2>

<p>If you want to delete your Apple Music account, you can do so with these steps:</p>

<ol>
  <li>Open the Music app on your device.</li>
  <li>Click on the Account menu in the sidebar.</li>
  <li>Click on the Delete Account button.</li>
  <li>Confirm you want to delete your account.</li>
</ol>

<p>Once you've completed these steps, your Apple Music account will be deleted. You won't be able to access any of your music or playlists anymore.</p>
