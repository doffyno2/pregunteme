<h2>Steps to Reset a Samsung Gas Stove</h2>

<p>Most Samsung gas stoves have a simple reset process that doesn’t require any tools or special knowledge. Here are the steps you should take to reset a Samsung gas stove:</p>

<h3>Step 1: Shut off the Gas Supply</h3>

<p>The first step to resetting a Samsung gas stove is to shut off the gas supply. This is done by turning off the gas valve on the stove. This will ensure that no gas is flowing to the stove while you are resetting it.</p>

<h3>Step 2: Unplug the Stove</h3>

<p>The next step is to unplug the stove from the wall. This is important because it ensures that no power is flowing to the stove while you are resetting it. Unplugging the stove will also reset any errors that are causing it to not heat up.</p>

<h3>Step 3: Wait 10 Minutes</h3>

<p>After unplugging the stove, you should wait 10 minutes. This allows the stove to reset itself after being unplugged. After 10 minutes, plug the stove back in and turn the gas valve back on.</p>

<h3>Step 4: Turn on the Stove</h3>

<p>Once the stove is plugged in and the gas valve is turned on, you can turn the stove back on. At this point, the stove should be reset and it should be working properly.</p>
