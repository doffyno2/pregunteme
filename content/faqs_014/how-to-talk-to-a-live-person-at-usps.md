<h2>How to Contact USPS Customer Service</h2>

<p>There are several ways to contact USPS customer service. You can call them, email them, or even talk to them online. Here are the best methods for getting in touch with USPS customer service: </p>

<h3>Call USPS Customer Service</h3>

<p>If you’d like to speak with a live person, the best way to do so is by calling USPS customer service. You can reach them at 1-800-ASK-USPS (1-800-275-8777). When you call, you’ll be connected to a customer service representative who can answer any questions you might have. </p>

<h3>Email USPS Customer Service</h3>

<p>If you’d prefer to communicate via email, you can also do that. You can send your email inquiries to USPS Customer Service at custserv@usps.gov. Keep in mind that they may take several days to respond to your email. </p>

<h3>Chat With USPS Customer Service</h3>

<p>If you’d like to get help quickly, you can also use the USPS online chat feature. To start a chat, simply go to the USPS website and click on the “Chat with Us” button. You’ll be connected to a customer service representative who can answer any questions you might have. </p>

<h2>What to Expect When You Call USPS Customer Service</h2>

<p>When you call USPS customer service, the first thing you’ll be asked to do is provide your tracking number. This will help the customer service representative locate your package and provide you with the best possible assistance. </p>

<p>Next, the customer service representative will ask you a few questions about your package. They may ask you when you shipped the package, where it’s being shipped to, and what type of package it is. This will help them provide you with the most accurate information about your package. </p>

<p>Once the customer service representative has all the information they need, they’ll be able to answer any questions you have about the package. They can provide you with tracking information, estimated delivery dates, and any other information you need. </p>
