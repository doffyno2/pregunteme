<h2>What is Planking?</h2>

Planking is a type of bodyweight exercise that involves holding your body in a position similar to a plank. It requires you to hold your body in a straight line, supported by your toes and forearms. Your arms should be bent at the elbows and your feet should be hip-width apart. You should hold the position for as long as you can, focusing on keeping your body in a straight line and engaging your core muscles.
