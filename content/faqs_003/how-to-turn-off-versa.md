<h2>How to Turn Off Versa in the App</h2>

<p>The easiest way to turn off your Versa is by using the Fitbit app. To do this, open the app and tap the account icon in the top right corner of the screen. Then tap the “Settings” section and select “Device Settings.” Here you will find a list of all the devices that are connected to your Fitbit account. Select the Versa device and then tap the “Turn Off” button. Your Versa will now be turned off.</p>

<h2>How to Turn Off Versa on the Device</h2>

<p>If you are unable to turn off your Versa using the app, then you can also do it directly on the device. To do this, press and hold the left-hand button on the Versa for five seconds. This will open the “Device Settings” menu. Scroll down to the “Power Off” option and tap it. Your Versa will now be turned off.</p>
