<h2>What Is HIIT?</h2>

<p>HIIT is an acronym for high-intensity interval training. It is a form of exercise that involves short bursts of intense physical activity followed by short periods of rest or low-intensity activity. This type of exercise has been proven to be an effective way to improve physical fitness, increase cardiovascular health, and burn more calories in a shorter amount of time.</p>

<p>HIIT workouts typically last anywhere from 10 to 30 minutes, depending on the intensity of the exercise. During these workouts, the intensity of the exercise is increased for a short period of time, usually between 20 to 90 seconds. After this period of intense exercise, the intensity is decreased for a short period of time, usually between 10 to 60 seconds. This pattern of intense exercise and rest is repeated for the duration of the workout.</p>
