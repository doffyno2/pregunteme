<p>A district is a unique tile on the map in Civ 6 that provides various benefits to your civilization. <p>Repairing a damaged district in Civ 6 requires resources, time, and a little bit of luck. First, you’ll need to make sure you have enough resources available to repair the district. Once you’ve gathered the necessary resources, you’ll need to select the damaged district and choose the ‘Repair’ option.
