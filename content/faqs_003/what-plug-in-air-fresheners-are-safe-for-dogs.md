<h2>What Are Plug-In Air Fresheners?</h2>

Plug-in air fresheners are a type of air freshener that are plugged into an electrical outlet, releasing a continuous scent of pleasant-smelling air. They come in a variety of styles and scents, from fresh and fruity to musky and woodsy. They are often used to mask odors in the home, such as cigarette smoke or pet odors.
