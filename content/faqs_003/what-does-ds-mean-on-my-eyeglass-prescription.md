<p>DS stands for "Distance Sphere," and is a measure of the lens power required to correct nearsightedness or farsightedness. It is one of several parameters used to determine the prescription for eyeglasses. <p>The DS parameter measures the spherical power of a lens. Astigmatism is a type of refractive error where the eye has an irregularly shaped cornea or lens.  The calculation is shown below:</p>

<p>DS = Refractive Error - Astigmatism Correction</p>
