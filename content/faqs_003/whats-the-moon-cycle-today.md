<p>The new moon is the first phase of the moon cycle and occurs when the moon is between the Earth and sun. This is the darkest phase of the moon cycle and is associated with beginnings and new projects. </p>

<h3>Waxing Crescent</h3>

<p>The waxing crescent moon is the second phase of the moon cycle and occurs when the moon is partially illuminated by sunlight. This phase is associated with growth, expansion, and creativity. </p>

<h3>Full Moon</h3>

<p>The full moon is the third phase of the moon cycle and is the brightest phase of the moon cycle. This phase is associated with climax, abundance, and completion. </p>

<h3>Waning Crescent</h3>

<p>The waning crescent is the fourth and final phase of the moon cycle and occurs when the moon is no longer illuminated by sunlight. This phase is associated with endings, reflection, and letting go. </p>
