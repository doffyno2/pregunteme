<h2>Safety Tips for Pregnant Women on Rides</h2>

<p>Before you decide to go on a ride, you should consult your doctor to make sure it is safe for you and your baby. If your doctor gives you the go ahead, there are a few safety tips to follow.</p>

<ul>
    <li>Make sure the ride is not too fast or too bumpy. Some roller coasters and other rides can be too intense for pregnant women, so you should avoid those.</li>
    <li>Choose rides that have padded seats and seat belts for added protection.</li>
    <li>Make sure your seatbelt is properly fastened at all times.</li>
    <li>Do not stay on the ride for too long. Most rides last up to a few minutes, and you should not stay on the ride any longer than that.</li>
    <li>Listen to your body. If you feel uncomfortable or dizzy, stop the ride immediately.</li>
</ul>
