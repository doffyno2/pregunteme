<p>Coleman propane lanterns are a great way to light up the night. Whether you are camping, tailgating, or just having a backyard cookout, these reliable and easy to use lanterns provide light in even the darkest of places. Lighting a Coleman propane lantern is a simple process that can be done in just a few steps. </p>

<h2>Step 1: Gather Your Supplies</h2>

<p>Before you can light your Coleman propane lantern, you will need to gather all of the necessary supplies. You will need a propane tank, a Coleman propane lantern, a match or lighter, and an adjustable wrench. Make sure that the propane tank is full and that the lantern is properly assembled.</p>

<h2>Step 2: Connect the Propane Tank to the Lantern</h2>

<p>Using the adjustable wrench, connect the propane tank to the lantern. Make sure that the connection is secure and that there are no leaks. If there are any leaks, tighten the connection with the wrench until the leak stops.</p>

<h2>Step 3: Turn on the Lantern</h2>

<p>Turn the Coleman propane lantern on by turning the knob clockwise. Make sure that you have the knob turned to the "on" position. If the knob is not turned all the way to the "on" position, the lantern will not light.</p>

<h2>Step 4: Light the Lantern</h2>

<p>Once the lantern is on, you can light it. Gently press the red igniter button on the side of the lantern. If the igniter does not light, make sure that there is fuel in the tank and that the connection is secure. If the igniter still does not light, try using a match or lighter to light the lantern.</p>

<h2>Step 5: Enjoy the Light</h2>

<p>Now you can enjoy the light from your Coleman propane lantern. Make sure that you keep the lantern away from flammable objects and that you check it regularly for leaks. When you are done using the lantern, turn the knob counter-clockwise to turn it off.</p>

<h2>Frequently Asked Questions</h2>

<h3>What kind of propane tank do I need for my Coleman propane lantern?</h3>

<p>You will need a Coleman propane tank to use with your Coleman propane lantern. These tanks come in various sizes, ranging from 16 ounce to 20 pound tanks. Make sure that the tank is full before attempting to light the lantern.</p>

<h3>How do I check for leaks in my propane lantern?</h3>

<p>The best way to check for leaks in your propane lantern is to use a mixture of water and dish soap. Put the mixture on the connections and watch for bubbles. If there are bubbles, the connection is leaking and needs to be tightened.</p>

<h3>What should I do if my Coleman propane lantern won't light?</h3>
