SAE measurement is an important aspect of engineering and automotive technology. It stands for Society of Automotive Engineers, and it is a set of standards that are used to measure the performance of vehicles, engines and components. The SAE measurement system is used to standardize the measurement of physical properties such as length, weight, area, volume, pressure, temperature, velocity and power.
