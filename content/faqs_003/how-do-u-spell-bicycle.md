<h2>What is Bicycle Used for?</h2>

<h2>Are There Different Types of Bicycles?</h2>

Yes, there are different types of bicycles. The most common type of bicycle is the road bike, which is designed for riding on roads and paved surfaces. Mountain bikes are designed for off-road use and feature wider tires and more durable frames. Touring bikes are designed for long-distance travel and are typically equipped with multiple cargo racks for carrying luggage. Finally, there are also hybrid bikes, which are a combination of road and mountain bikes, and are great for all-around use. 

<h2>What is the Difference Between a Bicycle and a Motorcycle?</h2>

Additionally, motorcycles typically have larger frames, handlebars, and seats than bicycles, and they also have a higher top speed.
