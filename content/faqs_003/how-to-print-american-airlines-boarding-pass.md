How to Print American Airlines Boarding Pass

When you are traveling with American Airlines, it is essential to have your boarding pass available. Without it, you cannot board the plane. Fortunately, it is straightforward to print your American Airlines boarding pass. In this article, we will cover how to print an American Airlines boarding pass and provide other helpful tips to ensure you are prepared for your flight.

<h2>Getting Started</h2>

The first step in preparing to print your American Airlines boarding pass is to make sure you have the necessary information. You will need to provide your confirmation code, ticket number, and your last name. If you are traveling with someone else, you will need to provide their information as well.

<h2>Print at Home</h2>

Once you have the necessary information, you can print your boarding pass at home. American Airlines provides a printable boarding pass on their website. All you have to do is enter the required information and you will be able to print your boarding pass.

<h2>Print at the Airport</h2>

If you are unable to print your boarding pass at home, you can always print it at the airport. American Airlines has kiosks available at most airports. All you have to do is enter your confirmation code and ticket number and you will be able to print your boarding pass. Keep in mind that there may be a fee associated with printing your boarding pass at the airport.

<h2>Mobile Boarding Pass</h2>

If you do not want to print your boarding pass at home or at the airport, you can always use the American Airlines mobile app. The app allows you to check in for your flight and access your boarding pass. All you have to do is enter your confirmation code and ticket number and you will be able to access your boarding pass. Keep in mind that some airports may not accept a mobile boarding pass so it is best to check with the airport before you arrive.

<h2>Check-In Times</h2>

It is important to keep in mind that American Airlines has different check-in times for domestic and international flights. For domestic flights, you must check in at least 45 minutes before your scheduled departure time. For international flights, you must check in at least 90 minutes before your scheduled departure time.
