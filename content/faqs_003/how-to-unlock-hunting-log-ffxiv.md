<p>The Hunting Log in FFXIV can be unlocked relatively early in the game and is unlocked by speaking to the Hunting Log NPC in South Shroud. The NPC can be found at coordinates 20,6 in South Shroud. Speak to the NPC and they will give you the quest “The Land of Opportunity”. Once you complete this quest you will unlock the Hunting Log.</p>

<h2>How to Use the Hunting Log in FFXIV</h2>

<p>Once you have unlocked the Hunting Log, you will be able to access it from your menu. To access the Hunting Log, open your menu and select the “Hunting Log” option. This will open up the Hunting Log window where you can view all of the tasks that you can complete. From here you can select a task to attempt and then travel to the location of the monster or objective you need to complete the task. You will then need to complete the task in order to gain credit for it in the Hunting Log.</p>

<p>After completing a task, you will be able to select it from the Hunting Log window and view the rewards that you will receive for completing it. You will also be able to select the “Register” button in order to register the task and receive the rewards.</p>
