<h2>Steps to Search for Classes on Peloton App</h2>

<p>The Peloton app is easy to use, and the steps to search for classes are simple. To get started, open the Peloton app on your mobile device. Once you are logged in, the main menu will appear. From the main menu, you can select “Classes” to search for classes. You can then choose from a variety of options, including Live Classes, On-Demand Classes, and Instructor-Led Classes. 

Once you have chosen your class type, you can then narrow down your search results by selecting filters such as difficulty, duration, music genre, language, and instructor type. You can also save your favorite classes so that they appear at the top of your search results. When you find the class you want to take, simply select it and you will be taken to the class page, where you can read more about the class and its instructor. Once you are ready to join, simply select “Join Class” and you will be ready to begin.</p>
