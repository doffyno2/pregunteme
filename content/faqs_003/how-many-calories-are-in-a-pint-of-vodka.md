<h2>Calories in Vodka</h2>

Vodka is made from fermented grains, such as wheat, rye, or barley. The fermentation process converts the sugars in the grains into alcohol. Since alcohol contains seven calories per gram, vodka has a relatively high caloric content. A standard pint of vodka contains around 400 calories, though some brands may have more or less.

<h2>Factors That Affect Calories in Vodka</h2>

The exact number of calories in a pint of vodka will depend on the brand and type of vodka. Some vodka brands have higher alcohol content, which means they have more calories. For example, a pint of 80-proof vodka will have more calories than a pint of 40-proof vodka. Additionally, flavored vodkas, such as citrus or berry flavored vodkas, may have added sugars that increase their caloric content.
