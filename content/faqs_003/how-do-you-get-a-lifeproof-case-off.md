<h2>Use a Hair Dryer to Heat Up the Case</h2>

<p>One of the best ways to get a Lifeproof case off without damaging your phone is by using a hair dryer. Simply turn the hair dryer on the medium heat setting and point it at the edges of the Lifeproof case. The heat from the hair dryer will soften the adhesive that binds the case to your phone, making it easier for you to remove the case without damaging your phone.</p>

<h2>Use a Case Opening Tool</h2>

<p>Another option for getting a Lifeproof case off without damaging your phone is to use a case opening tool. Case opening tools are designed specifically for removing cases from phones and are often more effective than using a hair dryer. To use a case opening tool, insert the tool into the seam between the case and your phone, and then carefully twist the tool to separate the adhesive. Be sure to take your time and be gentle, as it is easy to damage your phone if you are too aggressive.</p>

<h2>Use Dental Floss to Separate the Case</h2>

<p>If you don't have a case opening tool, you can also try using dental floss to separate the case from your phone. Simply insert a small piece of dental floss between the case and your phone, and then gently move it back and forth. The floss will help to separate the adhesive and allow you to remove the case without damaging your phone.</p>
