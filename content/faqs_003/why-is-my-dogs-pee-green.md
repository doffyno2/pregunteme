<h2>Is It Normal for My Dog’s Pee to Be Green?</h2>

<p>It’s not unusual for a dog’s pee to be green, but it’s not always normal either. Green pee can be caused by a variety of factors, including medical conditions, certain foods, and even something as simple as a change in diet or environment. </p>

<p>Some medical conditions can cause a dog’s pee to turn green. These include liver or kidney disease, urinary tract infections, or even a reaction to certain medications.
