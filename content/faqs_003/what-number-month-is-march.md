<h2>When Does Spring Begin?</h2>

In the Northern Hemisphere, spring is said to begin on the vernal equinox, which typically falls on March 20th or 21st. The vernal equinox marks the moment when the Sun is directly above the equator and day and night are of equal length. This marks the beginning of spring in the Northern Hemisphere and the beginning of fall in the Southern Hemisphere.
