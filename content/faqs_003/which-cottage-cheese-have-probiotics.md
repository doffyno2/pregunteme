<h2>What are Probiotics?</h2>

Probiotics are beneficial bacteria that live in our gut and promote good digestion and overall health. They help to break down food, extract nutrients, and protect us from harmful bacteria. Probiotics can be found naturally in foods such as yogurt, kefir, sauerkraut, and kimchi. They can also be taken as supplements. 

<h2>What are the Benefits of Probiotics?</h2>

Probiotics have been found to have a number of health benefits, including: 

<ul>
<li>Improved digestion and nutrient absorption</li>
<li>Reduced risk of infection and inflammation</li>
<li>Improved immune system function</li>
<li>Better mental health and mood</li>
<li>Reduced risk of allergies and asthma</li>
<li>Reduced risk of obesity and diabetes</li>
</ul>
