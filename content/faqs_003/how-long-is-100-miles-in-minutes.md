<h2>What is a Mile?</h2>

A mile is a unit of distance measuring 5,280 feet, or 1.609344 kilometers. It is used in the imperial system of measurement, which is still used in the United States and other countries. The abbreviation for mile is ‘mi’ or ‘m’. 

<h2>How Long Does it Take to Travel 100 Miles?</h2>

The time it takes to travel 100 miles depends on several factors, such as your mode of transportation and the speed limit. Generally, if you are travelling by car, it will take approximately 1.5 hours to cover 100 miles. This is based on an average speed of 65 mph and includes stops along the way. On the other hand, travelling by train can take anywhere from 2.5 to 3.5 hours, depending on the train line and the speed limits.
