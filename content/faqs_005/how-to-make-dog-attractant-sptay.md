<h2>Ingredients for Dog Attractant Spray</h2>

<p>The ingredients for making a dog attractant spray are simple and easy to find. The main ingredient is a base oil like coconut oil, olive oil, or almond oil. Depending on the type of oil you choose, you can add essential oils for different scents. Some popular essential oils for dog attractant sprays include lavender, eucalyptus, cedarwood, and orange. Additionally, you can add a few drops of distilled water to the mixture to make it easier to spray.</p>

<h2>Steps to Make Dog Attractant Spray</h2>

<p>Making the attractant spray is easy and quick. First, mix the base oil and essential oil together in a bowl or bottle. You can use anywhere between 20-30 drops of essential oil, depending on your desired scent. Once the oils are mixed, add a few drops of distilled water, shake the bottle, and it’s ready to use. </p>

<h2>How to Use Dog Attractant Spray</h2>

<p>Once you’ve made the spray, it’s time to use it. The spray can be used in a variety of situations to make your dog feel comfortable. Start by spraying the area that your dog is going to stay in. If you’re leaving your dog at a new place, spray the area before you leave. You can also spray your dog’s bed, toys, and even your own clothing to make them feel more relaxed. The spray can also be used on walks to make the area more inviting.</p>
