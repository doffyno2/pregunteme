<h2>What Types of IDs are Available for Minors in Texas?</h2>

There are two types of IDs available for minors in Texas: state-issued IDs and school IDs. Both forms of identification are accepted by law enforcement, employers, and other organizations. 

<h3>State-issued IDs</h3>

State-issued IDs are issued by the Texas Department of Public Safety. They are valid for up to six years and can be used for a variety of purposes, including identification for travel, school, and work. A state-issued ID will have a photo of the minor and their name, date of birth, address, and other information. 

<h3>School IDs</h3>

School IDs are issued by the school district where the minor is enrolled. They are valid only while the minor is enrolled in the school district and cannot be used for any other purpose. School IDs typically have the student's name, date of birth, school name, and a photo. 

<h2>What Documents are Required to Obtain an ID for a Minor in Texas?</h2>

The documents required for obtaining an ID for a minor in Texas vary depending on the type of ID being requested. 

<h3>State-issued IDs</h3>

In order to obtain a state-issued ID for a minor in Texas, the following documents must be presented: 
<ul>
  <li>Proof of identity: birth certificate, passport, or other government-issued ID</li>
  <li>Proof of residence: utility bill, bank statement, or other document showing the minor's name and address</li>
  <li>Proof of parental consent: if the minor is under 18, they must have a parent or legal guardian present to sign a consent form</li>
  <li>Two forms of payment: cash, check, or money order</li>
</ul>

<h3>School IDs</h3>

In order to obtain a school ID for a minor in Texas, the following documents must be presented: 
<ul>
  <li>Proof of enrollment: student ID card or enrollment verification</li>
  <li>Proof of residence: utility bill, bank statement, or other document showing the minor's name and address</li>
  <li>Proof of parental consent: if the minor is under 18, they must have a parent or legal guardian present to sign a consent form</li>
  <li>One form of payment: cash, check, or money order</li>
</ul>

<h2>Where to Obtain an ID for a Minor in Texas</h2>

State-issued IDs can be obtained at any Texas driver license office. School IDs can be obtained at the school district where the minor is enrolled.
