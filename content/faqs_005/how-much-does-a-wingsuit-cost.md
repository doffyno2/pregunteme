<h2>What Is a Wingsuit?</h2>

<p>A wingsuit is a specialized garment designed to be worn while skydiving. It consists of two long wings that extend from the arms to the legs and a webbed fabric that connects the two wings. The wings and webbing work together to create an airfoil that allows the wearer to glide through the air with greater control and speed. Wingsuits have become increasingly popular among experienced skydivers who are looking for an adrenaline rush.</p>

<h2>Types of Wingsuits</h2>

<p>The two main types of wingsuits are beginner wingsuits and performance wingsuits. Performance wingsuits, on the other hand, are designed for experienced skydivers who are looking for speed and control. These wingsuits are usually made of high-tech materials such as Kevlar and have a higher profile, meaning more air resistance.</p>
