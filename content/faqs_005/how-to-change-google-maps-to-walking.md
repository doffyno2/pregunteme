Changing Google Maps to walking is easy. Here's what you need to do:

<h3>Step 1: Open the Google Maps App</h3>

<h3>Step 2: Enter Your Destination</h3>

Once you have opened the app, enter your destination. You can either type in the address or use the map to search for the location. Once you have selected the destination, tap on the directions tab at the bottom of the screen.

<h3>Step 3: Change the Mode of Transportation</h3>

On the directions tab, you will see a list of transportation options. Select the option for walking and Google Maps will automatically adjust the route to the most efficient one for walking.

<h3>Step 4: Adjust the Route</h3>

If you want to customize your route, you can do so by tapping on the route options. You can change the start and end points of your route, as well as add other points of interest along the way. You can also choose to avoid certain roads or areas.

<h3>Step 5: Get Directions</h3>

Once you have adjusted your route, tap on the “Get Directions” button. Google Maps will then provide you with detailed directions, including the total time and distance of your route.
