Can I Wear Shorts for Motorcycle Riding?



When it comes to motorcycle riding, there are a lot of things you need to consider. From the type of motorcycle you should ride to the type of clothing you should wear, it can seem like there are endless decisions to make. A common question we get asked is: can I wear shorts for motorcycle riding? 

The short answer is yes, you can wear shorts for motorcycle riding. However, it is not recommended that you do so, as long trousers and other protective clothing offer more protection than shorts. In this article, we’ll go into more detail and explain why you should avoid wearing shorts when riding a motorcycle. 

<h2>Why You Should Avoid Wearing Shorts for Motorcycle Riding</h2>

As we mentioned above, you can wear shorts for motorcycle riding, but it is not recommended. There are a few reasons why you should avoid doing so: 

<h3>Shorts Don’t Offer Much Protection</h3>

The most important reason why you should avoid wearing shorts for motorcycle riding is that they don’t offer much protection. Shorts are made from lightweight materials and don’t provide a lot of coverage. As such, they won’t protect your skin from the heat of the engine, flying debris, or any potential road rash. 

<h3>Shorts Don’t Provide the Right Fit</h3>

Shorts also don’t offer the right fit for motorcycle riding. Ideally, your clothing should fit snugly and be comfortable to ride in. However, shorts typically come with a looser fit, which could be uncomfortable and even dangerous when riding a motorcycle. 

<h3>Shorts Don’t Have Any Pockets</h3>

Another issue with shorts is that they don’t typically have any pockets. This can be a problem when it comes to carrying essential items like your phone, wallet, or keys. Without pockets, you’ll have to carry these items in your hands, which can be dangerous and distracting when riding. 

<h2>What Type of Clothing Should You Wear for Motorcycle Riding?</h2>

We’ve established that you shouldn’t wear shorts for motorcycle riding. So, what type of clothing should you wear instead? The best type of clothing to wear when riding a motorcycle is long trousers, a long-sleeved shirt, and a good pair of gloves. 

<h3>Long Trousers</h3>

Long trousers are the most important item of clothing to wear when riding a motorcycle. They should be made from a thick, durable material, such as leather or Kevlar. This will protect your skin from the heat of the engine and any potential road rash. 

<h3>Long-Sleeved Shirt</h3>

You should also wear a long-sleeved shirt when riding a motorcycle. This will provide extra protection from the heat of the engine and any debris that could fly up from the road. It will also help protect your arms and hands in the event of a crash. 

<h3>Gloves</h3>

Finally, you should always wear a pair of gloves when riding a motorcycle. Gloves will help protect your hands from the heat of the engine and provide extra grip on the handlebars. They will also protect your hands in the event of a crash. 

<h2>Conclusion</h2>

In conclusion, it is not recommended that you wear shorts for motorcycle riding. Shorts don’t offer much protection and don’t provide the right fit. They also don’t have any pockets, which can be a problem when it comes to carrying essential items. The best type of clothing to wear when riding a motorcycle is long trousers, a long-sleeved shirt, and a good pair of gloves.
