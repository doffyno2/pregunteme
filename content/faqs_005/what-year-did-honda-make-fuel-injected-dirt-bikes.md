<h2>What Is Fuel Injection?</h2>

Fuel injection is a system that injects fuel into the engine's intake port or manifold. It is different from carburetors, which use atmospheric pressure and gravity to draw fuel into the engine. Fuel injection is more efficient and provides better performance than carburetors. It can also be tuned to optimize performance for different riding conditions.

<h2>What Are The Benefits Of Fuel Injection?</h2>

Fuel injection offers several benefits over carburetors. It is more efficient, allowing more power with less fuel consumption. It also provides better control over the mixture of air and fuel entering the engine, resulting in improved engine performance. Fuel injection also reduces emissions, making it a more environmentally friendly system.

<h2>What Are The Different Types Of Fuel Injection Systems?</h2>

There are several different types of fuel injection systems. The most common type is the sequential fuel injection (SFI) system, which injects fuel directly into the intake port. Another type is the multiport fuel injection (MPFI) system, which uses multiple injectors to deliver fuel to the engine. Finally, there is the direct fuel injection (DFI) system, which injects fuel directly into the combustion chamber.

<h2>What Are The Advantages Of Fuel Injected Dirt Bikes?</h2>
