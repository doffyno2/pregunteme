<h2>What Does Square Feet Mean?</h2>

Square feet (or sq ft) is a unit of measure used to describe the size of an area in terms of feet. To put it another way, it’s the amount of space contained within a square that’s one foot on each side. 

<h2>How to Measure Square Feet</h2>

To calculate the square footage of a room or space, take the length of the area (in feet) and multiply it by the width of the area (in feet). The resulting figure is the area of the space in square feet.
