<h2>How to Reset the Maintenance Light</h2>

<p>Resetting the maintenance light of a 2018 Subaru Impreza is a fairly simple process. The first step is to locate the maintenance light button on the dash. It is usually located near the steering wheel and is labeled “maintenance.” Once the button is located, press and hold it down for approximately five seconds. </p>

<p>The next step is to turn the key to the “on” position. It is important to ensure that the engine is not running during this process. Once the key is in the “on” position, press and hold the maintenance light button down for five more seconds. Once this is done, the maintenance light should be reset and the vehicle is ready to go. </p>
