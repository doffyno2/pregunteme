It's important to reset the maintenance light after service has been performed to make sure that it is functioning correctly. In this article, we'll explain how to reset the maintenance light on the 2014 Toyota Corolla.</p>

<h2>What Does the Maintenance Light Mean?</h2>

<p>The maintenance light on the 2014 Toyota Corolla is a reminder that it's time to take your car in for service. The light is triggered when the car has reached a certain mileage or has been in operation for a certain amount of time. It's important to take your car in for service at the intervals indicated by the maintenance light. If you don't, it could lead to problems with your car.</p>

<h2>How to Reset the Maintenance Light on a 2014 Toyota Corolla</h2>

<p>The process for resetting the maintenance light on the 2014 Toyota Corolla is fairly simple. Here's what you need to do:</p>

<ul>
  <li>Start the car and wait for the maintenance light to come on.</li>
  <li>Press and hold the “trip/reset” button located on the instrument panel.</li>
  <li>When the maintenance light starts blinking, release the trip/reset button.</li>
  <li>Turn the ignition off.</li>
  <li>Start the car again to verify that the maintenance light is off.</li>
</ul>

<p>If the light does not turn off, try repeating the steps above. If the light still won't turn off, you may need to take your car in for service or have a mechanic take a look at it.</p>
