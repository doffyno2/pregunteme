<p>It's easy to understand why people would want to know how long they can keep fish cakes in the fridge. Fish cakes are a delicious snack or meal and can be enjoyed in a variety of ways. <h2>How Long Do Fish Cakes Last in the Fridge?</h2>

<p>When stored properly in the refrigerator, fish cakes can last up to four days. This is due to the fact that fish cakes contain a large amount of moisture, which can cause them to spoil quickly. To ensure your fish cakes stay fresh for as long as possible, it's important to keep them tightly sealed in an airtight container or wrap them in plastic wrap. It's also important to store them in the coldest part of the refrigerator, as this will help to slow down the rate at which they spoil.</p>

<h2>How to Tell if Fish Cakes Have Gone Bad</h2>

<p>It's always best to err on the side of caution when it comes to food safety. If you're unsure about the freshness of your fish cakes, there are a few signs you can look for to determine if they have gone bad. If the fish cakes have a slimy or discolored appearance, have an off-putting odor, or have developed mold, it's best to discard them. Additionally, if the fish cakes have been stored in the refrigerator for more than four days, it's best to discard them as well.</p>

<h2>How to Store Fish Cakes</h2>

<p>To ensure your fish cakes stay fresh for as long as possible, it's important to store them properly in the refrigerator. First, make sure the fish cakes are completely cooled before storing them. This will help to prevent bacteria from growing. Once cooled, place the fish cakes in an airtight container or wrap them in plastic wrap. Finally, store the fish cakes in the coldest part of the refrigerator.</p>

<h2>How to Reheat Fish Cakes</h2>

<p>When it comes time to reheat fish cakes, it's important to do so in a way that won't cause them to become dry or rubbery. The best way to reheat fish cakes is in the oven or in the microwave. If reheating in the oven, preheat the oven to 350°F and place the fish cakes on a baking sheet. Bake for 10-15 minutes or until heated through. If reheating in the microwave, place the fish cakes on a microwave-safe plate and heat on high for 1-2 minutes or until heated through.</p>

<h2>How to Freeze Fish Cakes</h2>

<p>If you want to extend the shelf life of your fish cakes, one option is to freeze them. To freeze fish cakes, first allow them to cool completely. Then, place them in a freezer-safe container or plastic wrap. Label the container with the date you are freezing the fish cakes and place them in the freezer. When you're ready to enjoy them, thaw the fish cakes in the refrigerator. Once thawed, follow the instructions for reheating fish cakes as outlined above.</p>
