<h2>What Is a Single Spaced Page?</h2>

A single spaced page is a page that contains one line of text per line, with no extra space between lines. This type of formatting is usually used in academic writing and other professional documents. It’s also the default setting for Microsoft Word documents.

<h2>How Many Words Are in a Single Spaced Page?</h2>

The exact number of words that will fit on a single spaced page depends on the font type, font size, and margins. However, a general estimate is 250-280 words per single spaced page, or 500-560 words per double spaced page.

<h2>How to Decrease the Number of Words per Page</h2>
