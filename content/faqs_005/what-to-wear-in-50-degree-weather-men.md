<p>Shopping for clothes to wear during 50-degree weather can be a challenge. After all, 50 degrees is a tricky temperature, as it is too hot for heavy winter clothing and too cold for summer clothing. So what should you wear?</p>

<p>Whether you are looking for something casual or something more formal, there are plenty of outfit options for men in 50-degree weather. We've put together this guide to help you select the perfect outfit for any occasion.</p>

<h2>What to Wear in 50 Degree Weather: Men's Guide</h2>

<p>The key to dressing for 50-degree weather is layering. You want to be able to adjust your outfit depending on how hot or cold you feel, and layering gives you the ability to do that. Here are some tips for dressing for 50-degree weather.</p>

<h3>Lightweight T-Shirts and Sweaters</h3>

<p>A lightweight t-shirt is an excellent option for 50-degree weather. Choose a lightweight t-shirt that is made of a breathable fabric, such as cotton, linen, or bamboo. You can also layer a lightweight sweater over the t-shirt for extra warmth.</p>

<h3>Lightweight Jackets</h3>

<p>Lightweight jackets are a great way to stay warm in 50-degree weather. Look for jackets made with lightweight materials such as nylon, polyester, or even wool. These materials will provide enough warmth without making you too hot.</p>

<h3>Jeans and Chinos</h3>

<p>Jeans and chinos are classic wardrobe staples that can be worn in 50-degree weather. Choose a pair of jeans or chinos that are made of a lightweight fabric such as cotton or linen. This will ensure that you are comfortable, even when the temperature rises.</p>

<h3>Long Sleeve Shirts</h3>

<p>Long sleeve shirts are a great option for 50-degree weather. Choose a lightweight fabric such as linen or cotton to ensure that you are comfortable. Long sleeve shirts are also a great way to dress up an outfit, so they are perfect for a more formal occasion.</p>
