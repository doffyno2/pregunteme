When the temperature outside is 35 degrees, it can be tricky to decide what to wear. It's not so cold that you need a heavy winter coat, but it's also not warm enough to wear shorts and a T-shirt. <h2>Layering Your Clothing</h2>

Layering your clothing is a great way to stay warm in 35 degree weather. Start with a light base layer, such as a tank top or short-sleeved shirt. Then add a mid-layer, like a long-sleeved shirt or light sweater. Top it off with a light jacket, such as a denim jacket or bomber jacket, and you'll be ready to go.

<h2>Pants or Skirts</h2>
