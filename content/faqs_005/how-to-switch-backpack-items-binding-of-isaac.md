<h2>How to Switch Backpack Items in Binding of Isaac</h2>

<p>Switching backpack items in Binding of Isaac is simple and straightforward. All you need to do is open your inventory and click on the backpack icon at the top right. <p>You can also use the keyboard shortcuts to switch backpack items. Pressing the “I” key will open your inventory and the “B” key will open your backpack. Then, use the arrow keys to select the item you want to switch out and press the “Enter” key to confirm the selection.</p>
