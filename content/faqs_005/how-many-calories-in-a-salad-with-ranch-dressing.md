<h2>Calorie Count for a Salad with Ranch Dressing</h2>

The amount of calories in a salad with ranch dressing depends on the ingredients and the amount of dressing used. Generally speaking, a salad with one tablespoon of ranch dressing contains anywhere from 40 to 80 calories. Of course, if you add more dressing, the calorie count will increase.

If you are looking to keep the calorie count of your salad low, there are a few things you can do. First, opt for light ranch dressing, which contains fewer calories than regular ranch dressing. Second, consider using other salad dressings that are lower in calories, such as balsamic vinaigrette, which typically contains around 40 calories per tablespoon. Finally, you can also opt for a homemade dressing made with olive oil and vinegar.
