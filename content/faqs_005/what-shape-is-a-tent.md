<h3>A-Frame Tents</h3>

A-frame tents are the most basic type of tent and are usually the easiest to recognize. They are shaped like an A and typically have two poles that form the roof and two poles that form the sides. A-frame tents are usually the most affordable and lightweight option, making them great for camping trips.
