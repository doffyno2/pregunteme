<h2>What Is a BB Gun?</h2>

A BB gun is a type of air gun designed to shoot small metal pellets or “BBs” at low velocity. BB guns are usually powered by either a CO2 or spring-piston mechanism. BB guns come in a variety of shapes and sizes and can be used for target shooting, hunting small game, or recreational plinking.
