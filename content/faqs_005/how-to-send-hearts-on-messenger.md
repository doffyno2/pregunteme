<p>If you're a frequent user of Facebook Messenger, you may have noticed that the messaging app now offers a range of emoji reactions including hearts. In this guide, we'll show you how to send hearts on Messenger and why you should use them.</p>

<h2>What Are Hearts on Messenger?</h2>

<p>Hearts on Messenger are a fun way to express yourself in conversations. You can send them in response to messages, photos, or videos. When a heart is sent, a small love heart emoji appears below the message. It’s a great way to show appreciation, affection, or love, and it can be used with all your friends.</p>

<h2>How to Send Hearts on Messenger</h2>

<p>Sending hearts on Messenger is easy. All you need to do is open the conversation and press the “Love” button. Once you’ve done this, the heart will appear below the message. You can also send hearts by tapping the heart icon next to the message you want to react to. If you want to send a heart to multiple messages, you can simply long-press the heart icon.</p>

<h2>Why You Should Use Hearts on Messenger</h2>

<p>Hearts on Messenger are a great way to show your friends and family that you care. It’s a small gesture that can go a long way. For example, when you send a heart in response to a funny message, it shows that you appreciated the joke. Hearts also allow you to express yourself without saying a word. Instead of writing a long reply, you can simply send a heart to show your feelings. It’s a quick and easy way to communicate with people.</p>

<h2>Tips for Sending Hearts on Messenger</h2>

<p>Here are some tips for sending hearts on Messenger:</p>

<ul>
  <li>Be mindful of your reactions. Don’t send a heart to every message.</li>
  <li>Make sure to use the correct reaction. If someone sends you a sad message, don’t send a heart.</li>
  <li>Be creative. You don’t have to stick to the heart reaction. Try using other reactions to express yourself.</li>
  <li>Don’t overuse hearts. If you send too many hearts, it can seem excessive.</li>
</ul>
