<h2>Why Reset the Oil Maintenance Light?</h2>

<p>The oil maintenance light on a 2011 Prius is designed to remind the driver to have their oil changed. This is important as regular oil changes are necessary to ensure the engine runs properly and efficiently. Failure to have the oil changed can lead to engine damage and costly repairs. Resetting the light ensures that the driver is reminded to have the oil changed in a timely manner.</p>

<h2>Steps to Reset the Oil Maintenance Light</h2>

<p>The first step to resetting the oil maintenance light on a 2011 Prius is to turn the car off and make sure the ignition is in the “off” position. Then open the hood and locate the oil filter. Remove the oil filter and drain the oil from the filter into a container. Once the oil has been drained, replace the oil filter and close the hood.</p>

<p>Next, turn the ignition to the ON position, but do not start the engine. Then press and hold the “Trip” button on the dash and turn the key to the start position. Continue to hold the “Trip” button until the oil maintenance light starts to flash. This indicates that the oil maintenance light has been reset. Finally, turn the ignition off and the oil maintenance light should remain off.</p>
