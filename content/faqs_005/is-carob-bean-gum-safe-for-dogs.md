<h2>What Is Carob Bean Gum?</h2>

Carob bean gum is a natural gum produced from the fruit of the carob tree (Ceratonia siliqua). It is used as an emulsifier, stabilizer, and thickening agent in products such as pet food, ice cream, and chocolate. Carob bean gum is also used in human food and can be found in many products such as bread, processed meats, and dairy products.
