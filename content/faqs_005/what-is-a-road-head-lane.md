Road head lanes are a type of lane that is specifically designed to help reduce congestion and improve flow on highways and other roads. They are created by adding a lane to the side of the road that is separated from the main road by a physical barrier, such as a guardrail or concrete wall. The purpose of a road head lane is to provide a safe passing opportunity for vehicles traveling in the same direction, without the need to merge into the main lane. This type of lane can be found on highways, local streets, and even rural roads.
