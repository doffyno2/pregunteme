<h2> How Much Does a 20-Gallon Fish Tank Weigh?</h2>

<p>The weight of a 20-gallon fish tank will vary depending on the type of material it is made from. A 20-gallon glass tank will weigh significantly more than a 20-gallon plastic tank. On average, a glass tank of this size will weigh between 40 and 70 pounds, while a plastic tank will weigh between 10 and 15 pounds. This is due to the fact that glass is a much denser material than plastic.</p>
