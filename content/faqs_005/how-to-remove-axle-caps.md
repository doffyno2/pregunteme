<h2>What is an Axle Cap?</h2>

An axle cap is a metal or plastic cap that covers the end of an axle. It helps to keep the axle secure and helps to protect it from dirt and debris. The axle caps are usually held in place by either screws or a combination of nuts and bolts. 

<h2>Why Should You Check and Maintain Your Axle Caps?</h2>

Regularly checking and maintaining your axle caps is important as they can become corroded or damaged over time due to exposure to dirt, moisture, or other elements.
