Squirrel hunting is a great way to gain experience and sharpen your hunting skills. In Ohio, there are two types of squirrels commonly hunted: gray and fox. The season is divided into early and late seasons, with the early season running from mid-September to late October and the late season running from late October to mid-February. The early season is the more popular of the two, as squirrels tend to be more active during this period.
