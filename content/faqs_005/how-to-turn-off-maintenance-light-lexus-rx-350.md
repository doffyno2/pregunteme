<p>If you drive a Lexus RX 350, you may have noticed that the maintenance light is on. This light is designed to alert you when it's time to perform routine maintenance on your vehicle, but it can be confusing to know when and how to turn it off. The good news is that it's easy to turn off the maintenance light on a Lexus RX 350, and in this article, we'll show you how.</p>

<h2>What Does the Maintenance Light on a Lexus RX 350 Mean?</h2>

<p>The maintenance light on a Lexus RX 350 is an indicator light that informs you when it's time to perform routine maintenance on your vehicle. It's important to keep up with regular maintenance on your vehicle, as this will help keep it running smoothly and efficiently. The maintenance light will typically come on every 5,000 miles or 6 months, whichever comes first.</p>

<h2>How to Turn Off the Maintenance Light on a Lexus RX 350</h2>

<p>When it's time to turn off the maintenance light on a Lexus RX 350, you'll need to follow these steps:</p>

<ol>
    <li>Locate the maintenance light on the dashboard of your vehicle.</li>
    <li>Press and hold the “Reset” button until the light turns off.</li>
    <li>Start the engine and wait for the maintenance light to turn off.</li>
</ol>

<p>Once the maintenance light is off, you can have peace of mind knowing that your vehicle is up to date on its service and maintenance schedule.</p>

<h2>When Should You Perform Maintenance on a Lexus RX 350?</h2>

<p>It's important to keep up with regular maintenance on your Lexus RX 350 in order to keep it running smoothly and efficiently. It's recommended that you perform maintenance on your Lexus RX 350 at least every 5,000 miles or 6 months, whichever comes first. This will help keep your vehicle in top condition and help you avoid costly repairs in the future.</p>

<h2>What Maintenance Should be Performed on a Lexus RX 350?</h2>

<p>The type of maintenance that should be performed on a Lexus RX 350 will depend on the year, make, and model of the vehicle. However, some of the most common types of maintenance that should be performed include:</p>

<ul>
    <li>Oil change</li>
    <li>Tire rotation</li>
    <li>Brake inspection</li>
    <li>Fluid top-off</li>
    <li>Air filter replacement</li>
    <li>Spark plug replacement</li>
</ul>

<p>If you're not sure what type of maintenance should be performed on your Lexus RX 350, it's best to consult your owner's manual or take it to a certified mechanic for inspection.</p>

<h2>Can You Reset the Maintenance Light on a Lexus RX 350 Yourself?</h2>

<p>Yes, you can reset the maintenance light on a Lexus RX 350 yourself. All you need to do is locate the maintenance light on the dashboard of your vehicle, press and hold the “Reset” button until the light turns off, and then start the engine and wait for the maintenance light to turn off.</p>
