<h2>What Is Sleeping Beauty?</h2>

Sleeping Beauty is a classic fairy tale written by the Brothers Grimm in the early 19th century. The story tells the tale of a beautiful princess named Aurora who is cursed by an evil fairy to fall into an eternal sleep. The curse can only be broken by the kiss of a prince. After many years of searching, a handsome prince eventually finds her and kisses her, breaking the curse and awakening her.

<h2>How Long Does Sleeping Beauty Sleep?</h2>

The exact amount of time that Sleeping Beauty sleeps depends on the version of the story. In the original Brothers Grimm version, she is asleep for a hundred years before the prince finds her. However, in other versions of the story, the amount of time varies. In the Disney version, she is asleep for only three years before she is awoken by the prince.

Although Sleeping Beauty's sleep is a fairy tale, it is not completely unrealistic.
