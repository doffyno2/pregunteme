<h2>What is Nerve Damage?</h2>

<p>Nerve damage is when nerves are damaged, either by trauma, disease, or infection. This can cause a wide range of symptoms, depending on the type of nerve damage. It can cause numbness, tingling, and pain, as well as muscle weakness and difficulty with motor control. Nerve damage can also lead to complications, such as cognitive impairment and difficulty with speaking.</p>

<h2>Causes of Nerve Damage</h2>
