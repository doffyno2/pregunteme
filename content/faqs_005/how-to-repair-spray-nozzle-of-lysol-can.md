<p>The nozzle is an important part of a Lysol can, as it allows you to spray the product in the right direction and accurately measure the amount you want to use. With some simple tools and a little bit of know-how, you can repair a spray nozzle of a Lysol can yourself.</p>

<h2>Tools Needed</h2>

<p>You will need some basic tools to repair a Lysol can's spray nozzle. These include:</p>

<ul>
  <li>A pair of pliers</li>
  <li>A small screwdriver</li>
  <li>A soft cloth</li>
  <li>A can of Lysol</li>
</ul>

<h2>Steps to Take</h2>

<p>Follow these steps to repair your Lysol can's spray nozzle:</p>

<ol>
  <li>Start by making sure the Lysol can is in an upright position. This will prevent any of the product from spilling out.</li>
  <li>Next, use the pliers to unscrew the top of the nozzle. Be careful not to damage the plastic.</li>
  <li>Once the top is removed, you should be able to see the internal parts of the nozzle. Use the screwdriver to remove any dirt or debris that may have built up inside.</li>
  <li>Once all the debris is removed, use the cloth to wipe away any remaining residue. Be sure to get into all the nooks and crannies.</li>
  <li>Once the nozzle is clean, reassemble it and screw the top back on.</li>
  <li>Test the spray nozzle to make sure it is working properly.</li>
</ol>
