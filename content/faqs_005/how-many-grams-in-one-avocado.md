<h2>What is an Avocado?</h2>

Avocado is a fruit that is native to Mexico and Central America, and it has been cultivated for centuries. It is a member of the Lauraceae family and is related to camphor and cinnamon. Avocados are a rich source of healthy fats and minerals, and they are a great source of dietary fiber.

<h2>How Many Grams are in an Avocado?</h2>

The average weight of an avocado is around 200 grams. However, the size of the avocado can vary, so the weight can range from around 100 grams to 300 grams depending on the size of the fruit. The weight of an avocado also depends on the variety and the ripeness of the fruit.

<h2>What is the Nutritional Profile of Avocado?</h2>

Avocado is an excellent source of healthy fats and nutrients. It is also a great source of dietary fiber.
