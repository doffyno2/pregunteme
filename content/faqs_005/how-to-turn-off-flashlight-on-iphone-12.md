<h2>What is a Flashlight?</h2>

<p>A flashlight is a handheld device that emits light from a bulb or LED. It is a common household item that is used for a variety of tasks, such as finding your way in the dark, reading a book, camping, or simply providing light in a dark room. It is one of the most basic tools for anyone who needs to work in dark places.</p>
