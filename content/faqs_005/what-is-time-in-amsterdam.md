<h2>Time Zone in Amsterdam</h2>

Amsterdam is located in the Central European Time Zone, which is one hour ahead of GMT/UTC. This means that Amsterdam's time zone is GMT +1. The city follows Daylight Saving Time and adjusts its clocks in the summer months by an extra hour.

<h2>What is the Current Time in Amsterdam?</h2>

The current time in Amsterdam is determined by the Central European Time Zone. As of April 2021, the current local time in Amsterdam is 8:00 PM (20:00 hours).

<h2>What is the Daylight Saving Time in Amsterdam?</h2>

Daylight Saving Time (DST) is observed in Amsterdam, which means that clocks are adjusted forward by 1 hour for a period of 8 months and then adjusted back by 1 hour for the remaining 4 months. During DST, the time in Amsterdam is GMT +2.
