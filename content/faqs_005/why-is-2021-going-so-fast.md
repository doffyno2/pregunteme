<h2>What Is Time Dilation?</h2>

Time dilation is an effect of the theory of relativity. It states that time passes more quickly for a person who is moving faster than another person. The faster the speed the person is travelling, the more time will pass for them in comparison to someone who is standing still. This phenomenon has been demonstrated in experiments with atomic clocks, which show that time passes more quickly for a clock that is moving than one that is stationary.
