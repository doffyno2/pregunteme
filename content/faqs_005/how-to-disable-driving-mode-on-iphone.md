<h2>What is Driving Mode?</h2>

<p>Driving mode is a feature on iPhones that silences incoming calls and text messages, blocks notifications, and prevents certain apps from running while you’re driving. It’s designed to help you stay focused on the road and reduce distractions while driving.</p>

<h2>How to Enable Driving Mode on Your iPhone</h2>

<p>Driving mode is easy to enable on your iPhone. Here’s how:</p>

<h3>Step 1: Open the Settings App</h3>

<p>The first step is to open the Settings app on your iPhone. You can do this by tapping the Settings icon on your home screen.</p>

<h3>Step 2: Tap “Do Not Disturb While Driving”</h3>

<p>In the Settings menu, scroll down and tap “Do Not Disturb.” Then, tap “Do Not Disturb While Driving.”</p>

<h3>Step 3: Enable Driving Mode</h3>

<p>On the “Do Not Disturb While Driving” page, you’ll see a toggle switch at the top. Tap the switch to enable driving mode. When the switch turns green, driving mode is active.</p>
