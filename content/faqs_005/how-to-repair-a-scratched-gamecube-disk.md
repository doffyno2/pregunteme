<h2>How to Clean a Scratched GameCube Disk</h2>

<p>The first step in repairing a scratched GameCube disk is to clean it. To do this, you will need a cotton cloth and some isopropyl alcohol. <h2>How to Repair a Scratched GameCube Disk</h2>

<p>Once the disk is clean, you can start to repair any scratches that are present. Start by using some fine sandpaper to gently sand away the scratches.
