<h2>What You Need to Change Batteries in a Coleman Battery Lock Flashlight</h2>

<p>In order to change the batteries in a Coleman Battery Lock Flashlight, you will need:</p>

<ul>
  <li>Fresh alkaline batteries</li>
  <li>A Phillips head screwdriver</li>
</ul>

<h2>Steps to Change Batteries in a Coleman Battery Lock Flashlight</h2>

<p>Once you have the necessary supplies, follow these steps to change the batteries in your Coleman Battery Lock Flashlight:</p>

<ol>
  <li>Unscrew the base of the flashlight using the Phillips head screwdriver.</li>
  <li>Remove the old batteries from the flashlight, taking care not to damage the wiring or the battery compartment.</li>
  <li>Insert the new batteries into the flashlight, making sure that the positive and negative ends are properly aligned.</li>
  <li>Replace the base of the flashlight and tighten the screws.</li>
</ol>
