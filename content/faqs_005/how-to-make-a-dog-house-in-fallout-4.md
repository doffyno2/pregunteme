<h2>How to Craft a Dog House in Fallout 4</h2>

<p>Once you have all the items and tools you need to craft a dog house, it’s time to get started. Here’s how to craft a dog house in Fallout 4:</p>

<ol>
  <li>Go to a sawmill and use the sawmill to turn your wood into planks.</li>
  <li>Go to a forge and use the forge to turn your steel into nails.</li>
  <li>Go to the workbench and use the screwdriver and hammer to put the planks together to form the walls of the dog house.</li>
  <li>Use the nailgun to secure the planks and make sure they are firmly attached.</li>
  <li>Wrap the cloth around the exterior of the dog house to provide insulation.</li>
  <li>Place the ceramic inside the dog house to provide a comfortable surface for your pet.</li>
  <li>Use the adhesive to secure the ceramic in place.</li>
  <li>Once the dog house is complete, place it in your settlement and your pet will have a safe and comfortable place to sleep.</li>
</ol>
