<h2>Ingredients for Blue Dog Soda</h2>

<p>To make homemade blue dog soda, you will need the following ingredients:</p>

<ul>
  <li>One cup of sugar</li> 
  <li>Two tablespoons of citric acid</li> 
  <li>One teaspoon of baking soda</li> 
  <li>One teaspoon of blue food coloring</li> 
  <li>Four cups of cold water</li> 
  <li>One teaspoon of  blueberry extract</li>
</ul>

<h2>Equipment Needed to Make Blue Dog Soda</h2>

<p>To make homemade blue dog soda, you will need the following items:</p>

<ul>
  <li>A large pot</li> 
  <li>A whisk</li> 
  <li>A strainer</li> 
  <li>A funnel</li> 
  <li>A 1-liter bottle with a tight-fitting lid</li> 
  <li>A carbonator, such as a Sodastream or a carbonator bottle</li>
</ul>

<h2>Instructions on How to Make Blue Dog Soda</h2>

<p>Making blue dog soda is really simple. Simply follow these steps:</p>

<ol>
  <li>In a large pot, combine the sugar, citric acid, baking soda, and food coloring. Whisk to combine.</li>
  <li>Slowly add the cold water, whisking constantly. Bring to a boil and simmer for 10 minutes, stirring occasionally.</li>
  <li>Remove from the heat and stir in the blueberry extract. Strain the mixture through a fine mesh strainer into a large bowl.</li>
  <li>Set up your carbonator and fill the 1-liter bottle with the strained mixture. Secure the lid tightly.</li>
  <li>Place the bottle in the carbonator and carbonate according to the manufacturer’s instructions. This process usually takes about 5 minutes.</li>
  <li>Remove the carbonated blue dog soda from the carbonator and place it in the refrigerator to cool. Enjoy!</li>
</ol>
