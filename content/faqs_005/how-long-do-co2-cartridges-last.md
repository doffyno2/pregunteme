<h2>What Are CO2 Cartridges?</h2> 

CO2 cartridges are cylinders filled with pressurized carbon dioxide gas. The gas is held in the cartridges by a heavy-duty steel or aluminum casing. The cartridges come in a variety of sizes, from small 12g cartridges to larger 20oz cartridges. They are used in many different applications, from powering pneumatic tools to carbonating drinks.
