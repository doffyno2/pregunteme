<h2>What Does LT Mean in Shirt Size?</h2>

LT stands for “Large Tall” and is a size designation that refers to larger body types with longer arms and legs. The LT size is perfect for those who are taller and thinner than average, and want a shirt that fits their body perfectly. LT sizes are usually found in the men’s section of stores, but some women’s stores carry them as well.

<h2>What Are the Features of an LT Shirt?</h2>

LT shirts are designed to fit a taller body type. They are usually cut longer in the body and arms than regular sizes, ensuring that the shirt won’t be too short when worn by someone with a longer torso or arms. In addition, the sleeves will typically be slightly wider to accommodate the larger arm size.
