<h2>Reading the Model Number</h2>

<p>The first place to look for the year of a Yamaha dirt bike is the model number. Yamaha dirt bikes have a unique model number that tells you what type of bike it is, as well as the year it was made. The model number is usually printed on the frame of the bike, usually on the right side. It may be printed on a sticker or in permanent ink. The model number should include a 4-digit year code. For example, a model number of YZ250F-2018 indicates that the bike was made in 2018.</p>

<h2>Checking the Vin Number</h2>

<p>If you can't find the model number, or it doesn't include the year, you can look at the VIN number. The VIN number is a unique 17-digit code that can tell you the year, make, and model of the bike. It is usually printed on a sticker on the frame near the steering head. The tenth character of the VIN is the year of the bike. This character will be a letter from A through Y, with A representing 2010 and Y representing 2030.</p>

<h2>Checking the Owner's Manual</h2>

<p>If you don't have the VIN number or the model number, you can check the owner's manual for the year of the bike. Most Yamaha dirt bikes come with an owner's manual that includes all the information you would need to properly maintain the bike, including the year it was made. The year should be printed somewhere on the cover or inside the manual.</p>

<h2>Checking with a Dealer</h2>

<p>If you still can't find the year of your Yamaha dirt bike, you can always check with a local Yamaha dealer. This is especially helpful if you are looking to buy a used bike and want to make sure you're getting the right year.</p>
