<h2>How to Make Rice Water for Your Dog</h2>

<p>Making rice water for your dog is a simple process that only requires a few ingredients and a little bit of time. The first step is to rinse the rice in cold water to remove any dirt or debris. Then, add the rice to a large saucepan and cover with cold water. Bring the water to a boil and then reduce the heat to a simmer. Allow the rice to simmer for about 20 minutes, or until it is soft. After the rice is cooked, strain the liquid into a container and discard the solids. The resulting liquid is the rice water, which can be stored in the refrigerator for up to 5 days.</p>
