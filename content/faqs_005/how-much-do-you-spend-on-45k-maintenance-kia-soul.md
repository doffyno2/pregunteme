<h2>What is 45K Maintenance?</h2>

<p>45K maintenance is a type of maintenance that is recommended for cars that have been driven for 45,000 miles or more. During this service, the technician will inspect and replace any worn parts, as well as check the car for any other potential problems. Common items that are checked during the 45K service include the brakes, the oil and filter, the spark plugs and wires, the tires, the exhaust system, and the suspension. The technician will also check to make sure all fluid levels are correct and may even recommend certain preventative maintenance measures, such as replacing the timing belt.</p>
