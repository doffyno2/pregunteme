<h2>What Is the Army Tape Test?</h2>

The tape test is a component of the Army Physical Fitness Test (APFT). It is used to measure a soldier’s waist size to ensure they are within the standards set by the Army. The standards are based on gender and age. For example, a male soldier between the ages of 17 and 21 must have a waist size of no more than 32 inches.
