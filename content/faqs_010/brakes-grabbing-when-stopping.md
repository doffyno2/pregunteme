<h2>What Causes Brakes to Grab When Stopping?</h2>

<p>When brakes start to grab when stopping, it means that the brakes are not releasing properly. This can be caused by a few things. The most common cause of brakes grabbing is worn brake pads. If the brake pads are worn down, they can start to stick to the rotors, causing the brakes to grab when stopping. Other causes of brakes grabbing could be a worn caliper, a sticking caliper, warped brake rotors, or contaminated brake pads. </p>
