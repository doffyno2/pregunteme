<h2>What Is Certified Mail?</h2>

Certified mail is a service offered by the USPS that provides proof of mailing, delivery, and signature. When you send a certified mail letter, the USPS will provide you with a unique tracking number and a return receipt that includes the recipient's signature upon delivery. Certified mail is often used to send important documents, such as birth certificates, contracts, and court documents.
