<p>One of the most common questions asked when shopping for a wedding band is "how much does a wedding band weigh?" <h2>Weighing Wedding Bands</h2>

<p>The weight of a wedding band can range from very light to relatively heavy depending on the type of metal used, the size of the band, and the number of gemstones or other embellishments added. Platinum, for example, is one of the heavier metals used in wedding bands and a platinum wedding band will usually weigh more than a gold or silver wedding band. Similarly, thick wedding bands or those with many gemstones will be heavier than thinner, simpler bands.</p>

<p>The average weight of a wedding band is between 2 and 10 grams, depending on the type of metal and the size of the ring.
