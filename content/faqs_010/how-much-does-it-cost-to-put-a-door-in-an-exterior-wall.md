How Much Does it Cost to Put a Door in an Exterior Wall?

Doors are a critical component of any exterior wall. Not only do they provide security, but they also offer a decorative touch to the exterior of your home. But before you can install a door in an exterior wall, you need to understand the cost associated with the project. 

In this article, we'll break down the cost of putting a door in an exterior wall, including the cost of materials, labor, and any additional considerations you may need to make.

<h2>Cost of Materials Needed to Put a Door in an Exterior Wall</h2>

The cost of materials required to put a door in an exterior wall varies greatly depending on the type of door you choose. Generally speaking, you can expect to pay between $100 and $500 for a standard, ready-to-install exterior door. Prices may vary depending on the size and style of the door.

Additional materials, such as hinges, locks, and weather stripping, will add to the overall cost of the project. You can expect to pay between $50 and $150 for these materials.

<h2>Labor Costs to Put a Door in an Exterior Wall</h2>

The labor costs associated with putting a door in an exterior wall depend on the complexity of the job. If you're replacing an existing door, the job may be relatively simple, and you may be able to do it yourself. However, if you're installing a new door in a new wall, you'll likely need to hire a professional.

The cost of hiring a professional to install a door in an exterior wall can range from $200 to $1,000, depending on the complexity of the job.

<h2>Additional Considerations for Installing a Door in an Exterior Wall</h2>

In addition to the cost of the materials and labor, there are a few other considerations to keep in mind when putting a door in an exterior wall.

First, you'll need to make sure the door is properly sealed. This can be done by using weather stripping or caulk around the edges of the door.

Second, you'll need to make sure the door is properly secured. This can be done with locks, deadbolts, or other security devices.

Finally, you'll need to make sure the door is properly insulated. This can be done by using insulation material around the edges of the door.
