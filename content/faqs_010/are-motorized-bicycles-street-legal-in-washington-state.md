<h2>Are Motorized Bicycles Legal in Washington State?</h2>

<p>In Washington State, motorized bicycles are legal and classified as “motor-driven cycles” if they meet certain criteria. These criteria include:</p>

<ul>
  <li>The motor must be 50 cc or less.</li>
  <li>The motor must be no louder than 85 decibels.</li>
  <li>The motor must be able to be stopped with a single foot pedal.</li>
  <li>The motor must be able to be started with a single push-button.</li>
  <li>The motor must have an automatic transmission.</li>
</ul>

<p>If a motorized bicycle meets all of these criteria, it is considered a “motor-driven cycle” and is legal to operate in Washington State. Motor-driven cycles are subject to the same laws and regulations as other motor vehicles.</p>
