<h2>What is Gravel?</h2>

<p>Gravel is a material that is commonly used in fish tanks. It is a type of aggregate, meaning it is made up of small particles of rock and other materials. Gravel is available in a variety of sizes, colors, and textures, so you can find something that suits the look of your tank. The gravel serves several purposes. It provides a place for beneficial bacteria to live, which helps to keep the water clean. It also provides a place for the fish to hide and for plants to root. </p>
