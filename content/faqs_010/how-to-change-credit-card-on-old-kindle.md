<p>Are you an old Kindle user and looking to change your credit card information? If so, you’re in the right place. This article will walk you through the process of updating your credit card information on an old Kindle device. We’ll cover how to update your credit card information on a Kindle e-reader, the Kindle Fire tablet, and the Kindle app.</p> 

<h2>Updating Credit Card Information on Kindle E-Readers</h2>

<p>If you’re using an old Kindle e-reader, you can update your credit card information directly from the device. To start, go to the Home screen of your Kindle and tap on the Menu icon. From there, select “Settings” and “Registration.” Enter your Amazon account information and tap “Register.”</p>

<p>Once you’ve registered your device, you’ll be able to access the “Payment Methods” option. Here, you can add a new credit card or update the information for an existing card. When you’re finished, select “Save Changes.”</p>

<h2>Updating Credit Card Information on Kindle Fire Tablets</h2>

<p>If you’re using a Kindle Fire tablet, you can update your credit card information from the device. To start, open the Kindle Fire settings and select “Accounts & Sync.” Next, tap “Payment Methods.” Here, you can add a new credit card or update the information for an existing card. When you’re finished, select “Save Changes.”</p>

<h2>Updating Credit Card Information on the Kindle App</h2>

<p>If you’re using the Kindle app, you can update your credit card information from the app. To start, open the Kindle app and login to your Amazon account. From there, select “Settings” and “Payment Methods.” Here, you can add a new credit card or update the information for an existing card. When you’re finished, select “Save Changes.”</p>
