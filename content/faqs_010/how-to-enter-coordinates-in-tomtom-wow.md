<h2>What Are GPS Coordinates?</h2>

<p>GPS coordinates are a set of numbers that represent the exact location of a particular point, such as a building, park, or street corner. The coordinates are usually made up of two numbers: longitude and latitude. Longitude describes the east-west position of a point, while latitude describes the north-south position. Together, they form an exact location.</p>
