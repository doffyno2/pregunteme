Can dogs eat Ritz crackers? This is a common question for pet owners and one that should be answered with some caution. <h2>What Are Ritz Crackers?</h2>

Ritz crackers are a type of savory biscuit, made from enriched wheat flour, vegetable oils, sugar, and salt. They are usually round, about the size of a quarter, and have a distinctively salty, buttery flavor. Ritz crackers are often eaten as a snack with cheese, dips, spreads, or other accompaniments.

<h2>Are Ritz Crackers Safe for Dogs?</h2>
