<h2>What is Cottage Cheese?</h2>

Cottage cheese is a type of cheese that is made from the curds of cow’s milk. It has a mild, slightly tart flavor and a creamy texture. The curds are drained and the whey is separated from the solids, resulting in a soft, smooth cheese. Cottage cheese is a good source of protein, calcium, and other essential vitamins and minerals.

<h2>Why Is Cottage Cheese Good for Dogs?</h2>

Cottage cheese also contains a good amount of potassium, which helps to regulate fluid balance in the body and aids in digestion.
