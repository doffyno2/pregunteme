<p>In order for your iFit to connect to your ProForm treadmill, your treadmill needs to be connected to Wi-Fi. To do this, press the “Menu” button on your treadmill’s console. Then, select the “Settings” option and then select “Wi-Fi.” Choose your Wi-Fi network from the list of available networks and enter your Wi-Fi password. <h2>Step 4: Select a Workout</h2>
