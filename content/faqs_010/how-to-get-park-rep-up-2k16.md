Park reputation or park rep, is an important metric for skateboarders. It is used to determine how well a skateboarder is respected in the skateboarding community. It is based on the number of tricks a skateboarder can do, the number of tricks a skateboarder can do in a given amount of time, and the level of difficulty of tricks that a skateboarder can perform. A skateboarder's park rep will be determined by a combination of these factors. 

<h2>What Is Park Rep?</h2>

Park rep is a measure of a skateboarder's respect in the skateboarding community. It is based on a number of factors, including the number of tricks they can do, the level of difficulty of the tricks they can do, and the amount of time it takes them to do those tricks.
