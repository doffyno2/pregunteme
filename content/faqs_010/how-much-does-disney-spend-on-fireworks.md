<h2>What Are Fireworks?</h2>

Fireworks are a type of pyrotechnic display, usually seen during celebrations, holidays, and special events. They are launched into the sky and burst into a variety of colours and effects, creating a beautiful display. Fireworks often incorporate music and other sound effects, and can be choreographed to create an even more impressive display.

<h2>What Are Fireworks Used For?</h2>

Fireworks are often used to celebrate special occasions, such as holidays, weddings, anniversaries, birthdays, and graduations. Fireworks are also used to commemorate important events, such as the Fourth of July in the United States and New Year’s Eve in many countries around the world. Fireworks are also used in entertainment, such as at sports events and in movie and television productions.

<h2>What Type of Fireworks Does Disney Use?</h2>

Disney uses a variety of different types of fireworks in its shows. Some of the most popular types of fireworks used by Disney include Roman candles, aerial shells, and ground-based effects. Roman candles are cylindrical tubes filled with gunpowder and other fuel, that are launched into the sky, creating a variety of colourful effects. Aerial shells are large mortar shells that are launched into the sky, creating a large, colourful display. Ground-based effects are used to create various shapes and patterns, such as circles and stars.
