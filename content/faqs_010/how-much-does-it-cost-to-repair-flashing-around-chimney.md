<h2>What is Chimney Flashing?</h2>

<p>Chimney flashing is a sheet of metal that is used to seal the joint between the chimney and the roof. It helps keep water out of the home and prevents water damage to the roof. The flashing is installed in several layers and is usually made of galvanized steel, stainless steel, or copper. </p>

<h2>What Causes Chimney Flashing Damage?</h2>

<p>Chimney flashing can become damaged over time due to extreme weather conditions, such as high winds, hail, or snow. The flashing can also become damaged due to wear and tear over time. Additionally, the flashing can become corroded due to exposure to moisture and water. </p>
