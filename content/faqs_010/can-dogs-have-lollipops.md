<p>Most lollipops are made from a variety of ingredients, including sugar, syrup, flavoring, and coloring. Depending on the type of lollipop, the ingredients can vary. <p>The safest lollipop for dogs is one that is made with natural ingredients and doesn't contain any artificial additives or preservatives. <p>Once you've found a lollipop that is safe for your pup, it's important to make sure you give it to them in a safe manner.
