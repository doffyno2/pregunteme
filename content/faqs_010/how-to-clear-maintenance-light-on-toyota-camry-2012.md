<h2>What Do I Need Before I Start? </h2>

<p>Before you start the process of resetting the maintenance light on your Toyota Camry 2012, you'll need the following items:</p>

<li>A pair of pliers</li>
<li>A small flashlight</li>

<h2>How to Reset the Maintenance Light on Your Toyota Camry 2012</h2>

<p>The process of resetting the maintenance light on your Toyota Camry 2012 is relatively straightforward. Here's what you need to do:</p>

<ol>
<li>Start by opening the hood of your Toyota Camry 2012. Once it's open, locate the battery. </li>
<li>Using the flat head screwdriver, loosen and remove the negative terminal from the battery. </li>
<li>Now, take the Phillips head screwdriver and unscrew the battery cover. </li>
<li>Using the pliers, remove the battery from its housing. </li>
<li>Using the flashlight, locate the maintenance light on the dashboard. </li>
<li>Using the pliers, disconnect the maintenance light from its harness. </li>
<li>Now, reconnect the battery to its housing and close the battery cover. </li>
<li>Reattach the negative terminal to the battery and close the hood. </li>
<li>Turn the key in the ignition and start the car. </li>
<li>The maintenance light should be off. </li>
</ol>
