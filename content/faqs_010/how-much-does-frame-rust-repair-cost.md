<h2>What Causes Frame Rust?</h2>

<p>Frame rust is caused by a variety of factors. The most common cause is water being trapped in the frame. As the water gets trapped, it starts to corrode the metal, leading to rust. Other factors that can cause frame rust are road salt, dirt, and other environmental factors.</p>

<h2>What Are the Signs of Frame Rust?</h2>

<p>Frame rust can be easy to spot. The most common signs are discoloration and flaking of the metal. If the rust is left untreated, it can continue to spread and cause more damage to the frame.</p>
