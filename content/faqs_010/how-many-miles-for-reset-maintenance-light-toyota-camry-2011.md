<h2>What is the Maintenance Light?</h2>

The maintenance light is an indicator light located on the dashboard of your Toyota Camry. It is designed to alert you when it is time to take your vehicle in for servicing. The maintenance light does not indicate that there is a problem with the car, but rather that it is time to bring it in for regular maintenance.

<h2>When Does the Maintenance Light Come On?</h2>

The maintenance light will come on after a specific number of miles or after a certain amount of time has passed. For the Toyota Camry 2011, the maintenance light will come on after 5000 miles or after 6 months, whichever comes first. When the maintenance light does come on, it is a reminder to take your vehicle in for servicing.

If you ignore the maintenance light, you are putting your car at risk of potential damage. Servicing your car regularly is important in order to keep it running efficiently and safely. This can be done easily by pressing and holding the odometer button on the dashboard. Once the light has been reset, it will stay off until it is time to have your car serviced again.
