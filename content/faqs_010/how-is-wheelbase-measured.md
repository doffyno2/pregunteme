<h2>How Is Wheelbase Measured?</h2>

Wheelbase is measured by measuring the distance between the center of the front wheels and the center of the rear wheels. This is usually done with a tape measure, but a ruler or other measuring device can be used as well. To measure wheelbase, you first need to determine the center of the front wheels and the center of the rear wheels. You can do this by measuring from the center of one wheel to the center of the other wheel. Once you have the center points of both the front and rear wheels, you can then measure the distance between them to get the wheelbase.
