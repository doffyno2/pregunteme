<h2>What is Voltage? </h2>

<p>In layman's terms, voltage is a measure of electrical potential. It is the measure of the force that creates the flow of electrons from one point to another. When it comes to batteries, the voltage is the amount of energy that the battery can put out at any given time. </p>

<h2>What is the Voltage of a AAA Battery? </h2>

<p>The voltage of a AAA battery is 1.5 volts. This is the same voltage as two AA batteries. This means that when two AAA batteries are connected in series, they will have a total voltage of 3 volts. </p>

<h2>When is a AAA Battery Dead? </h2>

<p>A AAA battery is considered dead when the voltage has dropped to 1.2 volts or lower. This means that if you measure the voltage of the battery and it is below 1.2 volts, then it is time to replace it. </p>

<h2>How to Measure the Voltage of a AAA Battery? </h2>

<p>Measuring the voltage of a AAA battery is fairly simple. All you need is a multimeter or a voltmeter. Connect the positive and negative leads of the multimeter to the positive and negative terminals of the battery. The multimeter will then display the voltage of the battery on its screen. If the voltage is 1.2 volts or lower, then the battery is dead and needs to be replaced. </p>
