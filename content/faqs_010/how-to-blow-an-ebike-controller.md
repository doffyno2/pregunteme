<h2>What is an E-Bike Controller?</h2>

<p>An e-bike controller is a small electronic device that controls the power output of the motor in an electric bike. It is the most important component of an electric bike and the part that allows you to control how fast you go and how much power your motor needs. <h3>Step 2: Remove the Controller</h3>
