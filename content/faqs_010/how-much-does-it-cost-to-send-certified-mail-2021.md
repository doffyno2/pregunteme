<h2>What Is Certified Mail?</h2>

<p>Certified mail is a type of mail service offered by the United States Postal Service (USPS). It provides proof of mailing and delivery and includes a tracking number to monitor the status of the mail. Certified mail also provides the sender with a return receipt, which serves as proof that the mail was delivered to the recipient.</p>

<h2>What Are The Different Types of Certified Mail?</h2>

<p>The USPS offers two types of certified mail services. The first is standard certified mail, which is the most common and cost-effective option. This service includes a tracking number and delivery confirmation. The second is certified mail with return receipt, which offers the same features as standard certified mail but also includes a signature from the recipient to confirm delivery.</p>

<h2>How Much Does It Cost To Send Certified Mail?</h2>

<p>The cost of sending a certified mail depends on the weight of the item, the services you choose, and the type of delivery.
