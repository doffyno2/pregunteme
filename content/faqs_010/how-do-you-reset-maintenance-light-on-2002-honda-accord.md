<h2>Tools Needed to Reset the Maintenance Light on a 2002 Honda Accord</h2>

The only tool you will need to reset the maintenance light on your 2002 Honda Accord is a flathead screwdriver.

<h2>Steps for Resetting the Maintenance Light on a 2002 Honda Accord</h2>

<h3>Step 1: Locate the Maintenance Light</h3>

The first step in resetting the maintenance light on your 2002 Honda Accord is to locate the maintenance light on the dash. It should be located in the lower right corner of the dash near the speedometer.

<h3>Step 2: Use the Flathead Screwdriver to Access the Maintenance Light</h3>

Once you have located the maintenance light, you will need to use the flathead screwdriver to remove the panel that covers it. Carefully remove the panel and set it aside.

<h3>Step 3: Reset the Maintenance Light</h3>

Once you have removed the panel, you should see a small button labeled “Maint” or “Maintenance.” Press this button to reset the maintenance light on your 2002 Honda Accord.

<h3>Step 4: Replace the Panel</h3>

Once you have reset the maintenance light, you will need to replace the panel that you removed in Step 2. Be sure to secure it firmly in place to ensure that it does not come loose.
