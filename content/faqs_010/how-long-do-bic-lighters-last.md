The flint in a Bic lighter is responsible for providing the spark that ignites the fuel. The fuel chamber of a Bic lighter should be refilled regularly to ensure that the lighter works properly. To refill the fuel chamber, simply unscrew the fuel cap located at the top of the lighter and fill the chamber with lighter fluid.
