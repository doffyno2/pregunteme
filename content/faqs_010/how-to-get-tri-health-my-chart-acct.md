<p>MyChart is a secure online health management tool from TriHealth that lets you manage your medical information and stay connected with your care team. It’s easy to get started with a TriHealth MyChart account. You can create an account online, or your care team can help you get started. Here’s a step-by-step guide to getting a MyChart account. </p>

<h2>Step 1: Verify Your Eligibility</h2>

<p>Before you begin the process, make sure you’re eligible to create a MyChart account. To be eligible, you must be a TriHealth patient or the legal guardian of a TriHealth patient. If you meet these requirements, you’re ready to create an account. </p>

<h2>Step 2: Get a MyChart Activation Code</h2>

<p>If you already have a MyChart activation code, you can skip this step. To get an activation code, contact your care team or visit your local TriHealth office.  </p>

<h2>Step 3: Create an Account</h2>

<p>Once you have an activation code, you can create an account online. Visit the TriHealth MyChart website and click “Sign Up Now”. You’ll be asked to enter your activation code and other personal information. Once you’ve completed the registration process, you’ll be asked to create a username and password. Make sure you remember your username and password, as you’ll need them to log in to your MyChart account. </p>

<h2>Step 4: Log In to Your Account</h2>

<p>Once your account has been created, you can log in to your MyChart account. Visit the TriHealth MyChart website and click “Log In”. Enter your username and password, and you’ll be taken to your MyChart dashboard. From here, you can view your medical records, request prescription refills, and contact your care team. </p>
