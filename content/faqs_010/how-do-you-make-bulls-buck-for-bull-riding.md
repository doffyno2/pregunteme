<h2>What is Bull Riding? </h2>

Bull riding is a rodeo event in which riders compete to stay atop a bucking bull for eight seconds. Riders must remain on the bull's back with one hand gripping a rope that is attached to the bull's back. The riders are judged on their ability to stay on the bull for the full eight seconds. The bull riders who stay on the longest are declared the winners.
